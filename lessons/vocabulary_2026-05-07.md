# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-07

## 1. **Evacuee** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪˌvækjuˈiː/
**Definition:** A person evacuated from a place of danger to somewhere safer.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 被疏散者

**Example 1:**
The evacuees were taken to a temporary shelter after the earthquake.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
地震後，疏散者被帶到一個臨時庇護所。

**Example 2:**
All evacuees from the cruise ship underwent medical checks upon arrival.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
所有從郵輪疏散的人員在抵達後都接受了體檢。

## 2. **Mediator** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈmiːdieɪtər/
**Definition:** A person who attempts to make people involved in a conflict come to an agreement; a go-between.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 調解人

**Example 1:**
The mediator worked tirelessly to find common ground between the two sides.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
調解人不知疲倦地工作，以尋找雙方之間的共同點。

**Example 2:**
Pakistan is acting as a mediator in the ongoing conflict between Iran and the US.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
巴基斯坦在伊朗和美國之間持續的衝突中充當調解人。

## 3. **Caveat** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈkævieɪt/
**Definition:** A warning or proviso of specific stipulations, conditions, or limitations.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 警告；限制

**Example 1:**
The offer comes with a caveat: you must be willing to work weekends.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
這份工作帶有一個警告：你必須願意在週末工作。

**Example 2:**
Trump's hopes for an Iran peace deal come with several caveats.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
特朗普對伊朗和平協議的希望帶有幾個限制。

## 4. **Nursery** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnɜːsəri/
**Definition:** A place where young children are cared for during the day while their parents are working.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 托兒所

**Example 1:**
She enrolled her son in a local nursery when she returned to work.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
她重返工作崗位後，將兒子送到當地一間托兒所。

**Example 2:**
The nursery provides a stimulating environment for early learning.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
托兒所為早期學習提供了一個具有啟發性的環境。

## 5. **Escalation** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌeskəˈleɪʃən/
**Definition:** A rapid increase or intensification; a situation that is becoming more serious.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 升級

**Example 1:**
The recent escalation of violence in the region is deeply concerning.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
該地區最近的暴力升級令人深感擔憂。

**Example 2:**
We need to prevent any further escalation of the conflict.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我們需要阻止衝突的進一步升級。

## 6. **Display** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /dɪˈspleɪ/
**Definition:** To show or exhibit.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 顯示，表現

**Example 1:**
He started to display symptoms while aboard the ship.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
當他在船上時，他開始表現出病徵。

**Example 2:**
The company will display its new products at the trade show.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
該公司將在貿易展覽會上展示其新產品。
