# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-25

Hi there!

## 1. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased; delighted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心; 好興奮

**Example 1:**
I was absolutely chuffed to win the competition.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我贏咗個比賽，真係好開心。

**Example 2:**
She was chuffed with her new car.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢對佢架新車好滿意。

## 2. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed; devastated.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望; 好傷心

**Example 1:**
I was gutted when I didn't get the job.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我冇得到嗰份工，我好失望。

**Example 2:**
She was absolutely gutted that she couldn't go on holiday.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢去唔到旅行，佢好傷心。

## 3. **Queue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people waiting for something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊列; 排隊

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the bakery.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
麵包舖外面有一條好長嘅隊列。

**Example 2:**
We had to stand in a queue for over an hour.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋要排隊排咗超過一個鐘。

## 4. **Cheers** (Exclamation/Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃɪəz/
**Definition:** (Exclamation) Thank you; or, a toast when drinking. (Noun) A drink of alcohol.
**Cantonese Meaning:** (Exclamation) 多謝; 乾杯 (Noun) 飲嘢

**Example 1:**
Cheers for your help!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
多謝你幫忙!

**Example 2:**
Let's raise our glasses and say cheers!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
等我哋舉起杯，然後講乾杯！

## 5. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪljənt/
**Definition:** Extremely good; excellent.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好棒; 好出色

**Example 1:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰個係一個好棒嘅主意！

**Example 2:**
She gave a brilliant performance.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢嘅表演好出色。

## 6. **Dodgy** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈdɒdʒi/
**Definition:** Suspicious, unreliable, or potentially dangerous.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可疑嘅; 唔可靠嘅; 危險嘅

**Example 1:**
That food looks a bit dodgy.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
啲嘢食睇落有啲可疑。

**Example 2:**
I wouldn't trust him; he seems a bit dodgy.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我唔會信佢，佢睇落有啲唔可靠。
