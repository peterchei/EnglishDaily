## 1. Ubiquitous

**Definition:** Present, appearing, or found everywhere.
**Cantonese Translation:** 無處不在 (mou4 chyu3 bui3 zoi6)

**Example 1:**
In today's digital age, smartphones have become ubiquitous.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 在今天的數碼時代，智能手機已變得無處不在。
(Zoi1 gam1 tin1 dik1 sou3 maa3 si4 doi6, zi3 nang4 sau2 gei1 ji5 bin3 dak1 mou4 chyu3 bui3 zoi6.)

**Example 2:**
The company's logo is ubiquitous, appearing on everything from billboards to coffee mugs.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 該公司的標誌無處不在，從廣告牌到咖啡杯上都能看到。
(Goi1 gung1 si1 dik1 biu1 zi3 mou4 chyu3 bui3 zoi6, cung4 gwong2 gou3 paai4 dou3 gaa3 fei1 bui1 soeng6 dou1 nang4 dou3 dou2.)

---

## 2. Ephemeral

**Definition:** Lasting for a very short time.
**Cantonese Translation:** 短暫的 (dyun2 zaan6 dik1), 短暫易逝的 (dyun2 zaan6 ji6 sai6 dik1)

**Example 1:**
The beauty of cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a week or two.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 櫻花的 [jing1 faa1 dik1] 美麗是短暫的，只持續一兩週。
(Jing1 faa1 dik1 mei5 lai6 si6 dyun2 zaan6 dik1, zi2 cyu4 hang4 jat1 loeng5 zau1.)

**Example 2:**
Fame in the entertainment industry can often be ephemeral.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 娛樂圈的 [jyu4 lok6 hyun1 dik1] 名氣往往是短暫易逝的。
(Jyu4 lok6 hyun1 dik1 ming4 hei3 wong5 wong5 si6 dyun2 zaan6 ji6 sai6 dik1.)

---

## 3. Serendipity

**Definition:** The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.
**Cantonese Translation:** 意外發現 (ji3 noi6 faat3 gin3), 倖遇 (hing3 jyu6)

**Example 1:**
Finding that rare book in a small, dusty shop was a moment of pure serendipity.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 在一家塵土飛揚的小店裡找到那本稀有的書，純粹是意外發現。
(Zoi1 jat1 gaa1 can4 tou2 fei1 joeng4 dik1 siu2 dim3 leoi5 zaau2 dou3 naa5 bun2 hei1 jau5 dik1 syu1, seon4 seoi3 si6 ji3 noi6 faat3 gin3.)

**Example 2:**
Many scientific discoveries happen through serendipity rather than deliberate research.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 許多科學發現是透過意外發現而來，而非刻意研究。
(Heoi2 do1 fo1 hok6 faat3 gin3 si6 tau3 gwo3 ji3 noi6 faat3 gin3 ji4 loi4, fei1 woeng4 hak1 ji3 jin5 gau3.)

---

## 4. Meticulous

**Definition:** Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise.
**Cantonese Translation:** 一絲不苟 (jat1 si1 bat1 gau2), 仔細的 (zi2 sai3 dik1)

**Example 1:**
The artist was meticulous in her brushstrokes, ensuring every detail was perfect.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 這位藝術家在筆觸上 [neoi5] 一絲不苟，確保每個細節都完美無瑕。
(Ceoi4 mei6 ngai6 sou3 gaa1 zoi6 bat2 chuk1 soeng6 jat1 si1 bat1 gau2, kok3 syun2 mui5 go3 sai3 git3 dou1 mei5 jing4 mou4 haa4.)

**Example 2:**
A meticulous planner, she organized the entire event down to the smallest detail.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 作為一個一絲不苟的策劃者，她將整個活動安排得滴水不漏。
(Zok3 wai4 jat1 go3 jat1 si1 bat1 gau2 dik1 cak3 waak6 ze2, taa1 zoeng1 zing2 go3 fo2 dung6 on1 paai4 dak1 di1 seoi2 bat1 lau6.)

---

## 5. Cacophony

**Definition:** A harsh, discordant mixture of sounds.
**Cantonese Translation:** 刺耳的雜音 (ci3 ji5 dik1 zaap6 jam1), 嘈雜聲 (cou4 zaap6 seng1)

**Example 1:**
The city at rush hour was a cacophony of car horns, sirens, and shouting.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 交通高峰時段的城市是一個由汽車喇叭聲、警笛聲和叫喊聲組成的刺耳的雜音。
(Gaau1 tung1 gou1 fung1 si4 tyun5 dik1 sing4 si5 si6 jat1 go3 jau4 hei3 ce1 laa3 paak3 seng1, ging2 dai4 seng1 ho2 giu3 haan5 seng1 zou2 sing4 dik1 ci3 ji5 dik1 zaap6 jam1.)

**Example 2:**
The classroom erupted into a cacophony of excited chatter as the bell rang.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 鈴聲響起時，教室裡爆發出刺耳的喧鬧聲。
(Ling4 seng1 hoeng2 hei2 si4, gaau3 sat1 leoi5 baau3 faat1 keoi1 ci3 ji5 dik1 syun1 naau6 seng1.)

---

## 6. Resilience

**Definition:** The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness.
**Cantonese Translation:** 韌性 (ren6 sing3), 恢復力 (fui1 fuk6 lik6)

**Example 1:**
The plant showed remarkable resilience, growing back after being trampled.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 這株植物展現出驚人的韌性，被踩踏後重新長了出來。
(Ceoi4 zyu1 zik6 mat6 zin2 jin6 ceoi1 ging1 jan4 dik1 ren6 sing3, bei6 coi2 daa2 hau6 cung4 san1 zoeng2 liu5 ceoi1 loi4.)

**Example 2:**
Building resilience is crucial for navigating life's challenges.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 培養韌性對於應對生活中的挑戰至關重要。
(Pui4 joeng5 ren6 sing3 si6 deoi3 jyu1 jing3 doi3 sang1 wut6 zung1 dik1 tiu1 zin3 zi3 gwaan1 zung6 jiu3.)
