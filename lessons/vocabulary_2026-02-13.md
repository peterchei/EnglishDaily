# Daily Vocabulary: Emotional Intelligence

1. **Empathy** (Noun)
**Meaning**: The ability to understand and share the feelings of another.
**Cantonese**: 同理心 (Tung lei sam)
**Pronunciation**: /ˈempəθi/
**Example**: Empathy is crucial for building strong relationships.
**Translation**: 同理心對於建立良好關係至關重要。

2. **Resilient** (Adjective)
**Meaning**: Able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions.
**Cantonese**: 韌性 / 適應力強 (Yan sing / Sik ying lik keung)
**Pronunciation**: /rɪˈzɪliənt/
**Example**: She is resilient and never gives up easily.
**Translation**: 佢適應力好強，從來唔會輕易放棄。

3. **Constructive** (Adjective)
**Meaning**: Having or intended to have a useful or beneficial purpose.
**Cantonese**: 有建設性 (Yau gin chit sing)
**Pronunciation**: /kənˈstrʌktɪv/
**Example**: We need constructive feedback to improve the product.
**Translation**: 我哋需要有建設性嘅意見嚟改善產品。
