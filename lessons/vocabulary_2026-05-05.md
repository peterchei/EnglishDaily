# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-05

## 1. **Summit** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsʌm.ɪt/
**Definition:** A meeting between leaders of governments.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 高峰會

**Example 1:**
The Prime Minister is attending a crucial economic summit next week.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
首相下個星期會出席一個重要嘅經濟高峰會。

**Example 2:**
World leaders gathered at the summit to discuss climate change.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
世界各國領袖喺高峰會上面聚集，討論氣候變化。

## 2. **Settle** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈset.əl/
**Definition:** To reach a decision or agreement about something after a period of discussion or argument.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 解決、和解

**Example 1:**
They decided to settle the dispute out of court.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢哋決定庭外和解呢場糾紛。

**Example 2:**
The company agreed to settle the claim for £5 million.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
公司同意賠償五百萬英鎊嚟解決呢個申索。

## 3. **Slated** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsleɪ.tɪd/
**Definition:** Scheduled or planned for a particular time.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 預定嘅、計劃好嘅

**Example 1:**
The meeting is slated for next Tuesday.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
個會議預定下個星期二舉行。

**Example 2:**
The new product launch is slated for the end of the year.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
新產品嘅推出預定喺年尾。

## 4. **Operator** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɒp.ə.reɪ.tər/
**Definition:** A person or company that runs a business or service.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 經營者、營運者

**Example 1:**
The tour operator cancelled the trip due to bad weather.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
因為天氣唔好，旅行社取消咗行程。

**Example 2:**
The new train operator promises to improve services.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
新嘅火車營運者承諾會改善服務。

## 5. **Commercial Vessel** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kəˈmɜːʃəl ˈvesəl/
**Definition:** A ship used for trade or business purposes.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 商船

**Example 1:**
The commercial vessel was carrying goods to Europe.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰艘商船運送緊貨物去歐洲。

**Example 2:**
Many commercial vessels pass through the English Channel every day.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
每日都有好多商船經過英倫海峽。

## 6. **Fundraiser** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfʌndˌreɪ.zər/
**Definition:** An event or activity organised to raise money for a particular purpose.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 籌款活動

**Example 1:**
The school is holding a fundraiser to buy new equipment.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
學校搞緊籌款活動嚟買新設備。

**Example 2:**
The charity organized a gala as a major fundraiser.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
慈善機構舉辦咗一個盛大嘅晚會作為主要嘅籌款活動。

## 7. **Statuesque** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌstætʃ.uˈesk/
**Definition:** (especially of a woman) tall and attractively shaped like a statue.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 如雕像般美麗嘅、高挑而且身材姣好嘅（通常指女性）

**Example 1:**
She has a statuesque figure that commands attention.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢嘅身材好似雕像咁靚，好引人注目。

**Example 2:**
The model's statuesque pose made her look even more elegant.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
模特兒嘅雕像般嘅姿勢令佢睇起嚟更加優雅。
