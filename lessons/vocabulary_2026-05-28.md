# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-28

Hello everyone, welcome to today's English lesson!

## 1. **Protracted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /prəˈtræktɪd/
**Definition:** Lasting for a long time or longer than expected or usual.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 拖延嘅, 持續耐嘅

**Example 1:**
The protracted negotiations finally led to a ceasefire agreement.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
經過漫長嘅談判，終於達成咗停火協議。

**Example 2:**
She suffered a protracted illness before finally passing away.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢患咗一場好耐嘅病，最後先過身。

## 2. **Swathes** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /sweɪðz/
**Definition:** Large areas; wide strips or areas of something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 大片, 廣闊嘅區域

**Example 1:**
Swathes of farmland were destroyed by the floods.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
大片農田被洪水摧毀咗。

**Example 2:**
The new policy will affect swathes of the population.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
新嘅政策將會影響大片嘅人口。

## 3. **Hampering** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈhæmpərɪŋ/
**Definition:** Hindering or impeding the progress or movement of something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 阻礙, 妨礙

**Example 1:**
The bad weather is hampering rescue efforts.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
惡劣嘅天氣阻礙咗救援工作。

**Example 2:**
Bureaucracy is hampering the development of small businesses.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
官僚主義阻礙咗小型企業嘅發展。

## 4. **Flashbacks** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈflæʃbæks/
**Definition:** Vivid, sudden recollections of a past event, typically a traumatic one.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 閃回, 突然回憶起嘅畫面

**Example 1:**
The loud noise triggered flashbacks of the car accident.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
巨大嘅噪音觸發咗佢對車禍嘅閃回。

**Example 2:**
She still suffers from flashbacks years after the incident.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
事隔多年，佢仍然受到閃回嘅困擾。

## 7. **Out of reach** (Phrase)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌaʊt əv ˈriːtʃ/
**Definition:** Beyond one's grasp or capability; unattainable.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 遙不可及, 無法達到

**Example 1:**
Owning a home feels out of reach for many young people.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
對好多後生仔嚟講，擁有自己嘅屋企似乎遙不可及。

**Example 2:**
Success can seem out of reach when you face constant setbacks.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
當你面對不斷嘅挫折時，成功可能顯得遙不可及。
