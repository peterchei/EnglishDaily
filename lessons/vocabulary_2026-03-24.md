# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-24

Hi there, welcome to today's lesson!

## 1. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased; delighted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心; 好高興

**Example 1:**
I was really chuffed to win the competition.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我贏咗比賽真係好開心。

**Example 2:**
She was absolutely chuffed with her birthday present.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢收到生日禮物真係好高興。

## 2. **Dodgy** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈdɒdʒi/
**Definition:** Suspicious, unreliable, or potentially dangerous.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可疑嘅; 靠唔住嘅; 危險嘅

**Example 1:**
That looks like a dodgy street; let's avoid it.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰條街睇落好可疑，我哋避開佢啦。

**Example 2:**
I bought a dodgy phone from a market stall and it broke the next day.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我喺街邊檔買咗部靠唔住嘅電話，第二日就壞咗。

## 3. **Queue** (Noun/Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people waiting for something. To form or wait in a line.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊; 排隊

**Example 1:**
There's a long queue outside the bakery this morning.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今朝麵包舖出面有條長長嘅隊。

**Example 2:**
We had to queue for an hour to get tickets to the concert.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋要排隊排一個鐘先買到演唱會嘅飛。

## 4. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed or upset.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望; 好傷心

**Example 1:**
I was gutted when I failed the exam.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我考試唔合格嗰陣好失望。

**Example 2:**
She was absolutely gutted when her cat ran away.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢隻貓走失咗嗰陣佢好傷心。

## 5. **Taking the Mickey** (Phrase)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈteɪkɪŋ ðə ˈmɪki/
**Definition:** To tease or make fun of someone in a playful way.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 玩嘢; 戲弄; 攞人嚟講笑

**Example 1:**
Are you taking the mickey out of my accent?
**Cantonese Example 1:**
你係咪玩嘢，笑我嘅口音啊？

**Example 2:**
Stop taking the mickey and be serious for once.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好再戲弄人，認真一次啦。

## 6. **Knackered** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnækəd/
**Definition:** Very tired; exhausted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好攰; 精疲力盡

**Example 1:**
I'm absolutely knackered after that long walk.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
行完咁長嘅路之後，我真係好攰。

**Example 2:**
She was too knackered to cook dinner, so we ordered takeaway.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢攰到煮唔到晚餐，所以我哋叫咗外賣。
