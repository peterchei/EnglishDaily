# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-26

Hi there.

## 1. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased or happy.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心，好滿意

**Example 1:**
I was absolutely chuffed to win the competition.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我贏咗個比賽，真係好開心。

**Example 2:**
She was chuffed with her exam results.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢對自己嘅考試成績好滿意。

## 2. **Queue** (Noun/Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people or vehicles waiting for something. To stand in a line.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊列，排隊

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the shop.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
間舖頭出面排咗條好長嘅隊。

**Example 2:**
We had to queue for an hour to get in.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋要排隊排成個鐘先入到去。

## 3. **Knackered** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnækəd/
**Definition:** Very tired; exhausted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好攰，累到散晒

**Example 1:**
I'm absolutely knackered after that run.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
跑完步之後，我真係累到散晒。

**Example 2:**
She looked completely knackered after working all night.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢通宵工作之後，睇落去完全冇晒精神。

## 4. **Dodgy** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈdɒdʒi/
**Definition:** Suspicious, unreliable or potentially dangerous.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可疑嘅，唔可靠嘅，可能有危險嘅

**Example 1:**
That guy looks a bit dodgy to me.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我覺得嗰條友仔有啲可疑。

**Example 2:**
I wouldn't eat that; it looks a bit dodgy.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我唔會食嗰樣嘢，佢睇落去有啲唔妥。

## 5. **Rubbish** (Noun/Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrʌbɪʃ/
**Definition:** Waste material; nonsense. Of very poor quality.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 垃圾；胡說八道；好差

**Example 1:**
Don't talk rubbish! That's not true.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
唔好講埋啲垃圾嘢啦！唔係真嘅。

**Example 2:**
The film was complete rubbish.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
套戲真係好差。

## 6. **Skive** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /skaɪv/
**Definition:** To avoid work or school by staying away or pretending to be ill.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 偷懶，逃學

**Example 1:**
He's always trying to skive off work.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢成日都想偷懶唔做嘢。

**Example 2:**
They used to skive lessons and go to the park.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋以前成日逃學，然後去公園。

## 7. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪlɪənt/
**Definition:** Exceptionally clever or talented; very good.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 聰明；非常好

**Example 1:**
She's a brilliant student.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢係一個好聰明嘅學生。

**Example 2:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰個係一個非常好嘅主意！
