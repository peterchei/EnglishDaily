# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-20

## 1. **Cede** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /siːd/
**Definition:** To give up (power or territory).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 放棄 (權力或領土)

**Example 1:**
The government was unwilling to cede any of its authority.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府唔願意放棄任何權力。

**Example 2:**
They were forced to cede control of the company.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋被迫放棄公司嘅控制權。

## 2. **Staggered** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈstæɡərd/
**Definition:** Deeply shocked or astonished.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 極度震驚或驚訝

**Example 1:**
I was staggered by the news of his sudden death.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我對佢突然過身嘅消息感到非常震驚。

**Example 2:**
The sheer scale of the project left her staggered.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢個項目嘅巨大規模令佢感到震驚。

## 3. **Withhold** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /wɪθˈhəʊld/
**Definition:** To suppress or hold back (something).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 扣留或隱瞞 (某樣嘢)

**Example 1:**
The police withheld crucial information from the public.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
警方向公眾隱瞞咗關鍵信息。

**Example 2:**
The company decided to withhold dividends this year.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
公司決定今年唔派股息。

## 4. **Looming** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈluːmɪŋ/
**Definition:** Appearing as a large, indistinct, and often threatening shape.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 朦朧咁出現，通常帶有威脅性

**Example 1:**
The threat of recession is looming over the country.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
經濟衰退嘅威脅籠罩住成個國家。

**Example 2:**
A deadline is looming, and we need to finish the project.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
死線臨近，我哋需要完成個項目。

## 5. **Purée** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈpjʊəreɪ/
**Definition:** A smooth, creamy food made of crushed or liquidized fruits or vegetables.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 由壓碎或液化嘅水果或蔬菜製成嘅幼滑奶油狀食物。

**Example 1:**
The baby enjoyed the apple purée.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
個BB好鍾意食蘋果蓉。

**Example 2:**
She made a delicious tomato purée for the pasta sauce.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢整咗美味嘅番茄蓉嚟做意粉醬。

## 6. **Malicious** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /məˈlɪʃəs/
**Definition:** Intending or intended to do harm.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 意圖或旨在造成傷害嘅。

**Example 1:**
The rumours were spread by malicious individuals.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
啲謠言係由惡意嘅人散播嘅。

**Example 2:**
His comments were clearly malicious and intended to hurt her feelings.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢嘅評論明顯帶有惡意，旨在傷害佢嘅感情。
