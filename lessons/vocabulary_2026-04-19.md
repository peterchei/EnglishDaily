# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-19

## 1. **Blockade** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /blɒˈkeɪd/
**Definition:** An act or means of sealing off a place to prevent goods or people from entering or leaving.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 封鎖

**Example 1:**
The naval blockade prevented essential supplies from reaching the city.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
海軍封鎖阻止咗必需品運到嗰個城市。

**Example 2:**
The protestors formed a human blockade to stop the construction trucks.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
示威者組成咗人鏈封鎖，阻止建築車輛通過。

## 2. **Hostage** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈhɒstɪdʒ/
**Definition:** A person seized or held as security for the fulfillment of a condition.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 人質

**Example 1:**
The gunman released one hostage as a sign of good faith.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
槍手釋放咗一個人質，以示誠意。

**Example 2:**
Negotiations are ongoing to secure the release of all the hostages.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
談判緊進行中，以確保所有人質得到釋放。

## 3. **Arson** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɑːrsən/
**Definition:** The criminal act of deliberately setting fire to property.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 縱火

**Example 1:**
Police are investigating the arson attack on the warehouse.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
警方正在調查倉庫嘅縱火襲擊。

**Example 2:**
The suspect was charged with arson and endangering lives.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
疑犯被控縱火同埋危害生命。

## 4. **Misinterpreted** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪtɪd/
**Definition:** To interpret (something) wrongly.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 誤解

**Example 1:**
His comments were widely misinterpreted by the media.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢嘅評論被媒體廣泛誤解。

**Example 2:**
I think you've misinterpreted what I was trying to say.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我覺得你誤解咗我想講嘅嘢。

## 5. **Sophisticated** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /səˈfɪstɪkeɪtɪd/
**Definition:** Having, revealing, or involving a great deal of worldly experience and knowledge.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 精明; 複雜

**Example 1:**
Scammers are using increasingly sophisticated techniques to steal money.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
騙徒使用越嚟越精明嘅技術去偷錢。

**Example 2:**
The new software has a sophisticated security system.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢個新軟件有一個複雜嘅安全系統。

## 6. **Address** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈdres/
**Definition:** To speak to (a person or an assembly), typically in a formal way. Also, to deal with or tackle (a matter or problem).
**Cantonese Meaning:** (對...）講話; 處理

**Example 1:**
The Prime Minister will address the nation tonight.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
首相今晚將會向全國發表講話。

**Example 2:**
We need to address the problem of climate change urgently.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋需要緊急處理氣候變化嘅問題。

## 7. **Firm** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /fɜːrm/
**Definition:** A business organization; a company or partnership.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 公司

**Example 1:**
She works for a large accounting firm.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢喺一間大型會計公司工作。

**Example 2:**
The technology firm announced record profits.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰間科技公司宣佈咗破紀錄嘅利潤。
