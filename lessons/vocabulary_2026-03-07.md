# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-07

Hi there,

## 1. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased or delighted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心, 好滿意

**Example 1:**
I was really chuffed to win the prize.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我贏咗個獎真係好開心。

**Example 2:**
She's absolutely chuffed with her new car.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢對佢架新車非常滿意。

## 2. **Queue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people waiting their turn.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊, 排隊

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the cinema.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
戲院外面有條好長嘅隊。

**Example 2:**
Please join the queue and wait for your turn.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
請排隊，等輪到你。

## 3. **Rubbish** (Noun/Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrʌbɪʃ/
**Definition:** Waste material; nonsense.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 垃圾; 廢話

**Example 1:**
The bin is full of rubbish.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
個垃圾桶裝滿垃圾。

**Example 2:**
That's a load of rubbish! He's lying.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰啲全部都係廢話！佢講緊大話。

## 4. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪljənt/
**Definition:** Exceptionally clever or talented; very good.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好叻, 好出色; 好好

**Example 1:**
She's a brilliant scientist.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢係一個好出色嘅科學家。

**Example 2:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰個係個好好嘅主意！

## 5. **Cheers** (Exclamation)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃɪəz/
**Definition:** A toast; thanks; goodbye.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 乾杯; 多謝; 再見

**Example 1:**
Cheers, everyone! Let's drink to the happy couple.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
乾杯，各位！我哋一齊為呢對幸福嘅新人慶祝。

**Example 2:**
Cheers for your help!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
多謝你嘅幫忙！

## 6. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed; devastated.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望, 好沮喪

**Example 1:**
I was gutted when I failed the exam.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我考試唔合格嗰陣，我好失望。

**Example 2:**
She's absolutely gutted that she missed the concert.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢錯過咗演唱會，佢覺得好沮喪。
