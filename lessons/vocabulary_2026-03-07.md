# Vocabulary Lesson: March 7, 2026

Today's lesson focuses on some wonderful English words to enrich your vocabulary.

## 1. Serendipity
*   **Cantonese:** 意外的發現，尤指幸運的巧合 (jat6 noi6 dik1 faat3 jin6, jau4 zi2 sing3 hang6 dik1 gaau2 hap6)
*   **Definition:** The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.

    **Example 1:** Finding a rare book in a dusty antique shop was a moment of pure serendipity.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 在布滿灰塵的古董店裡找到一本稀有的書，真是純粹的意外之喜。(zoi6 bou3 man5 fat1 fan1 mai5 dung3 dim3 leoi5 zaau2 dou3 jat1 bun2 hei1 jau5 ge3 syu1, zan1 si6 seon4 seoi3 dik1 ji3 ngoi6 zi1 hei2.)

    **Example 2:** Their meeting was a serendipity that changed both their lives.
    **Cantonese Example 2:** 他們的相遇是一種改變兩人一生的幸運巧合。(taa1 mun4 ge3 soeng1 jyu6 si6 jat1 zung2 goi2 bin3 loeng5 jan4 jat1 sang1 ge3 hang6 wan6 gaau2 hap6.)

## 2. Ephemeral
*   **Cantonese:** 短暫的，倏忽的 (dyun2 zaam6 dik1, suk1 hwat1 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Lasting for a very short time.

    **Example 1:** The beauty of a cherry blossom is ephemeral, lasting only a week or two.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 櫻花的盛開是短暫的，只能持續一兩個星期。(jing1 faa1 dik1 sing4 hoi1 si6 dyun2 zaam6 dik1, zi2 nang4 ci4 zat1 jat1 leong5 go3 sing1 kei4.)

    **Example 2:** Fame can be ephemeral.
    **Cantonese Example 2:** 名聲可以是短暫的。(ming4 sing1 ho2 ji5 ji5 syu6 dyun2 zaam6 dik1.)

## 3. Ubiquitous
*   **Cantonese:** 無處不在的，普遍存在的 (mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi6 dik1, pou2 pin3 cyun1 zoi6 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Present, appearing, or found everywhere.

    **Example 1:** Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 智能手機在現代社會已經變得無處不在。(zi1 jing4 sau2 gei1 zoi6 jin6 doi6 se5 wui6 ji5 ging1 bin3 dou1 mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi6.)

    **Example 2:** Coffee shops are ubiquitous in the city center.
    **Cantonese Example 2:** 市中心到處都是咖啡店。(si5 zung1 sam1 dou3 cyu3 dou1 dou1 gaa3 fei1 dim3.)

## 4. Mellifluous
*   **Cantonese:** 聲音甜美的，悅耳的 (sing1 jam1 tim4 mei5 dik1, jyut6 jiu3 dik1)
*   **Definition:** (of a voice or words) Sweet or musical; pleasant to hear.

    **Example 1:** The singer's mellifluous voice captivated the audience.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 歌手那悅耳的歌聲迷住了觀眾。(go1 sau2 naa5 jyut6 jiu3 dik1 go1 seng1 mai4 zyu6 soeng5 gwaan1 zung3.)

    **Example 2:** He spoke in a mellifluous tone that put everyone at ease.
    **Cantonese Example 2:** 他以一種溫柔悅耳的語氣說話，讓每個人都感到放鬆。(taa1 ji5 jat1 zung2 wan1 jau4 jyut6 jiu3 dik1 jyu5 hei3 syut3 waa6, joeng6 mui5 go3 jan4 dou1 gam2 dou3 fong3 sung1.)

## 5. Resilient
*   **Cantonese:** 有彈性的；能迅速恢復的 (jau5 taan4 sing3 dik1; nang4 seon3 fat1 wui6 fu6 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions.

    **Example 1:** Despite the setbacks, the team remained resilient and focused on their goal.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 儘管遇到了挫折，團隊仍然保持堅韌，專注於目標。(gan2 gwong2 jyu5 jim6 dou3 cou3 zit6, tyun4 deoi6 jing4 joeng4 bou2 ci4 gan1 jan6, zyun1 zuk3 jyu5 muk6 biu1.)

    **Example 2:** Children are often more resilient than adults.
    **Cantonese Example 2:** 孩子們往往比成年人更有彈性。(haai4 zi2 mun4 wong5 wong5 bei2 sing4 nin4 jan4 gang3 jau5 taan4 sing3.)

## 6. Petrichor
*   **Cantonese:** 雨後泥土的芬芳 (jyu5 hau6 nai4 tou2 dik1 fan1 fong1)
*   **Definition:** A pleasant smell that frequently accompanies the first rain after a long period of warm, dry weather.

    **Example 1:** After the storm, the air was filled with the fresh scent of petrichor.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 雨停之後，空氣中瀰漫著雨後泥土的清新香味。(jyu5 ting4 zi1 hau6, hung1 hei3 zung1 mei4 maan6 zoek6 jyu5 hau6 nai4 tou2 dik1 cing1 san1 hoeng1 mei6.)

    **Example 2:** She loved walking after the rain, breathing in the petrichor.
    **Cantonese Example 2:** 她喜歡雨後散步，呼吸著泥土的芬芳。(taa1 hei2 fun1 jyu5 hau6 saan3 bou3, fu1 hei1 zoek6 nai4 tou2 dik1 fan1 fong1.)

## 7. Eloquent
*   **Cantonese:** 口才好的，能言善辯的 (hau2 coi4 hou2 dik1, nang4 jin4 sin6 bin3 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing.

    **Example 1:** The politician delivered an eloquent speech that moved the crowd.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 這位政治家發表了一篇口才極佳的演講，感動了在場的群眾。(ze2 wai6 zing3 zi6 gaa1 faat1 baau1 liu5 jat1 pin1 hau2 coi4 kap1 gaa1 gei3 jin2 zoeng2, gam2 dung6 liu5 zoi6 coeng4 gei3 kwan4 zung3.)

    **Example 2:** Her essay was eloquent and insightful.
    **Cantonese Example 2:** 她的文章文筆優美且見解深刻。(taa1 dik1 man1 zoeng1 man4 bat1 jau1 mei6 cyu2 gin3 gaai2 sam1 san1 hat6.)
