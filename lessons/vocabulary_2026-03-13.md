# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-13

Hi there,

## 1. **Indeed** (Adverb)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪnˈdiːd/
**Definition:** In truth; in fact; certainly.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 確實, 真的

**Example 1:**
It is very cold. Indeed, I’m freezing.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
真係好凍。確實，我凍到震。

**Example 2:**
That is a good point indeed.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰個真係一個好嘅觀點。

## 2. **Queue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people waiting for something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊伍

**Example 1:**
We had to stand in a long queue to get tickets.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我哋要排長隊先買到飛。

**Example 2:**
Please join the queue at the back.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
請喺後面排隊。

## 3. **Cheers** (Exclamation)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃɪərz/
**Definition:** (Informal) Thank you; goodbye; a toast.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 多謝, 再見, 乾杯

**Example 1:**
Cheers for your help!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
多謝你嘅幫忙!

**Example 2:**
Cheers, see you later!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
再見，遲啲見!

## 4. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed or upset.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望, 好傷心

**Example 1:**
I was absolutely gutted when I failed my exam.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我考試唔合格嘅時候，真係好失望。

**Example 2:**
She was gutted when her favourite team lost.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢鍾意嘅球隊輸咗，佢好傷心。

## 5. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪliənt/
**Definition:** Very good; outstanding; clever.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好好, 好叻, 聰明

**Example 1:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰個係一個好好嘅主意！

**Example 2:**
She's a brilliant student.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢係一個好叻嘅學生。

## 6. **Takeaway** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈteɪkəˌweɪ/
**Definition:** A meal bought at a restaurant or shop and eaten elsewhere.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 外賣

**Example 1:**
Let's get a takeaway tonight.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我哋今晚叫外賣啦。

**Example 2:**
I'm ordering a Chinese takeaway.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我叫緊中式外賣。
