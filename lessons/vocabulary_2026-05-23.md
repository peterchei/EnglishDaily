# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-23

Hello everyone! Today we're learning some useful words and phrases from the news.

## 1. **Vows** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /vaʊz/
**Definition:** Solemnly promise to do a specified thing.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 發誓 (faat3 sai6)

**Example 1:**
The president vows to improve healthcare for all citizens.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
總統發誓要改善所有公民嘅醫療保健。 (zung2 tung2 faat3 sai6 jiu3 gaai2 sin6 so2 jau5 gung1 man4 ge3 ji1 liu4 bou2 gin6.)

**Example 2:**
She vows never to speak to him again after their argument.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢發誓喺佢哋嘈交之後永遠唔會再同佢講嘢。(keoi5 faat3 sai6 hai2 keoi5 dei6 caau2 gaau1 zi1 hau6 wing5 jyun5 m4 wui5 zoi3 tung4 keoi5 gong2 je5.)

## 2. **Custodial** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /kʌˈstəʊdiəl/
**Definition:** Relating to or denoting imprisonment.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 監禁嘅 (gaam1 gam3 ge3)

**Example 1:**
The judge handed down a custodial sentence of five years.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
法官判咗五年監禁刑期。 (faat3 gun1 pun3 zo2 ng5 nin4 gaam1 gam3 ying4 kei4.)

**Example 2:**
Custodial care is provided in prisons and detention centres.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
監禁式嘅照顧喺監獄同拘留中心提供。(gaam1 gam3 sik1 ge3 ziu3 gu3 hai2 gaam1 juk6 tung4 keoi1 lau4 zung1 sam1 tai4 cung1.)

## 3. **Reassure** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌriːəˈʃʊə(r)/
**Definition:** To say or do something to remove the doubts and fears of someone.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 使安心 (sai2 on1 sam1)

**Example 1:**
The doctor tried to reassure the patient that the treatment was safe.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
醫生試圖令病人安心，話治療係安全嘅。(ji1 sang1 si3 tou4 ling6 beng6 jan2 on1 sam1, waa6 zi6 liu4 hai6 on1 cyun4 ge3.)

**Example 2:**
He reassured her that everything would be alright.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢令佢安心，話一切都會好嘅。(keoi5 ling6 keoi5 on1 sam1, waa6 jat1 cai3 dou1 wui5 hou2 ge3.)

## 4. **Trials** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtraɪəlz/
**Definition:** A formal examination of evidence before a judge, and typically a jury, in order to decide guilt in a case of criminal or civil proceedings.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 審訊 (sam2 seon3)

**Example 1:**
The company announced they would begin clinical trials for the new drug.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
公司宣布佢哋會開始新藥嘅臨床試驗。(gung1 si1 syun1 bou3 keoi5 dei6 wui5 hoi1 ci2 san1 joek6 ge3 lam4 cong4 si3 jim6.)

**Example 2:**
He is currently on trial for fraud.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢而家因為詐騙案而接受審訊。(keoi5 ji4 gaa1 jan1 wai6 zaa3 pin3 on3 ji4 zip3 sau6 sam2 seon3.)

## 5. **Detained** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /dɪˈteɪnd/
**Definition:** To keep (someone) in official custody, typically for questioning about a crime or in politically sensitive circumstances.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 拘留 (keoi1 lau4)

**Example 1:**
The suspect was detained by police for questioning.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
疑犯被警方拘留問話。(ji4 faan2 bei2 ging2 fong1 keoi1 lau4 man6 waa6.)

**Example 2:**
Several protestors were detained during the demonstration.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
示威期間，有幾名示威者被拘留。(si6 wai1 kei4 gaan1, jau5 gei2 ming4 si6 wai1 ze2 bei2 keoi1 lau4.)

## 6. **Flooded** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈflʌdɪd/
**Definition:** Covered or overwhelmed with water.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 水浸 (seoi2 zam3)

**Example 1:**
The streets were flooded after the heavy rain.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
大雨之後，街道被水浸咗。(daai6 yu5 zi1 hau6, gaai1 dou6 bei2 seoi2 zam3 zo2.)

**Example 2:**
The basement was flooded due to a burst pipe.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
由於水管爆裂，地庫被水浸咗。(jau4 yu1 seoi2 gun2 baau3 lit6, dei6 fu3 bei2 seoi2 zam3 zo2.)
