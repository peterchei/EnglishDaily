# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-05

## Word 1: Ephemeral
**Cantonese:** 短暫的，朝生暮死的
**Definition:** Lasting for a very short time; fleeting.
**Example 1:** The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a week or two each spring.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 櫻花的盛開期非常短暫，每年春天只持續一兩個星期。
**Example 2:** Social media trends are often ephemeral, quickly replaced by new ones.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 社交媒體的潮流往往轉瞬即逝，很快就會被新的潮流取代。

## Word 2: Serendipity
**Cantonese:** 意外發現珍寶的運氣
**Definition:** The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.
**Example 1:** Finding a rare book in a dusty antique shop was a moment of pure serendipity.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 在一家塵封的舊貨店裡找到一本稀有的書，這真是一種純粹的意外發現。
**Example 2:** Their meeting was a happy serendipity that led to a lifelong friendship.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 他們的相遇是一次愉快的巧合，並發展成了一段終生的友誼。

## Word 3: Ubiquitous
**Cantonese:** 無處不在的
**Definition:** Present, appearing, or found everywhere.
**Example 1:** Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society, connecting people worldwide.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 在現代社會，智能手機已變得無處不在，將世界各地的人們聯繫起來。
**Example 2:** Coffee shops are ubiquitous in most cities, offering a place to relax or work.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 咖啡店在大多數城市都隨處可見，提供了一個放鬆或工作的場所。

## Word 4: Mellifluous
**Cantonese:** 聲音甜美的
**Definition:** (of a voice or words) Sweet or musical; pleasant to hear.
**Example 1:** The singer's mellifluous voice captivated the audience.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 這位歌手甜美的聲音深深吸引了觀眾。
**Example 2:** He spoke in a mellifluous tone that soothed everyone in the room.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 他用一種悅耳動聽的語調說話，讓房間裡的每個人都感到舒緩。

## Word 5: Quixotic
**Cantonese:** 唐吉訶德式的
**Definition:** Exceedingly idealistic; unrealistic and impractical.
**Example 1:** His quixotic quest to end world poverty was admirable but ultimately unrealistic.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 他結束世界貧困的唐吉訶德式的追求是值得讚賞的，但最終不切實際。
**Example 2:** Starting a business with no funding and no plan is a quixotic endeavor.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 在沒有資金也沒有計劃的情況下創辦一家公司，這是一項不切實際的努力。

## Word 6: Elucidate
**Cantonese:** 闡明，說明
**Definition:** Make (something) clear; explain.
**Example 1:** The professor used diagrams to elucidate the complex scientific theory.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 教授使用圖表來闡明這個複雜的科學理論。
**Example 2:** Could you please elucidate the main points of your proposal?
**Cantonese Example 2:** 你能否請你闡明你提案的主要論點？

## Word 7: Resilient
**Cantonese:** 有韌性的，能迅速恢復的
**Definition:** Able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions.
**Example 1:** The community proved resilient in the face of natural disasters, rebuilding stronger than before.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 面對自然災害，社區展現了強大的韌性，重建後比以前更加強大。
**Example 2:** Children are often more resilient than adults, bouncing back from setbacks with surprising speed.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 孩子們通常比成年人更有韌性，能以驚人的速度從挫折中恢復過來。
