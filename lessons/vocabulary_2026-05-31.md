# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-31

Hello everyone! Here are a few useful English words from today's news.

## 1. **Quell** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kwɛl/
**Definition:** To suppress or subdue (a riot or other disturbance).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 鎮壓, 平息

**Example 1:**
The police used tear gas to quell the unrest.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
警察用催淚彈嚟鎮壓暴動。

**Example 2:**
The government tried to quell public fears about the new virus.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
政府試圖平息公眾對新型病毒嘅恐懼。

## 2. **Enigmatic** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪk/
**Definition:** Mysterious and difficult to understand.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 神秘莫測嘅

**Example 1:**
He had an enigmatic smile on his face.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢面上帶住一個神秘莫測嘅笑容。

**Example 2:**
The company's future plans remain enigmatic.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
公司嘅未來計劃仍然神秘莫測。

## 3. **Decimated** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈdɛsɪmeɪtɪd/
**Definition:** To destroy or severely damage a large proportion of something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 大量摧毀

**Example 1:**
The forest was decimated by the fire.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰片森林俾大火大量摧毀咗。

**Example 2:**
The disease decimated the local population.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰隻病大量摧毀咗本地人口。

## 4. **Pact** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /pækt/
**Definition:** A formal agreement or treaty between two or more parties.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 協定，條約

**Example 1:**
The two countries signed a defence pact.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰兩個國家簽署咗一份國防協定。

**Example 2:**
They made a pact to always be honest with each other.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋達成咗一個協定，要永遠對大家坦誠。

## 5. **Alarming** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈlɑːrmɪŋ/
**Definition:** Causing worry or fear.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 令人擔憂嘅, 令人恐懼嘅

**Example 1:**
The rise in crime is alarming.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
犯罪率嘅上升令人擔憂。

**Example 2:**
The news about the economy is quite alarming.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
有關經濟嘅消息非常令人恐懼。

## 6. **Authenticity** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌɔːθenˈtɪsɪti/
**Definition:** The quality of being genuine or true.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 真實性，真確性

**Example 1:**

**Cantonese Example 1:**

**Example 2:**

**Cantonese Example 2:**

## 7. **Grounded** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡraʊndɪd/
**Definition:** Sensible, realistic, and closely connected to practical matters.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 腳踏實地嘅, 實際嘅

**Example 1:**
She is a grounded and sensible person.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢係一個腳踏實地同埋理智嘅人。

**Example 2:**
The company needs a more grounded approach to its finances.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
