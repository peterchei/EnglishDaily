# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-25

Hello everyone! Here are some useful English words and phrases based on today's news.

## 1. **Spare** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /spɛər/
**Definition:** To refrain from harming or destroying; to give something to someone because it is needed.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 饒恕; 撥出

**Example 1:**
The judge decided to spare the boys from a prison sentence.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
法官決定饒恕嗰班男仔，判佢哋唔使坐監。

**Example 2:**
Could you spare a few minutes to help me with this task?
**Cantonese Example 2:**
你可唔可以撥啲時間幫我做呢個任務？

## 2. **Brace** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /breɪs/
**Definition:** To prepare oneself mentally or physically for something unwelcome or challenging.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 準備; 支撐

**Example 1:**
The UK is bracing for the hottest May day on record.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
英國正準備迎接有紀錄以來最熱嘅五月天。

**Example 2:**
Brace yourself, this news might be difficult to hear.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
準備定，呢個消息可能唔容易接受。

## 3. **Jam** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /dʒæm/
**Definition:** To deliberately interfere with or block (a radio signal or other transmission).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 阻塞; 干擾

**Example 1:**
The RAF jet's signal was jammed near the Russian border.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
皇家空軍噴射機嘅訊號喺俄羅斯邊境附近被干擾。

**Example 2:**
Someone is trying to jam our communications.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
有人想阻塞我哋嘅通訊。

## 4. **Fled** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /flɛd/
**Definition:** Past tense and past participle of 'flee': to run away from a place or situation of danger.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 逃離; 逃走

**Example 1:**
She fled the country after the ban on girls' education.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
喺禁止女仔接受教育之後，佢逃離咗個國家。

**Example 2:**
They fled their homes due to the ongoing conflict.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋因為持續嘅衝突而逃離家園。

## 5. **Hypersonic** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌhaɪpəˈsɒnɪk/
**Definition:** Relating to or denoting speeds of more than five times the speed of sound.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 高超音速嘅

**Example 1:**
Russia used a hypersonic missile in the attack on Ukraine.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
俄羅斯喺襲擊烏克蘭嘅時候使用咗高超音速導彈。

**Example 2:**
Hypersonic technology is being developed for military applications.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
高超音速技術正被開發用於軍事用途。

## 6. **Riot police** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈraɪət pəˈliːs/
**Definition:** A police force specially trained and equipped to deal with riots.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 防暴警察

**Example 1:**
Turkish riot police stormed the opposition offices.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
土耳其防暴警察衝入反對派辦公室。

**Example 2:**
The riot police used tear gas to disperse the crowd.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
防暴警察使用催淚彈驅散人群。

## 7. **Abundance of caution** (Phrase)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈbʌndəns əv ˈkɔːʃən/
**Definition:** Taking extra care or being especially careful in a situation, even if it seems unnecessary.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 極度謹慎; 非常小心

**Example 1:**
Waymo paused robotaxis out of an abundance of caution.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
Waymo 出於極度謹慎而暫停咗無人駕駛的士。

**Example 2:**
We cancelled the event out of an abundance of caution due to the bad weather forecast.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
由於惡劣嘅天氣預報，我哋出於極度謹慎而取消咗活動。
