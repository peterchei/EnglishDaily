# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-02

Hi there,

## 1. **Cheers** (Exclamation/Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃɪəz/
**Definition:** An informal expression of goodwill or gratitude; a toast.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 多謝; 乾杯

**Example 1:**
"Cheers for helping me with my homework!"
**Cantonese Example 1:**
"多謝你幫我做功課呀！"

**Example 2:**
"Let's raise our glasses and say cheers to the happy couple!"
**Cantonese Example 2:**
"我哋一齊舉杯，祝呢對新人乾杯！"

## 2. **Queue** (Noun/Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people waiting for something. To stand in line.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊; 排隊

**Example 1:**
"There's a long queue outside the bakery this morning."
**Cantonese Example 1:**
"今朝麵包舖外面排咗條好長嘅隊。"

**Example 2:**
"We need to queue up to buy tickets for the concert."
**Cantonese Example 2:**
"我哋要排隊買演唱會嘅飛。"

## 3. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed or upset.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望; 好傷心

**Example 1:**
"I was gutted when I failed the exam."
**Cantonese Example 1:**
"我考試唔合格，好失望。"

**Example 2:**
"She was absolutely gutted when her favourite football team lost."
**Cantonese Example 2:**
"佢最鍾意嘅足球隊輸咗，佢非常傷心。"

## 4. **Knackered** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnækəd/
**Definition:** Very tired; exhausted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好攰; 疲憊

**Example 1:**
"I'm absolutely knackered after that long walk."
**Cantonese Example 1:**
"行咗咁耐，我真係好攰。"

**Example 2:**
"He looked completely knackered after working all night."
**Cantonese Example 2:**
"佢通宵工作之後，睇起嚟好疲憊。"

## 5. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪliənt/
**Definition:** Extremely clever or talented; very good.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好叻; 非常好

**Example 1:**
"She's a brilliant scientist."
**Cantonese Example 1:**
"佢係一個好叻嘅科學家。"

**Example 2:**
"That's a brilliant idea!"
**Cantonese Example 2:**
"嗰個係一個非常好嘅主意！"

## 6. **Takeaway** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈteɪkəweɪ/
**Definition:** A meal bought at a restaurant or shop to be eaten elsewhere.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 外賣

**Example 1:**
"Let's get a takeaway tonight, I don't feel like cooking."
**Cantonese Example 1:**
"我哋今晚叫外賣啦，我唔想煮飯。"

**Example 2:**
"The Chinese takeaway down the street is really good."
**Cantonese Example 2:**
"街尾嗰間中式外賣真係好好食。"

## 7. **Fortnight** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfɔːrtnaɪt/
**Definition:** A period of two weeks.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 兩星期; 十四日

**Example 1:**
"We're going on holiday for a fortnight."
**Cantonese Example 1:**
"我哋會去旅行兩個星期。"

**Example 2:**
"The results will be available in a fortnight."
**Cantonese Example 2:**
"結果將會喺兩星期後公佈。"
