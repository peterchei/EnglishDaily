# Daily English Lesson - 2026-07-01

Hi there! Here are 6 useful words and phrases for today's lesson, selected from the latest news headlines.

## 1. **In-tray** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɪn.treɪ/
**Definition:** A physical or metaphorical tray or list containing tasks, documents, or issues that someone is responsible for dealing with.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 待辦事項；處理中的文件

**Example 1:**
The new minister found a difficult in-tray waiting for her on her first day in office.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
新部長上任第一天，就發現有一堆棘手的待辦事項等著她處理。

**Example 2:**
Managing the rising energy prices will be a major priority in the Prime Minister's in-tray.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
處理能源價格上漲將會是首相待辦事項中的首要任務。

## 2. **Kicks in** (Phrasal Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kɪks ɪn/
**Definition:** To begin to take effect or become operative.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 開始生效；開始發揮作用

**Example 1:**
The new price cap for electricity bills kicks in at the start of next month.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
新的電費價格上限將於下月初生效。

**Example 2:**
You will notice the effects of the medication once it kicks in after about twenty minutes.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
大約二十分鐘後藥效開始發揮，你就會感覺到效果了。

## 3. **Outpaces** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌaʊtˈpeɪ.sɪz/
**Definition:** To go faster or develop more quickly than someone or something else.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 超過；領先；發展速度快於

**Example 1:**
His investment in cryptocurrency far outpaces his traditional earnings from property.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
他在加密貨幣上的投資回報遠遠超過了他從房地產獲得的傳統收入。

**Example 2:**
Demand for new housing currently outpaces the speed at which we can build them.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
目前對新住宅的需求超過了我們建設的速度。

## 4. **Birthright** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbɜːθ.raɪt/
**Definition:** A particular right or privilege that a person is entitled to from birth, especially the right to citizenship.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 與生俱來的權利（尤指出生公民權）

**Example 1:**
The court ruling protected the principle of birthright citizenship for those born in the country.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
法庭裁決保障了在該國出生者的出生公民權原則。

**Example 2:**
He believes that access to clean water should be a basic birthright for every human.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
他認為獲得乾淨的水應是每個人與生俱來的基本權利。

## 5. **Scrubbed** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /skrʌbd/
**Definition:** To remove something, especially evidence or records, or to cancel a planned event.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 刪除；擦掉；取消（計劃）

**Example 1:**
Authorities scrubbed the surveillance footage to hide the damage caused by the collision.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
當局刪除了監控錄像，以掩蓋碰撞造成的損壞。

**Example 2:**
The space mission was scrubbed due to the extreme weather conditions at the launch site.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
由於發射地點天氣惡劣，太空任務被迫取消。

## 6. **Commuting** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kəˈmjuː.tɪŋ/
**Definition:** The act of travelling some distance between one's home and place of work or study on a regular basis.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 通勤

**Example 1:**
Many people in London rely on e-scooters for their daily commuting despite the legal restrictions.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
儘管有法律限制，許多倫敦人仍依賴電動滑板車進行日常通勤。

**Example 2:**
I spend over an hour each day commuting by train, which gives me time to read.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我每天搭火車通勤花費超過一小時，這讓我有些時間可以閱讀。
