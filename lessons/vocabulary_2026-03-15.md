# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-15

Hi there, today we'll learn some useful English words and phrases.

## 1. **Cheers** (Exclamation)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃɪəz/
**Definition:** A friendly expression used for saying goodbye or thank you, or when raising a glass in a toast.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 多謝 (do1 ze6), 再見 (zoi3 gin3), 乾杯 (gon1 bui1)

**Example 1:**
Cheers for helping me with that!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
多謝你幫我做嗰件事呀！ (do1 ze6 nei5 bong1 ngo5 zou6 go2 gin6 si6 aa3!)

**Example 2:**
Right, I'm off now. Cheers!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
好啦，我走先啦。拜拜！ (hou2 laa1, ngo5 zau2 sin1 laa1. baai1 baai3!)

## 2. **Queue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people waiting for something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊 (deoi2), 排隊 (paai4 deoi2)

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the cinema.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
戲院出面有一條好長嘅隊。 (hei3 jyun2 chut1 min6 jau5 jat1 tiu4 hou2 cheung4 ge3 deoi2.)

**Example 2:**
We had to stand in the queue for over an hour.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋要喺條隊度企咗超過一個鐘。 (ngo5 dei6 jiu3 hai2 tiu4 deoi2 dou6 kei5 zo2 chiu1 gwo3 jat1 go3 jung1.)

## 3. **Lovely** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈlʌvli/
**Definition:** Beautiful; very pleasing or enjoyable.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好靚 (hou2 leng3), 好正 (hou2 zeng3), 好好 (hou2 hou2)

**Example 1:**
What a lovely day!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今日天氣真係好好！ (gam1 jat6 tin1 hei3 zan1 hai6 hou2 hou2!)

**Example 2:**
That's a lovely dress you're wearing.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
你著緊嘅嗰條裙好靚呀。 (nei5 jeuk3 gan2 ge3 go2 tiu4 kwan4 hou2 leng3 aa3.)

## 4. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪljənt/
**Definition:** Exceptionally clever or talented; very good.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好叻 (hou2 lek1), 好棒 (hou2 paang5), 好正 (hou2 zeng3)

**Example 1:**
She gave a brilliant performance.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢嘅演出好棒。 (keoi5 ge3 yin2 chut1 hou2 paang5.)

**Example 2:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢個主意真係好正！ (ni1 go3 zyu2 yi3 zan1 hai6 hou2 zeng3!)

## 5. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed or upset.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望 (hou2 sat1 mong6), 好唔開心 (hou2 m4 hoi1 sam1)

**Example 1:**
I was gutted when I failed the exam.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我考試唔合格，真係好失望。 (ngo5 haau2 si5 m4 hap6 gaak3, zan1 hai6 hou2 sat1 mong6.)

**Example 2:**
He was gutted that he couldn't come to the party.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢嚟唔到個派對，佢覺得好唔開心。 (keoi5 lei4 m4 dou2 go3 paai3 deoi2, keoi5 gok3 dak1 hou2 m4 hoi1 sam1.)

## 6. **To fancy** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfænsi/
**Definition:** To want something or want to do something; to be attracted to someone.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 鍾意 (zung1 yi3), 想 (seung2), 想要 (seung2 jiu3)

**Example 1:**
Do you fancy going to the cinema tonight?
**Cantonese Example 1:**
你今晚想唔想去睇戲呀？ (nei5 gam1 maan1 seung2 m4 seung2 heui3 tai2 hei3 aa3?)

**Example 2:**
I think he fancies her.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我覺得佢鍾意咗佢。 (ngo5 gok3 dak1 keoi5 zung1 yi3 zo2 keoi5.)
