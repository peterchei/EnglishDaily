# Daily English Lesson - 2026-07-02

## 1. **Casualties** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈkæʒjuəltiz/
**Definition:** People killed or injured in a war or an accident.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 傷亡人員

**Example 1:**
The official report confirmed a high number of casualties following the missile strike.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
官方報告證實導彈襲擊後有大量傷亡。

**Example 2:**
Emergency services are working hard to locate any further casualties in the area.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
緊急服務人員正努力搜尋該區是否有更多傷亡者。

## 2. **Windfall** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈwɪndfɔːl/
**Definition:** An amount of money that you receive unexpectedly.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 意外之財

**Example 1:**
The unexpected tax refund came as a welcome windfall for the family.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
這筆意料之外的退稅對這個家庭來說是一筆受歡迎的意外之財。

**Example 2:**
Critics argue that such a massive financial windfall should be taxed more heavily.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
批評者認為如此巨大的財務意外之財應該被徵收更高的稅。

## 3. **Appealing** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈpiːlɪŋ/
**Definition:** Attractive or interesting.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 有吸引力的

**Example 1:**
The travel agency made the holiday package sound very appealing to young professionals.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
旅行社將這個度假套餐描述得對年輕專業人士非常有吸引力。

**Example 2:**
The government wants to make public transport more appealing to reduce car usage.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
政府希望令公共交通工具更具吸引力，以減少汽車使用量。

## 4. **Backlash** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbæklæʃ/
**Definition:** A strong negative reaction by a large number of people.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 強烈反對；反彈

**Example 1:**
The company faced a massive public backlash after increasing their subscription fees without notice.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
該公司在未經通知的情況下增加訂閱費用，面臨巨大的公眾反彈。

**Example 2:**
The politician decided to change his stance to avoid a backlash from his voters.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
該政客決定改變立場，以避免選民的強烈反對。

## 5. **Crackdown** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈkrækdaʊn/
**Definition:** Severe measures to restrict or discourage undesirable or illegal people or behaviour.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 嚴厲制裁；掃蕩

**Example 1:**
Police have announced a new crackdown on illegal parking in the city centre.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
警方宣布將對市中心的違例泊車採取新的掃蕩行動。

**Example 2:**
The international crackdown on smuggling has resulted in many arrests this week.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
本週針對走私的國際掃蕩行動導致多人被捕。

## 6. **Implications** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌɪmplɪˈkeɪʃnz/
**Definition:** The possible effects or consequences of an action or decision.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 影響；含意

**Example 1:**
The new law could have serious implications for small business owners.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
這項新法律可能會對小企業主產生嚴重的影響。

**Example 2:**
Scientists are still studying the environmental implications of the global temperature rise.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
科學家仍在研究全球氣溫上升對環境的影響。

## 7. **Seizure** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsiːʒə(r)/
**Definition:** The act of taking possession of something by legal authority.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 查封；沒收

**Example 1:**
The customs officer ordered the seizure of all undeclared goods.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
海關官員下令沒收所有未申報的貨物。

**Example 2:**
The police carried out the seizure of assets linked to organized crime.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
警方對與有組織犯罪有關的資產進行了查封。
