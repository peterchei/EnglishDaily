# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-17

Hi there, let's learn some new words!

## 1. **Jostling** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈdʒɒs.lɪŋ/
**Definition:** Pushing, elbowing, or bumping against someone roughly, typically in a crowd. Figuratively, struggling or competing with others to obtain something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 推撞; 爭奪

**Example 1:**
The politicians were jostling for position during the leadership debate.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
喺領導辯論入面，啲政客爭緊有利嘅位置。

**Example 2:**
There was a lot of jostling as people tried to get on the train.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
好多人推撞，因為佢哋想上火車。

## 2. **Ungovernable** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ʌnˈɡʌv.ən.ə.bəl/
**Definition:** Impossible to control, manage, or govern.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 難以管治嘅

**Example 1:**
Some people believe the country has become ungovernable due to widespread dissatisfaction.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
有啲人相信因為普遍嘅不滿，呢個國家已經變得難以管治。

**Example 2:**
The prison became ungovernable after the riot.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
監獄喺暴動之後變得難以管治。

## 3. **Flop** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /flɒp/
**Definition:** A complete failure; something that is not successful.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 失敗; 慘敗

**Example 1:**
The movie was a complete flop at the box office.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢套戲喺票房方面完全失敗。

**Example 2:**
Their new product launch was a flop, with very few sales.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋嘅新產品推出係一個慘敗，得好少銷量。

## 4. **Assess** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈses/
**Definition:** To evaluate or estimate the nature, ability, or quality of something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 評估; 評價

**Example 1:**
The government will assess the damage caused by the flood.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府會評估洪水造成嘅破壞。

**Example 2:**
Teachers assess students' progress throughout the year.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
老師成年都會評估學生嘅進度。

## 5. **Tactic** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtæk.tɪk/
**Definition:** A plan or procedure used to achieve a particular aim.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 策略; 手法

**Example 1:**
The company used aggressive marketing tactics to increase sales.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
間公司用咗進取嘅營銷策略嚟增加銷量。

**Example 2:**
The police are using new tactics to combat crime.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
警察用緊新嘅策略嚟打擊罪案。

## 6. **Deliberates** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /dɪˈlɪb.ə.reɪts/
**Definition:** To consider carefully and thoroughly; to discuss something formally.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 仔細考慮; 商議

**Example 1:**
The jury deliberates the evidence before reaching a verdict.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
陪審團喺作出裁決之前仔細考慮證據。

**Example 2:**
The committee deliberates on the proposed changes to the policy.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
委員會商議緊對政策嘅擬議修改。

## 7. **Passers-by** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌpɑː.zəzˈbaɪ/
**Definition:** People who are walking or travelling past a particular place.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 行人; 路人

**Example 1:**
Passers-by rushed to help the injured after the accident.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
意外發生後，路人衝上前去幫助傷者。

**Example 2:**
The street artist drew a crowd of interested passers-by.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
街頭藝術家吸引咗一班有興趣嘅路人。
