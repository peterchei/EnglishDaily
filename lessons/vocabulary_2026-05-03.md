# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-03

Hi there! Today, we'll learn some useful words and phrases from the news.

## 1. **Nous** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /nuːs/
**Definition:** Practical intelligence; common sense.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 判斷力; 常識

**Example 1:**
He demonstrated considerable political nous during the negotiations.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
喺談判期間，佢展示咗相當嘅政治判斷力。

**Example 2:**
Her business nous helped the company achieve record profits.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢嘅商業常識幫助公司獲得咗破紀錄嘅利潤。

## 2. **Foreseeable** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /fɔːˈsiːəbl/
**Definition:** Able to be foreseen or predicted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可預見嘅

**Example 1:**
It is not foreseeable that interest rates will rise significantly this year.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今年利率大幅上升係唔可以預見嘅。

**Example 2:**
In the foreseeable future, the company plans to expand its operations overseas.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
喺可預見嘅將來，公司計劃擴大海外業務。

## 3. **Sanctions** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsæŋkʃənz/
**Definition:** Penalties or other means of enforcement used to provide incentives for obedience with the law, or with rules and regulations.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 制裁

**Example 1:**
The government imposed economic sanctions on the country.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府對該國實施咗經濟制裁。

**Example 2:**
The company faces sanctions for violating environmental regulations.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
該公司因違反環境法規而面臨制裁。

## 4. **Pioneers** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌpaɪəˈnɪəz/
**Definition:** Develop or be the first to use or apply (a new method, area of knowledge, or activity).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 開創; 率先使用

**Example 1:**
The company pioneers new technologies in renewable energy.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
該公司喺可再生能源領域開創咗新技術。

**Example 2:**
She pioneered a new approach to teaching mathematics.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢率先使用一種新嘅數學教學方法。

## 5. **Scalability** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌskeɪləˈbɪləti/
**Definition:** The ability of a system, network, or architecture to handle a growing amount of work in a capable manner or its ability to be enlarged to accommodate that growth.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可擴展性

**Example 1:**
The scalability of the software is crucial for future growth.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
該軟件嘅可擴展性對於未來嘅增長至關重要。

**Example 2:**
We need to ensure the system has good scalability to meet increasing demand.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋需要確保系統具有良好嘅可擴展性，以滿足日益增長嘅需求。

## 6. **Deteriorating** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /dɪˈtɪəriəreɪtɪŋ/
**Definition:** Becoming progressively worse.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 惡化

**Example 1:**
The patient's health is rapidly deteriorating.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
病人嘅健康狀況迅速惡化。

**Example 2:**
The relationship between the two countries is deteriorating.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
兩國之間嘅關係正在惡化。
