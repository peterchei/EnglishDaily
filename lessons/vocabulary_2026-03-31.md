# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-31

Hi there,

## 1. **Cheeky** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtʃiːki/
**Definition:** Impudent or irreverent, typically in a charming or humorous way.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 厚面皮, 曳

**Example 1:**
He gave me a cheeky grin and winked.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢對我厚面皮咁笑咗一下，仲眨咗下眼。

**Example 2:**
Don't be so cheeky to your grandmother!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好對你嫲嫲咁曳！

## 2. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed; devastated.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望, 傷心到極

**Example 1:**
I was absolutely gutted when I failed the exam.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我考試唔合格，真係好失望。

**Example 2:**
She was gutted to hear about the cancellation of the concert.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢聽到演唱會取消咗，覺得好傷心。

## 3. **Queue** (Noun/Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people or vehicles waiting for something. To stand in a line.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊, 排隊

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the bakery this morning.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今朝麵包舖外面排咗一條長龍。

**Example 2:**
We had to queue for an hour to get in.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋要排隊排成個鐘先入到去。

## 4. **Sorted** (Adjective/Verb - past participle)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɔːtɪd/
**Definition:** Organized, dealt with, or resolved successfully.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 搞掂, 解決咗

**Example 1:**
Great! That's all sorted then.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
好嘢！咁就搞掂晒啦。

**Example 2:**
I've sorted out the problem with the computer.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我已經解決咗電腦嘅問題。

## 5. **Fancy** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfænsi/
**Definition:** To want something or want to do something. Also, to be attracted to someone.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 想, 鍾意

**Example 1:**
Do you fancy going to the cinema tonight?
**Cantonese Example 1:**
你今晚想唔想去睇戲呀？

**Example 2:**
I think he fancies her.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我覺得佢鍾意咗佢。

## 6. **Knackered** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnækəd/
**Definition:** Extremely tired; exhausted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好攰, 殘晒

**Example 1:**
I'm absolutely knackered after that long walk.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
行咗咁耐，我真係殘晒。

**Example 2:**
She looked completely knackered after the marathon.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
跑完馬拉松之後，佢睇落好攰。
