# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-26

## 1. **Venue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈvenjuː/
**Definition:** The place where something happens, especially an organized event such as a concert, conference, or sports event.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 場地 (cheung4 dei6)

**Example 1:**
The concert will be held at a new venue near the city centre.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
演唱會將會喺市中心附近嘅一個新場地舉行。
(yin2 cheung3 wui2 jeung1 wui2 hai2 si5 chung1 sam1 fu6 gan6 ge3 yat1 go3 san1 cheung4 dei6 hoi2 hang4.)

**Example 2:**
The hotel is a popular venue for weddings.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢間酒店係一個受歡迎嘅結婚場地。
(ni1 gaan1 jau5 dim3 hai6 yat1 go3 sau6 fun1 ying1 ge3 git3 fan1 cheung4 dei6.)

## 2. **Checkpoint** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtʃekpɔɪnt/
**Definition:** A place where vehicles or people are stopped for inspection, especially at a border or in a military zone.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 檢查站 (gim2 cha4 jaam6)

**Example 1:**
The car was stopped at a security checkpoint.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
架車喺一個安全檢查站俾人截停咗。
(gaa3 che1 hai2 yat1 go3 on1 chuen4 gim2 cha4 jaam6 bei2 yan4 jit6 ting4 zo2.)

**Example 2:**
There is a border checkpoint between the two countries.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
喺呢兩個國家之間有一個邊境檢查站。
(hai2 ni1 leung5 go3 gwok3 ga1 ji1 gaan1 yau5 yat1 go3 bin1 ging2 gim2 cha4 jaam6.)

## 3. **Shortages** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈʃɔːrtɪdʒɪz/
**Definition:** A situation in which there is not enough of something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 短缺 (dyun2 kyut3)

**Example 1:**
The drought led to water shortages in the region.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
乾旱導致咗嗰個地區嘅水短缺。
(gon1 hon1 dou6 ji3 zo2 go2 go3 dei6 kui1 ge3 shui2 dyun2 kyut3.)

**Example 2:**
There are shortages of medical supplies in the hospital.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
醫院入面有醫療物資短缺嘅情況。
(yi1 yun2 yap6 min6 yau5 yi1 liu4 mat6 ji1 dyun2 kyut3 ge3 ching4 kwong3.)

## 4. **Speculation** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌspekjuˈleɪʃən/
**Definition:** The forming of a theory or conjecture without firm evidence.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 推測 (teui1 chak1)

**Example 1:**
There is a lot of speculation about who will be the next prime minister.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
有好多關於邊個會係下一個首相嘅推測。
(yau5 ho2 do1 gwaan1 yu1 bin1 go3 wui5 hai6 haa6 yat1 go3 sau2 seung3 ge3 teui1 chak1.)

**Example 2:**
The newspaper article was based on pure speculation.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰篇報紙文章係基於純粹嘅推測。
(go2 pin1 bou3 ji2 man4 jeung1 hai6 gei1 yu1 seun4 seui6 ge3 teui1 chak1.)

## 5. **Vigil** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈvɪdʒɪl/
**Definition:** A period of keeping awake during the time usually spent asleep, especially to keep watch or pray.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 守夜 (sau2 ye6)

**Example 1:**
The family held a candlelight vigil for the missing child.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢家人為咗失蹤嘅細路仔舉行咗一個燭光守夜。
(ni1 gaan1 yan4 wai6 zo2 sat1 zung1 ge3 sai3 lou2 zai2 geui2 hang4 zo2 yat1 go3 chuk1 gwong1 sau2 ye6.)

**Example 2:**
They maintained a vigil at the hospital bedside.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋喺醫院嘅床邊守夜。
(keui5 dei6 hai2 yi1 yun2 ge3 chong4 bin1 sau2 ye6.)

## 6. **Envoy** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈenvɔɪ/
**Definition:** A diplomatic agent.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 使節 (si2 jit3)

**Example 1:**
The president sent an envoy to negotiate with the rebels.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
總統派咗一個使節去同叛軍談判。
(zung2 tung2 paai3 zo2 yat1 go3 si2 jit3 heui3 tung4 bun6 gwan1 taam4 bun6.)

**Example 2:**
He served as a special envoy to the United Nations.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢擔任聯合國嘅特別使節。
(keui5 daam1 yam6 lyun4 hap6 gwok3 ge3 dak6 bit3 si2 jit3.)

## 7. **Amnesty** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈæmnəsti/
**Definition:** An official pardon for people who have been convicted of political offenses.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 特赦 (dak6 se3)

**Example 1:**
The government granted amnesty to all political prisoners.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府特赦咗所有政治犯。
(jing4 fu2 dak6 se3 zo2 so2 yau5 jing3 chi3 faan2.)

**Example 2:**
An amnesty was declared for illegal immigrants.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
政府宣佈咗非法移民嘅特赦。
(jing4 fu2 syun1 bou3 zo2 fei1 faat3 yi4 man4 ge3 dak6 se3.)
