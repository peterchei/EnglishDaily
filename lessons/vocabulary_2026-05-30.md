# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-30

Hi there! Let's learn some new words today.

## 1. **Residential** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌrɛzɪˈdɛnʃəl/
**Definition:** Designed for or including places where people live.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 住宅嘅；居民嘅

**Example 1:**
The drone hit a residential block in Romania.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
架無人機擊中咗羅馬尼亞嘅一棟住宅大樓。

**Example 2:**
This area is primarily residential, with very few shops.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢個區域主要係住宅區，得好少鋪頭。

## 2. **Determination** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /dɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/
**Definition:** The act of deciding something firmly; the quality of being determined.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 決心; 決定

**Example 1:**
The leaders met to make a final determination on the agreement.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
領導人會面，想就協議作最終決定。

**Example 2:**
Her determination to succeed is admirable.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢成功嘅決心令人欽佩。

## 3. **Portable** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈpɔːrtəbəl/
**Definition:** Able to be easily carried or moved.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可攜帶嘅; 手提嘅

**Example 1:**
She carries a portable charger for her phone.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢帶住個手機嘅手提充電器。

**Example 2:**
A portable radio is useful for camping trips.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
一部手提收音機對露營旅行好有用。

## 4. **Screen** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /skriːn/
**Definition:** To examine people or things to decide if they are suitable for a particular purpose.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 篩選; 檢查

**Example 1:**
The new test will screen patients to see if they need chemotherapy.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
新嘅測試會篩選病人，睇下佢哋需唔需要化療。

**Example 2:**
All luggage is screened at the airport.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
所有行李喺機場都會被檢查。

## 5. **Authenticity** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌɔːθenˈtɪsɪti/
**Definition:** The quality of being genuine or true.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 真實性; 可信性

**Example 1:**
The game prides itself on its military authenticity.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢隻遊戲以佢嘅軍事真實性自豪。

**Example 2:**
Experts questioned the authenticity of the painting.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
專家質疑呢幅畫嘅真實性。

## 6. **Trajectory** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /trəˈdʒɛktəri/
**Definition:** The curved path of an object thrown into space.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 軌跡

**Example 1:**
The drone's trajectory was altered by air defences.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
架無人機嘅軌跡被空防系統改變咗。

**Example 2:**
The missile followed a precise trajectory.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
導彈遵循咗一條精確嘅軌跡。

## 7. **Bedraggled** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /bɪˈdræɡəld/
**Definition:** Wet, dirty, and untidy.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 濕淋淋，骯髒又邋遢

**Example 1:**
The bedraggled man was rescued from the cave.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰個濕淋淋嘅男人從山洞裡面獲救。

**Example 2:**
She arrived home looking bedraggled after walking in the rain.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢喺雨中行完之後返到屋企，睇落濕淋淋。
