# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-18

Hello everyone, let's learn some new words today!

## 1. **Saga** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɑːɡə/
**Definition:** A long and complicated series of events.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 漫長而複雜嘅事件

**Example 1:**
The Mandelson saga has dominated the news for weeks.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
文德森事件已經佔據新聞頭條幾個星期。

**Example 2:**
Their family saga is full of secrets and betrayals.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋屋企嘅漫長故事充滿咗秘密同背叛。

## 2. **Respite** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrɛspaɪt/
**Definition:** A short period of rest or relief from something difficult or unpleasant.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 暫緩，休息

**Example 1:**
The ceasefire offered a brief respite from the fighting.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
停火提供咗一個短暫嘅時間去休息，唔使再打仗。

**Example 2:**
She enjoys gardening as a respite from her stressful job.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢鍾意園藝，用嚟舒緩工作壓力。

## 3. **Swirl** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /swɜːl/
**Definition:** To move rapidly round and round.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 旋轉，盤旋

**Example 1:**
Questions continue to swirl around the former Prime Minister.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
問題繼續圍繞住前首相。

**Example 2:**
The wind made the leaves swirl around in the garden.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
風令花園嘅葉喺度旋轉。

## 4. **Wrongfully** (Adverb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrɒŋfʊli/
**Definition:** Unjustly or unfairly.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 錯誤地，唔公正地

**Example 1:**
He was wrongfully accused of the crime.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢被錯誤噉指控犯罪。

**Example 2:**
She was wrongfully dismissed from her job.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢被唔公正噉解僱。

## 5. **Conviction** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kənˈvɪkʃən/
**Definition:** A formal declaration by the verdict of a jury or the decision of a judge in a court of law that someone is guilty of a criminal offence.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 定罪

**Example 1:**
His conviction was overturned after new evidence emerged.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
喺新證據出現之後，佢嘅定罪被推翻咗。

**Example 2:**
She has a strong conviction that she is innocent.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢堅信自己係無辜嘅。

## 6. **Proportion** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /prəˈpɔːʃən/
**Definition:** A part, share, or number considered in comparative relation to a whole.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 比例

**Example 1:**
A large proportion of young adults are still living at home.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
有相當大比例嘅年輕人仍然同屋企人住。

**Example 2:**
The proportion of women in management has increased.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
女性喺管理層嘅比例有所增加。

## 7. **Palaver** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /pəˈlɑːvə/
**Definition:** Unnecessary trouble and bother.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 麻煩，瑣事

**Example 1:**
The whole affair was a messy palaver.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
成件事真係好麻煩。

**Example 2:**
I don't want to go through all that palaver again.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我唔想再次經歷咁多麻煩事。
