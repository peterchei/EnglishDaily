# Daily English Lesson: March 4th, 2026

Here are today's vocabulary words and phrases to help you expand your English knowledge.

## 1. Serendipity (n.)
**Definition:** The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.
**Cantonese:** 意外的發現；機緣巧合 (yi6 ngoi6 dik1 faat3 jin6; gei1 jyun4 haau2 hap6)

**Example 1:**
Finding a rare book in a small, dusty shop was a moment of pure serendipity.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
在一家塵土飛揚的小店裡找到一本稀有的書，真是純粹的機緣巧合。 (Zoi6 jat1 gaa1 can4 tou2 fei1 joeng4 dik1 siu2 dim3 leoi5 zaau2 dou3 jat1 bun2 hei1 jau5 dik1 syu1, zan1 si6 seon4 seoi3 gei1 jyun4 haau2 hap6.)

**Example 2:**
Their meeting was a happy serendipity that led to a lifelong friendship.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
他們'的相遇是一個快樂的意外發現，最終發展成了一生的友誼。(Taa1 mun4 gei1 soeng1 hai6 si6 jat1 go3 faai3 lok6 dik1 ji3 ngoi6 faat3 jin6, zeoi3 zung1 faat1 zin2 sing4 zo2 jat1 sang1 dik1 jau5 ji6.)

## 2. Ephemeral (adj.)
**Definition:** Lasting for a very short time.
**Cantonese:** 短暫的；瞬間的 (dyun2 zaam6 dik1; seon1 gan1 dik1)

**Example 1:**
The beauty of cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a few weeks.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
櫻花的綻放之美是短暫的，只持續幾週。(Jing1 faa1 dik1 taan3 fong3 zi1 mei5 hai6 dyun2 zaam6 dik1, zi2 ceot4 zi6 gei2 zau1.)

**Example 2:**
Fame in the digital age can be ephemeral, quickly forgotten as new trends emerge.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
在數位時代，名聲可能轉瞬即逝，隨著新趨勢的出現而被迅速遺忘。(Zoi6 sou3 wai6 doi6 doi6, ming4 sing1 ho2 nang4 zyun2 seon2 zap1 si6, seoi4 zoek6 san1 keoi1 si6 dik1 ceot1 jin6 bin3 bei2 seon3 suk1 ji4 mong4.)

## 3. Ubiquitous (adj.)
**Definition:** Present, appearing, or found everywhere.
**Cantonese:** 無處不在的；普遍存在的 (mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi6 dik1; pou5 pin3 cyun4 zoi6 dik1)

**Example 1:**
Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
智慧型手機在現代社會中已變得無處不在。(Zi3 wai6 jing4 sau2 gei1 zoi6 jin6 doi6 se5 wui6 zung1 ji5 bin3 dak1 mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi6.)

**Example 2:**
The company's logo is ubiquitous, appearing on billboards, buses, and merchandise.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
該公司的標誌無處不在，出現在廣告牌、巴士和商品上。(Goi1 gung1 si1 dik1 biu1 zi3 mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi6, ceot1 jin6 zoi6 gwong2 gou3 paai4, baa1 si2 wo4 soeng5 ban2 soeng6.)

## 4. Mellifluous (adj.)
**Definition:** (Of a voice or words) sweet or musical; pleasant to hear.
**Cantonese:** 悅耳的；流暢的 (jyut6 ji5 dik1; lau4 soeng6 dik1)

**Example 1:**
The singer's mellifluous voice captivated the audience.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
這位歌手悅耳的嗓音迷住了聽眾。(Zei1 wai6 ho2 sau2 jyut6 ji5 dik1 song3 jam1 mai4 zyu6 ling5 zung3.)

**Example 2:**
He spoke in a mellifluous tone, putting everyone at ease.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
他用一種流暢的語氣說話，讓大家都感到自在。(Taa1 jung6 jat1 zung2 lau4 soeng6 dik1 jyu5 hei3 syut3 waa6, jong6 daai6 gaa1 dou1 gam2 dou3 zi6 zoi6.)

## 5. Ebullient (adj.)
**Definition:** Cheerful and full of energy.
**Cantonese:** 興高采烈；精神飽滿 (hing1 gou1 coi2 lit6; zing1 san4 baau2 mun5)

**Example 1:**
Her ebullient personality made her the life of every party.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
她興高采烈的個性讓她成為每次派對的焦點。(Taa1 hing1 gou1 coi2 lit6 dik1 go3 sing4 joeng6 taa1 sing44 mei6 ci3 paai3 deoi3 gei1 gaau1 dim3.)

**Example 2:**
The team was ebullient after their surprising victory.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
球隊在意外獲勝後感到精神飽滿。(Cau4 deoi2 zoi6 ji3 ngoi6 wok6 sing3 hau6 gam2 dou3 zing1 san4 baau2 mun5.)

## 6. Panacea (n.)
**Definition:** A solution or remedy for all difficulties or diseases.
**Cantonese:** 萬靈藥；靈丹妙藥 (maan6 ling4 joek3; ling4 daan1 miu6 joek3)

**Example 1:**
There is no single panacea for the world's complex problems.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
世界上複雜的問題沒有單一的萬靈藥。(Sai3 gaai3 soeng6 faan6 zaap6 man6 tai4 mut1 jau5 daan1 jat1 gei1 maan6 ling4 joek3.)

**Example 2:**
Technology is often seen as a panacea for educational challenges, but it's not always the case.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
科技常被視為解決教育挑戰的靈丹妙藥，但事實並非總是如此。(Ke1 zi3 soeng4 bei3 si6 wai4 gaai3 kok3 gaau3 juk6 tiu1 zin2 gei1 ling4 daan1 miu6 joek3, daan6 si6 sat6 bing3 fei1 zung2 si6 jyu4 ci2.)

## 7. Resilience (n.)
**Definition:** The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness.
**Cantonese:** 適應力；恢復力；韌性 (sik1 jing6 lik6; foon1 fuk6 lik6; jan6 sing6)

**Example 1:**
Her resilience in the face of adversity was inspiring.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
她在逆境面前展現出的恢復力令人鼓舞。(Taa1 zoi6 nei6 ging6 min6 cin4 zin2 jin6 gei1 taa1 foon1 fuk6 lik6 ling6 jan4 gu2 mou5.)

**Example 2:**
Building resilience is crucial for navigating life's challenges.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
培養韌性對於應對生活的挑戰至關重要。(Pooi4 joeng5 jan6 sing6 deoi3 jyu1 jing3 deoi6 sang1 fo6 gei1 tiu1 zin2 zi3 gwaan1 zung6 jiu3.)
