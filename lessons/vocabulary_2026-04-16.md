# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-16

## 1. **Extradition** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌekstrəˈdɪʃən/
**Definition:** The surrender of an accused or convicted person by one state or country to another (usually under the provisions of a treaty).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 引渡

**Example 1:**
The former US Marine pilot is fighting his extradition to the United States.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰位前美國海軍陸戰隊飛行員正在對抗佢被引渡返美國。

**Example 2:**
The extradition treaty between the two countries is currently under review.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
兩個國家之間嘅引渡條約目前正在審查中。

## 2. **Scrutiny** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈskruːtɪni/
**Definition:** Critical observation or examination.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 仔細檢查, 審查

**Example 1:**
The government's spending plans are under intense scrutiny.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府嘅開支計劃受到嚴格嘅審查。

**Example 2:**
Her qualifications were subjected to close scrutiny before she was offered the job.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
喺佢得到呢份工作之前，佢嘅資歷受到仔細嘅審查。

## 3. **Ceasefire** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsiːsfaɪər/
**Definition:** A temporary suspension of fighting, typically one during which peace talks take place; a truce.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 停火

**Example 1:**
The fragile ceasefire is holding for now, but tensions remain high.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
目前脆弱嘅停火協議仲有效，但係局勢依然緊張。

**Example 2:**
Both sides have agreed to a ceasefire starting at midnight.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
雙方都同意從午夜開始停火。

## 4. **Rift** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /rɪft/
**Definition:** A serious break or division.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 裂痕，分歧

**Example 1:**
The rift between the two leaders is widening.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
兩個領導人之間嘅分歧正在擴大。

**Example 2:**
A major rift developed within the party over the new policy.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
黨內因為新嘅政策出現咗嚴重嘅分歧。

## 5. **Aspirations** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌæspɪˈreɪʃənz/
**Definition:** A hope or ambition of achieving something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 願望，抱負

**Example 1:**
Many young people have high aspirations for their future careers.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
好多後生仔女對自己嘅事業前途都有好高嘅抱負。

**Example 2:**
The government aims to fulfil the aspirations of its citizens.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
政府嘅目標係實現市民嘅願望。

## 6. **Executives** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪɡˈzekjətɪvz/
**Definition:** People in senior management positions in a business.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 行政人員, 高管

**Example 1:**
The company's executives are meeting to discuss the quarterly results.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
公司嘅行政人員正在開會討論季度業績。

**Example 2:**

## 7. **Hijacking** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈhaɪdʒækɪŋ/
**Definition:** The action of seizing an aircraft, vehicle, or ship while it is in transit. Also, unauthorized control of something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 劫持，盜用

**Example 1:**
The airline improved security after a series of hijacking incidents.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
喺發生一連串劫機事件之後，航空公司改善咗保安措施。

**Example 2:**
Booking.com customers were warned of 'reservation hijacking' after a hack.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
Booking.com嘅顧客被警告喺黑客入侵後要小心「預訂盜用」。
