# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-19

Hi there! Let's learn some new words.

## 1. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased or satisfied.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心, 好滿意

**Example 1:**
She was absolutely chuffed to win the prize.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢贏咗個獎，真係好開心。

**Example 2:**
I'm chuffed to bits that you could make it to the party.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我真係好開心你可以嚟到派對。

## 2. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed or upset.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望, 心都碎埋

**Example 1:**
I was gutted when I failed my driving test.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我考車牌唔合格，真係好失望。

**Example 2:**
He's absolutely gutted that his flight was cancelled.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢班機取消咗，佢真係好失望。

## 3. **Knackered** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnækəd/
**Definition:** Extremely tired or exhausted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好攰, 累到散晒

**Example 1:**
I'm absolutely knackered after that long hike.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
行完咁長嘅山路，我真係累到散晒。

**Example 2:**
She was knackered after working a double shift.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢返完雙更，攰到死。

## 4. **Queue** (Noun / Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people or vehicles waiting for something; to stand in line.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊伍; 排隊

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the cinema.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
戲院外面排咗條好長嘅隊。

**Example 2:**
We had to queue for ages to get tickets.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋排咗好耐隊先買到飛。

## 5. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪliənt/
**Definition:** Extremely clever or talented; very good.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好聰明, 好叻; 好正, 好正斗

**Example 1:**
She's a brilliant student, always getting top marks.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢係個好叻嘅學生，成日都攞最高分。

**Example 2:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢個主意好正呀！

## 6. **Cheeky** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtʃiːki/
**Definition:** Impudent or disrespectful in a playful or amusing way.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 厚面皮, 曳曳地, 串串地

**Example 1:**
He gave me a cheeky grin.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢對我笑咗個曳曳地嘅笑容。

**Example 2:**
Don't be so cheeky to your elders!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好對長輩咁串！
