# Daily Vocabulary - February 10, 2026

1. **Liaise** (Verb)
   - **Pronunciation**: /liˈeɪz/
   - **Meaning**: To work with someone in order to exchange information with them.
   - **Cantonese**: 聯絡、溝通、對接。喺公司環境好常用，例如「同第個部門對接」。
   - **Example**: "I will **liaise** with the marketing team to finalize the project details."
   - **Cantonese Example**: 「我會同市場部**對接**，去定好個項目細節。」

2. **Mitigate** (Verb)
   - **Pronunciation**: /ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/
   - **Meaning**: To make something less severe, serious, or painful.
   - **Cantonese**: 減輕、緩解、緩和。通常用喺解決問題或者減少風險。
   - **Example**: "They took steps to **mitigate** the risks of the new investment."
   - **Cantonese Example**: 「佢哋採取咗措施去**緩解**新投資嘅風險。」

3. **Substantial** (Adjective)
   - **Pronunciation**: /səbˈstænʃl/
   - **Meaning**: Of considerable importance, size, or worth.
   - **Cantonese**: 大量嘅、重大嘅、實質性嘅。比 "a lot" 或者 "big" 更加專業。
   - **Example**: "The company has made **substantial** profits this year."
   - **Cantonese Example**: 「公司今年賺咗**一大筆**利潤。」
