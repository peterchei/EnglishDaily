# Daily Vocabulary - February 11, 2026

1. **Acknowledge** (Verb)
   - **Pronunciation**: /əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/
   - **Meaning**: To accept, admit, or recognize the existence or truth of something.
   - **Cantonese**: 承認、確認、收到。常用於電郵或訊息中表示「收到」或「確認收到」。
   - **Example**: "Please **acknowledge** receipt of this email."
   - **Cantonese Example**: 「請**確認**收到呢封電郵。」

2. **Feasible** (Adjective)
   - **Pronunciation**: /ˈfiːzəbl/
   - **Meaning**: Possible to do easily or conveniently.
   - **Cantonese**: 可行嘅、做得通嘅。喺討論計劃或諗法時好常用。
   - **Example**: "It's not **feasible** to complete the project by tomorrow."
   - **Cantonese Example**: 「聽日之前完成呢個項目係唔**可行**嘅。」

3. **Incentive** (Noun)
   - **Pronunciation**: /ɪnˈsentɪv/
   - **Meaning**: A thing that motivates or encourages someone to do something.
   - **Cantonese**: 誘因、動機、獎勵。通常指公司提供嘅福利或金錢獎勵。
   - **Example**: "The company offers a cash **incentive** for high sales performance."
   - **Cantonese Example**: 「公司會為高銷售表現提供現金**獎勵**。」

4. **Leverage** (Verb)
   - **Pronunciation**: /ˈliːvərɪdʒ/
   - **Meaning**: To use something to maximum advantage.
   - **Cantonese**: 利用、槓桿、發揮。常用於利用現有資源去達到更好效果。
   - **Example**: "We should **leverage** our existing network to find new clients."
   - **Cantonese Example**: 「我哋應該**利用**現有嘅網絡去搵新客戶。」
