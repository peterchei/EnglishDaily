# Daily English Lesson - 2026-06-01

Hello everyone! Here are some useful words from today's news.

## 1. **Fortress** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfɔː.trəs/
**Definition:** A building or place that has been made stronger and protected against attack, so that it can be defended from the enemy.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 堡壘 (bou2 leoi5)

**Example 1:**
The ancient fortress overlooked the city, a reminder of its long history.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰個古老嘅堡壘可以俯瞰成個城市，提醒人哋佢悠長嘅歷史。

**Example 2:**
They built a fortress around their headquarters to protect it from attack.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋喺總部周圍起咗個堡壘嚟保護佢免受攻擊。

## 2. **Runoff** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrʌn.ɒf/
**Definition:** A second election held because no candidate in the first election won enough votes.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 決選 (kyut3 syun2)

**Example 1:**
The two leading candidates will face each other in a runoff election next month.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰兩位領先嘅候選人將會喺下個月嘅決選入面碰面。

**Example 2:**
A runoff was necessary to determine the final winner of the mayoral race.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
需要進行決選嚟決定市長選舉嘅最終勝利者。

## 3. **Projections** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /prəˈdʒek.ʃənz/
**Definition:** Estimates or forecasts of future situations or trends based on current data.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 預測 (jyu6 chak1)

**Example 1:**
Government projections show a significant increase in renewable energy use over the next decade.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府嘅預測顯示，喺未來十年，可再生能源嘅使用量將會顯著增加。

**Example 2:**
The company's sales projections for next year are very optimistic.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
間公司對下年嘅銷售預測非常樂觀。

## 4. **Controversial** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌkɒn.trəˈvɜː.ʃəl/
**Definition:** Causing disagreement or discussion.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 有爭議嘅 (jau5 zang1 ji5 ge3)

**Example 1:**
The new policy has been controversial from the start.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢項新政策從一開始就具有爭議性。

**Example 2:**
He is a controversial figure in British politics.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢係英國政壇上一個備受爭議嘅人物。

## 5. **Strategic** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /strəˈtiː.dʒɪk/
**Definition:** Relating to the careful planning of something, especially a military operation, in order to achieve a particular goal.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 戰略性嘅 (zin3 loek6 sing3 ge3)

**Example 1:**
The location of the airport is strategic for international trade.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
機場嘅位置對國際貿易嚟講係具有戰略性嘅。

**Example 2:**
The company made a strategic decision to expand into new markets.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
間公司做出咗一個戰略性嘅決定，就係擴展到新嘅市場。

## 6. **Clashes** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /klæʃɪz/
**Definition:** Violent confrontations or conflicts.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 衝突 (chung1 dat6)

**Example 1:**
There were violent clashes between protesters and police in the city centre.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
喺市中心，示威者同警察之間發生咗暴力衝突。

**Example 2:**
The clashes erupted after the announcement of the election results.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
選舉結果公佈後，爆發咗衝突。

## 7. **Ground Offensive** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌɡraʊnd əˈfen.sɪv/
**Definition:** A military attack carried out by soldiers on land.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 地面攻勢 (dei6 min6 gung1 sai3)

**Example 1:**
The army launched a ground offensive to retake the territory.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
軍隊發動咗地面攻勢嚟奪回領土。

**Example 2:**
The ground offensive resulted in heavy casualties on both sides.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
地面攻勢導致雙方都傷亡慘重。
