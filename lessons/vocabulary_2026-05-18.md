# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-18

Hello everyone.

## 1. **Concessions** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kənˈseʃənz/
**Definition:** Things that are allowed or given up, often in order to end a disagreement.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 讓步 (joeng6 bou6)

**Example 1:**
The government made several concessions to the protesters in an attempt to calm the situation.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府向示威者作出咗幾項讓步，試圖平息局勢。 (zheng3 fu2 hoeng3 si3 wai6 je2 jok3 chut1 zo2 gei2 hong6 joeng6 bou6, si3 tou4 ping4 sik1 guk6 sai3.)

**Example 2:**
Neither side seems willing to make any concessions in the ongoing trade negotiations.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
喺目前嘅貿易談判入面，雙方似乎都唔願意作出任何讓步。(hai2 muk6 chin4 ge3 mau6 yik6 taam4 pun3 yap6 min6, seung1 fong1 chi5 fu4 dou1 m4 jyun4 yi3 jok3 chut1 yam6 ho4 joeng6 bou6.)

## 2. **Stalls** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /stɔːlz/
**Definition:** To stop making progress or developing.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 停滯不前 (ting4 zai6 bat1 chin4)

**Example 1:**
The peace process has stalled due to disagreements over key issues.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
由於喺關鍵問題上存在分歧，和平進程已經停滯不前。(yau4 yu1 hai2 gwaan1 gin6 man6 tai4 seung6 chyun4 zoi6 fan1 kei4, wo4 ping4 jun3 ching4 yi5 ging1 ting4 zai6 bat1 chin4.)

**Example 2:**
The car engine stalls if you don't give it enough gas.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
如果你唔俾足夠嘅油，架車嘅引擎就會熄火。(yu4 gwo2 nei5 m4 bei2 juk1 gau3 ge3 yau4, ga3 che1 ge3 yan5 ching5 jau5 wui5 sik1 fo2.)

## 3. **Surge** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /sɜːdʒ/
**Definition:** A sudden large increase.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 激增 (gik1 zang1)

**Example 1:**
There has been a surge in political executions since the start of the war.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
自從戰爭開始以來，政治處決嘅數量激增。(ji6 chung4 jin1 jaang1 hoi1 chi2 yi5 loi4, jing3 chi6 chu3 kyut3 ge3 so3 leung6 gik1 zang1.)

**Example 2:**
The river surged after several days of heavy rain.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
連日大雨之後，條河嘅水位暴漲。(lin4 yat6 daai6 yu5 ji1 hau6, tiu4 ho4 ge3 shui2 wai2 bou6 jeung2.)

## 4. **Verified** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈverɪfaɪd/
**Definition:** To prove that something is true or correct.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 核實 (hat6 sat6)

**Example 1:**
The UN says it has verified the execution of at least 32 political prisoners.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
聯合國話佢哋已經核實咗至少32名政治犯嘅處決。(lyun4 hap6 gwok3 wa2 kui2 dei6 yi5 ging1 hat6 sat6 zo2 chiu2 siu2 saam1 sap6 ji6 ming4 jing3 chi6 faan2 ge3 chu3 kyut3.)

**Example 2:**
Please verify your email address by clicking on the link we sent you.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
請你點擊我哋發俾你嘅連結嚟驗證你嘅電郵地址。(ching2 nei5 dim2 gik1 ngo5 dei6 faat3 bei2 nei5 ge3 lin4 git3 lei4 yim6 jing3 nei5 ge3 din6 yau4 dei6 chi2.)

## 5. **Collide** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kəˈlaɪd/
**Definition:** To hit something violently.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 相撞 (seung1 chong6)

**Example 1:**
Two fighter jets collided mid-air during an air show.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
兩架戰鬥機喺一個航空展嘅半空中相撞。(leung5 ga3 jin3 dau3 gei1 hai2 yat1 go3 hong4 hung1 zin2 ge3 bun3 hung1 chung1 seung1 chong6.)

**Example 2:**
The two cars collided at the intersection.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰兩架車喺十字路口相撞。(go2 leung5 ga3 che1 hai2 sap6 zi6 lou6 hau2 seung1 chong6.)

## 6. **Deliberates** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /dɪˈlɪbəreɪts/
**Definition:** To consider or discuss something carefully.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 審議 (sam2 ngi5)

**Example 1:**
The jury is currently deliberating the verdict.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
陪審團目前正喺度審議判決。(pui4 sam2 tyun4 muk6 chin4 jing3 hai2 dou6 sam2 ngi5 pun3 kyut3.)

**Example 2:**
The committee will deliberate on the proposed changes to the policy.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
委員會將會審議政策嘅擬議變更。(wai2 yun4 wui2 jeung1 wui2 sam2 ngi5 jing3 chak1 ge3 ni5 yi5 bin3 gang1.)

## 7. **Eject** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪˈdʒekt/
**Definition:** To force something or someone to leave a place or position.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 彈射 (daan6 se6)

**Example 1:**
The pilots were able to eject safely before the plane crashed.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
喺架飛機撞毀之前，啲機師能夠安全彈射出嚟。(hai2 ga3 fei1 gei1 chong6 wai2 ji1 chin4, di1 gei1 si1 nang4 gau3 on1 chyun4 daan6 se6 chut1 lei4.)

**Example 2:**
The security guard ejected the disruptive fan from the stadium.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
個保安將個搞事嘅球迷趕出咗體育場。(go3 bou2 on1 jeung1 go3 gaau2 si6 ge3 kau4 mei4 gon2 chut1 zo2 tai2 yuk6 cheung4.)
