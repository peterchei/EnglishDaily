# English Daily Lesson - 2026-02-25

This lesson focuses on useful vocabulary that can add depth and nuance to your English communication.

## Vocabulary

### 1. Ephemeral (adj.)
*   **Cantonese:** 短暫的 (dyun2 zoi3 dik1), 剎那的 (caat3 naa5 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Lasting for a very short time.
*   **Example 1:** The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a week or two.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 櫻花的美麗是短暫的，只能持續一兩週。 (jing1 faa1 ge3 mei5 ling6 hai6 dyun2 zoi3 dik1, zi2 nang4 ci4 lau4 jat1 loeng5 zau1)
*   **Example 2:** Social media trends are often ephemeral, quickly replaced by new ones.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 社交媒體的趨勢常常是短暫的，很快就會被新的取代。 (se3 gaau1 mei4 tai2 ge3 fan1 sau3 soeng4 soeng4 hai6 dyun2 zoi3 dik1, han2 faai3 zau6 wui5 bei3 san1 ge3 ceoi2 tai3)

### 2. Ubiquitous (adj.)
*   **Cantonese:** 無處不在的 (mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi3 dik1), 普遍存在的 (pou2 pin3 cyun4 zoi3 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Present, appearing, or found everywhere.
*   **Example 1:** Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 在現代社會，智能手機已經變得無處不在。 (zai6 jin6 doi6 se5 gaai3, zi3 nang4 sau2 gei1 ji5 ging1 bin3 dak1 mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi3)
*   **Example 2:** Coffee shops are ubiquitous in most cities, offering a place to relax or work.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 在大多數城市，咖啡店隨處可見，提供了一個放鬆或工作的場所。 (zai6 daai6 do1 sou3 soeng2 sing4 si5, gaa3 fe1 dim1 ceoi4 cyu3 ho2 gin3, tai4 gung1 jat1 go3 go3 fong3 sung1 waak6 gung1 zo6 ge3 coeng4 so2)

### 3. Serendipity (n.)
*   **Cantonese:** 意外發現 (ji3 noi6 faat3 jin6), 巧事 (haau2 si6)
*   **Definition:** The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.
*   **Example 1:** Finding that rare book in a small, dusty shop was a moment of pure serendipity.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 在一家塵土飛揚的小店裡找到那本稀有的書，簡直是意外發現。 (zai6 jat1 gaa1 can4 tou2 fei1 joeng4 ge3 siu2 dim3 leoi5 zaau2 dou3 naa5 bun2 hei1 jau5 ge3 syu1, gaan2 zit6 hai6 ji3 noi6 faat3 jin6)
*   **Example 2:** Their meeting was a stroke of serendipity; neither had planned to be there.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 他們的相遇純屬巧事；誰都沒有計劃要在那裡。 (taa1 dei6 ge3 soeng1 jyu6 seon4 suk6 haau2 si6; seoi4 dou1 mut6 jau5 gei3 waak6 jiu3 zoi6 naa5 leoi5)

### 4. Mellifluous (adj.)
*   **Cantonese:** 悅耳的 (jyut6 ji5 dik1), 甜美的 (tin4 mei5 dik1)
*   **Definition:** (of a voice or music) sweet or musical; pleasant to hear.
*   **Example 1:** The singer's mellifluous voice captivated the audience.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 這位歌手甜美的嗓音迷住了觀眾。 (ze5 wai3 go3 sau2 tim4 mei5 ge3 song2 jam1 mai4 zyu6 zo2 gun1 zong3)
*   **Example 2:** The sound of a gentle stream can be mellifluous and relaxing.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 潺潺溪流的聲音可以讓人感到悅耳和放鬆。 (can4 can4 hei1 lau4 ge3 sing1 jam1 ho2 ji5 joeng6 jan4 gam2 dou3 jyut6 ji5 wo3 fong3 sung1)

### 5. Conundrum (n.)
*   **Cantonese:** 難題 (naan4 tai4), 謎題 (mai4 tai4)
*   **Definition:** A confusing and difficult problem or question.
*   **Example 1:** How to solve the economic crisis is a complex conundrum facing the government.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 如何解決經濟危機是政府面臨的一個複雜難題。 (jyu4 ho2 gaai2 gwat6 ging1 zai3 ngai4 gei1 hai6 zing3 fu2 min6 lam2 ge3 jat1 go3 fuk1 zaap6 naan4 tai4)
*   **Example 2:** The detective faced a conundrum: the suspect had an alibi but the evidence pointed to him.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 偵探面臨一個謎題：嫌疑人有不在場證明，但證據卻指向他。 (zan1 taam3 min6 lam2 jat1 go3 mai4 tai4: jin4 ji4 jan4 jau5 bat1 zoi3 cong4 zing3 ming4, daan6 zing3 geoi3 koek3 zi2 hoeng3 taa1)

### 6. Resilience (n.)
*   **Cantonese:** 韌性 (ren6 sing3), 恢復力 (fui1 fuk6 lik6)
*   **Definition:** The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness.
*   **Example 1:** Her resilience in the face of adversity was inspiring.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 她面對逆境時展現出的韌性令人鼓舞。 (taa1 min6 deoi3 nei6 ging3 si4 zan2 jin6 ceoi1 ge3 ren6 sing3 ling6 jan4 gu2 mou5)
*   **Example 2:** Building resilience takes time and practice, but it is a valuable life skill.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 培養韌性需要時間和練習，但這是一項寶貴的人生技能。 (pou2 joeng5 ren6 sing3 seoi1 jiu3 si4 gaan1 wo3 lin6 zaap6, daan6 ze5 hai6 jat1 hoeng3 bou2 gwai3 ge3 jan4 sang1 gei1 nang4)
