# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-30

Hi there! Today we'll learn some useful words.

## 1. **Queue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people waiting for something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊列 (deui6 lit6)

**Example 1:**
There's a long queue outside the bakery this morning.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今朝麵包舖出面排咗條好長嘅隊。
(gam1 ziu1 min6 baau1 pou3 ceot1 min6 paai4 zo2 tiu4 hou2 coeng4 ge3 deui2.)

**Example 2:**
Please join the queue for the bus.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
請你排隊等巴士。
(cing2 nei5 paai4 deui2 dang2 baa1 si2.)

## 2. **Cheers** (Interjection)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃɪəz/
**Definition:** A toast, a thank you, or goodbye.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 多謝 (do1 ze6), 乾杯 (gon1 bui1), 再見 (zoi3 gin3)

**Example 1:**
Cheers! To a happy and healthy new year.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
Cheers! 祝你新年快樂，身體健康。
(Cheers! zuk1 nei5 san1 nin4 faai3 lok6, san1 tai2 gin6 hong1.)

**Example 2:**
Thanks for your help, cheers!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
多謝你幫忙，唔該晒！
(do1 ze6 nei5 bong1 mong4, m4 goi1 saai3!)

## 3. **Rubbish** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrʌbɪʃ/
**Definition:** Waste material; trash. Also, something of very poor quality.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 垃圾 (laap6 saap3), 廢嘢 (fai3 je5)

**Example 1:**
Please put the rubbish in the bin.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
請將垃圾放入垃圾桶。
(cing2 zoeng1 laap6 saap3 fong3 jap6 laap6 saap3 tung2.)

**Example 2:**
That film was absolute rubbish!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰套戲真係廢到冇人有！
(go2 tou3 hei3 zan1 hai6 fai3 dou3 mou5 jan4 jau5!)

## 4. **Lovely** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈlʌvli/
**Definition:** Beautiful or delightful.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好靚 (hou2 leng3), 好正 (hou2 zeng3)

**Example 1:**
What a lovely day!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今日天氣真係好好啊！
(gam1 jat6 tin1 hei3 zan1 hai6 hou2 hou2 aa3!)

**Example 2:**
You have a lovely garden.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
你嘅花園好靚啊。
(nei5 ge3 faa1 jyun2 hou2 leng3 aa3.)

## 5. **Fortnight** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfɔːtnaɪt/
**Definition:** A period of two weeks.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 兩個禮拜 (loeng5 go3 lai5 baai3)

**Example 1:**
We're going on holiday for a fortnight.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我哋會去放兩個禮拜嘅假。
(ngo5 dei6 wui5 heoi3 fong3 loeng5 go3 lai5 baai3 ge3 gaa3.)

**Example 2:**
The report is due in a fortnight.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
份報告喺兩個禮拜後要交。
(fan6 bou3 gou3 hai2 loeng5 go3 lai5 baai3 hau6 jiu3 gaau1.)

## 6. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪliənt/
**Definition:** Very good or clever.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好勁 (hou2 ging6), 好叻 (hou2 lek1)

**Example 1:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢個主意真係好正！
(ni1 go3 zyu2 ji3 zan1 hai6 hou2 zeng3!)

**Example 2:**
She is a brilliant student.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢係一個好叻嘅學生。
(keoi5 hai6 jat1 go3 hou2 lek1 ge3 hok6 sang1.)

## 7. **Takeaway** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈteɪkəweɪ/
**Definition:** A restaurant or shop that sells cooked food to be eaten elsewhere. The food itself.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 外賣 (ngoi6 maai6)

**Example 1:**
Let's get a takeaway tonight.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我哋今晚叫外賣啦。
(ngo5 dei6 gam1 maan1 giu3 ngoi6 maai6 laa1.)

**Example 2:**
This Chinese takeaway is very popular.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢間中式外賣好受歡迎。
(ni1 gaan1 zung1 sik1 ngoi6 maai6 hou2 sau6 fun1 jing4.)
