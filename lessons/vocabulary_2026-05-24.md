## 1. **Bystander** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbaɪˌstændər/
**Definition:** A person who is present at an event or incident but does not take part.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 旁觀者 (pong1 guan1 ze2)

**Example 1:**
A bystander was injured when a stray bullet hit him.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
一個旁觀者被流彈擊中受傷。
(jat1 go3 pong1 guan1 ze2 bei2 lau4 daan2 gik1 zung1 sau6 soeng1.)

**Example 2:**
Several bystanders witnessed the accident and gave statements to the police.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
幾個旁觀者目擊咗呢場意外，並向警方提供咗口供。
(gei2 go3 pong1 guan1 ze2 muk6 gik1 zo2 ni1 coeng4 ji3 ngoi6, bing6 hoeng3 ging2 fong1 tai4 cung1 zo2 hau2 gung1.)

## 2. **Forecast** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfɔːrkæst/
**Definition:** To predict or estimate (a future event or trend).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 預測 (jyu6 cak1)

**Example 1:**
The weather forecast predicts a heatwave for next week.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
天氣預測話下個星期會有熱浪。
(tin1 hei3 jyu6 cak1 waa6 haa6 go3 sing1 kei4 wui5 jau5 jit6 long6.)

**Example 2:**
Economists are forecasting a recession in the coming months.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
經濟學家預測未來幾個月將會出現經濟衰退。
(ging1 zai3 hok6 gaa1 jyu6 cak1 mei6 loi4 gei2 go3 jyut6 zoeng1 wui5 ceot1 hin6 ging1 zai3 seoi1 teoi3.)

## 3. **Occupied** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɒkjupaɪd/
**Definition:** (of a place) controlled by a military force or group of people.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 佔領嘅 (zim3 ling5 ge3)

**Example 1:**
The town is located in Russian-occupied eastern Ukraine.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
該鎮位於俄羅斯佔領嘅烏克蘭東部。
(goi1 zan3 wai6 jyu1 ngo4 lo4 si1 zim3 ling5 ge3 wu1 hak1 laan1 dung1 bou6.)

**Example 2:**
The building has been occupied by protesters for several days.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢棟建築物已經被示威者佔領咗幾日。
(ni1 dung6 gin3 zuk1 mat6 ji5 ging1 bei2 si6 wai1 ze2 zim3 ling5 zo2 gei2 jat6.)

## 4. **Electrify** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪ/
**Definition:** To excite or thrill (someone).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 使激動 (sai2 gik1 dung6)

**Example 1:**
Zara Larsson electrified the crowd with her performance.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
Zara Larsson 嘅表演令觀眾激動不已。
(Zara Larsson ge3 biu2 jin2 ling6 gun1 zung3 gik1 dung6 bat1 ji2.)

**Example 2:**
The news of the discovery electrified the scientific community.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢個發現嘅消息令科學界振奮。
(ni1 go3 faat3 hin2 ge3 siu1 sik1 ling6 fo1 hok6 gaai3 zan3 fan5.)

## 5. **Blast** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /blɑːst/
**Definition:** An explosion.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 爆炸 (baau3 zaa3)

**Example 1:**
The blast at the coal mine killed at least 82 people.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
煤礦嘅爆炸造成至少 82 人死亡。
(mui4 kwong3 ge3 baau3 zaa3 zo6 sing4 ziu2 siu2 baat3 sap6 ji6 jan4 sei2 mong4.)

**Example 2:**
The bomb blast shook the entire city.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
炸彈爆炸震動咗成個城市。
(zaa3 daan2 baau3 zaa3 zan3 dung6 zo2 seng4 go3 sing4 si5.)

## 6. **Affordable** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈfɔːrdəbəl/
**Definition:** Not too expensive; within one's financial means.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 負擔得起嘅 (fu6 daam1 dak1 hei2 ge3)

**Example 1:**
Researchers hope to find affordable drugs to treat brain conditions.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
研究人員希望搵到負擔得起嘅藥物嚟治療腦部疾病。
(jin4 gau3 yan4 jyun4 hei1 mong6 wan2 dou2 fu6 daam1 dak1 hei2 ge3 joek6 mat6 lai4 zi6 liu4 nou5 bou6 zat6 beng6.)

**Example 2:**
Affordable housing is a major concern in many cities.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
可負擔嘅房屋係好多城市嘅主要問題。
(ho2 fu6 daam1 ge3 fong4 uk1 hai6 hou2 do1 sing4 si5 ge3 zyu2 jiu3 man6 tai4.)

## 7. **Caution** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈkɔːʃən/
**Definition:** Care taken to avoid danger or mistakes.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 小心 (siu2 sam1)

**Example 1:**
Waymo paused robotaxis "out of an abundance of caution."
**Cantonese Example 1:**
Waymo "因為非常小心" 而暫停咗無人駕駛的士服務。
(Waymo "jan1 wai6 fei1 soeng4 siu2 sam1" ji4 zaam6 ting4 zo2 mou4 jan4 gaa3 si2 dik1 si2 fuk1 mou6.)

**Example 2:**
Drivers are urged to proceed with caution in icy conditions.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呼籲司機喺冰雪路面小心駕駛。
(fu1 jyu6 si1 gei1 hai2 bing1 syut3 lou6 min6 siu2 sam1 gaa3 si2.)
