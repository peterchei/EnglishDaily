# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-14

Hi there, let's learn some new English words today!

## 1. **Vibrant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈvaɪ.brənt/
**Definition:** Full of energy and enthusiasm.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 充滿活力嘅

**Example 1:**
The city was once a vibrant hub of culture and commerce.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢個城市曾經係一個充滿活力嘅文化同商業中心。

**Example 2:**
She has a vibrant personality that makes everyone smile.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢嘅性格充滿活力，令每個人都笑。

## 2. **Inquiry** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪnˈkwaɪə.ri/
**Definition:** An official investigation.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 調查

**Example 1:**
The government launched an inquiry into the scandal.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府就呢單醜聞展開咗調查。

**Example 2:**
The police are conducting an inquiry into the theft.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
警察就呢單盜竊案進行緊調查。

## 3. **Advocating** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈæd.və.keɪ.tɪŋ/
**Definition:** Publicly supporting or suggesting an idea, development, or way of doing something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 倡議，主張

**Example 1:**
He is advocating for stricter gun control laws.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢主張更嚴格嘅槍械管制法例。

**Example 2:**
The organization is advocating for the rights of refugees.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
個組織提倡難民嘅權利。

## 4. **Treasury** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtreʒ.ər.i/
**Definition:** The government department responsible for a country's finances.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 財政部

**Example 1:**
The Treasury announced new economic measures today.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
財政部今日宣佈咗新嘅經濟措施。

**Example 2:**
She works as an advisor in the Treasury.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢喺財政部做顧問。

## 5. **Feud** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /fjuːd/
**Definition:** A prolonged and bitter quarrel or disagreement.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 仇怨，不和

**Example 1:**
The family feud has been going on for generations.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢個家族仇怨已經持續咗幾代。

**Example 2:**
Their public feud has damaged both their reputations.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋公開嘅不和損害咗佢哋雙方嘅聲譽。

## 6. **Paramedics** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌpær.əˈmed.ɪk/
**Definition:** People who are trained to give medical treatment to sick or injured people, usually in an emergency, before they are taken to a hospital.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 護理人員，醫療輔助人員

**Example 1:**
The paramedics arrived quickly and provided immediate assistance.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
護理人員迅速趕到並提供咗即時嘅援助。

**Example 2:**
Paramedics often work in very stressful conditions.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
護理人員通常喺壓力好大嘅環境下工作。

## 7. **Undercover** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌʌn.dəˈkʌv.ər/
**Definition:** Done secretly, often involving deceiving people.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 秘密嘅，臥底嘅

**Example 1:**
The police conducted an undercover investigation into the drug ring.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
警方對販毒集團進行咗秘密調查。

**Example 2:**
The journalist went undercover to expose the factory's illegal practices.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
個記者臥底去揭露工廠嘅非法行為。
