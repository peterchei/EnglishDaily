# Daily English Lesson - 2026-06-20

Hi there! Today we are looking at some useful vocabulary appearing in recent news headlines, specifically tailored for your British English learning journey.

## 1. **Major incident** (Noun phrase)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈmeɪ.dʒə ˈɪn.sɪ.dənt/
**Definition:** An event that requires special arrangements by emergency services, such as police, fire, and ambulance, due to the scale of the emergency.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 重大事故（指需要緊急救援部門啟動特別應對方案的事件）

**Example 1:**
The train crash was declared a major incident, meaning extra resources were dispatched to the scene.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
那宗火車相撞事故被宣佈為重大事故，意味著有額外資源被派往現場。

**Example 2:**
Hospitals are preparing for more patients after the fire was classified as a major incident.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
在火災被列為重大事故後，醫院正準備接收更多傷者。

## 2. **Timetable** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtaɪmˌteɪ.bəl/
**Definition:** A plan of when events or tasks should happen.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 時間表

**Example 1:**
The Prime Minister is under pressure to establish a clear timetable for his departure.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
首相正面臨壓力，需要就其離任制定清晰的時間表。

**Example 2:**
Please check the train timetable before you head to the station.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
前往車站前，請先查閱火車時間表。

## 3. **Amber** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈæm.bər/
**Definition:** A warning level in the UK weather system, indicating that the weather could cause significant disruption or danger to life.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 黃色（在英國氣象警報中，指可能造成重大干擾或危險的預警級別）

**Example 1:**
The Met Office has issued an amber heat warning, so take precautions during the hot weather.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
氣象局已發佈黃色高溫警報，因此在炎熱天氣下請採取預防措施。

**Example 2:**
Drivers should be careful as the amber weather warning suggests difficult travel conditions.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
由於黃色天氣警報顯示交通狀況可能困難，司機應格外小心。

## 4. **Frayed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /freɪd/
**Definition:** (Of a relationship or nerves) weakened or worn thin, often becoming tense or strained.
**Cantonese Meaning:** （關係或神經）變得緊張、受損、不和

**Example 1:**
The diplomatic ties between the two leaders have frayed after the recent public argument.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
在最近公開爭吵後，兩位領導人之間的外交關係已經轉趨緊張。

**Example 2:**
Tempers frayed during the long meeting as everyone was exhausted.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
漫長的會議期間，由於大家都精疲力竭，情緒開始變得煩躁。

## 5. **Knockout** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnɒk.aʊt/
**Definition:** A stage in a competition where if you lose a match, you are eliminated from the tournament.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 淘汰賽

**Example 1:**
The fans were thrilled to see their team advance to the knockout round of the tournament.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
球迷們很高興看到他們的球隊晉級到賽事的淘汰賽階段。

**Example 2:**
Pressure is high in the knockout stages because there is no room for error.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
淘汰賽階段的壓力很大，因為容不得半點差錯。

## 6. **Disrespectful** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌdɪs.rɪˈspekt.fəl/
**Definition:** Showing a lack of respect; rude.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 無禮的；不尊重的

**Example 1:**
Ukraine described the decision to strip the honour as disrespectful.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
烏克蘭形容剝奪該項榮譽的決定是不尊重的。

**Example 2:**
It is considered disrespectful to talk while someone else is giving a presentation.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
當別人在演講時交談被視為無禮。

## 7. **Watchdog** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈwɒtʃ.dɒɡ/
**Definition:** An organization or person that monitors the activities of companies or governments to ensure they follow the law.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 監管機構；監督者

**Example 1:**
The head of the data watchdog resigned following an investigation into his conduct.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
在針對其行為進行調查後，數據監管機構的負責人辭職了。

**Example 2:**
The financial watchdog is investigating the bank for potential market manipulation.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
金融監管機構正就潛在的市場操縱行為調查該銀行。
