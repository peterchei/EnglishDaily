# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-11

Hi there, let's learn some new words today.

## 1. **Embark** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪmˈbɑːk/
**Definition:** To go on board a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle; to begin a course of action, especially one that is important or demanding.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 上船; 開始 (重要嘅事情)

**Example 1:**
We are about to embark on a new project.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我哋就嚟要開始一個新嘅項目。

**Example 2:**
Passengers must embark the ship before 2 PM.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
乘客必須喺下晝兩點之前上船。

## 2. **Crunch** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /krʌntʃ/
**Definition:** A critical point or situation, especially one involving a difficult decision.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 關鍵時刻; 緊要關頭

**Example 1:**
This is the crunch point for the peace negotiations.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢個係和平談判嘅關鍵時刻。

**Example 2:**
The company is facing a financial crunch.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
間公司正面臨財政緊絀。

## 3. **Proposal** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /prəˈpəʊzl/
**Definition:** A plan or suggestion, especially a formal or written one, put forward for consideration or discussion by others.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 建議; 提案

**Example 1:**
The committee rejected the initial proposal.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
委員會拒絕咗最初嘅提案。

**Example 2:**
We need to submit a detailed proposal by Friday.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋需要喺星期五之前提交一份詳細嘅建議書。

## 4. **Isolating** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈaɪsəleɪtɪŋ/
**Definition:** Remaining apart from others, often due to illness or a potential health risk, in order to prevent the spread of disease.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隔離

**Example 1:**
They are isolating to prevent further spread of the virus.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢哋隔離緊以防止病毒進一步擴散。

**Example 2:**
After returning from the trip, he started isolating immediately.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
從旅行返嚟後，佢即刻開始隔離。

## 5. **Fallout** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfɔːlaʊt/
**Definition:** The adverse results of a situation or action; consequences.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 後果; 影響

**Example 1:**
The political fallout from the scandal was significant.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
醜聞嘅政治後果非常嚴重。

**Example 2:**
There's likely to be considerable fallout from the economic downturn.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
經濟衰退可能會帶來重大嘅影響。

## 6. **Appalling** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈpɔːlɪŋ/
**Definition:** Causing shock, horror, or disgust.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 令人震驚嘅; 可怕嘅

**Example 1:**
The conditions in the prison were appalling.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
監獄入面嘅環境令人震驚。

**Example 2:**
It was an appalling waste of resources.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢個係對資源嘅可怕浪費。

## 7. **Divisive** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /dɪˈvaɪsɪv/
**Definition:** Tending to cause disagreement or hostility between people.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 引起分歧嘅; 具爭議性嘅

**Example 1:**
The issue of immigration is highly divisive.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
移民問題極具爭議性。

**Example 2:**
His comments proved to be very divisive.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢嘅評論證明非常有爭議性。
