## 1. Resilient

*   **Cantonese Translation:** 堅韌不拔 (gin1 jan6 bat1 bat6), 有彈性 (jau5 daan6 sing3)
*   **Definition:** Able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions.

**Example 1:** The company proved to be resilient during the economic downturn.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 這間公司在經濟不景氣時展現了堅韌不拔的精神。(Zhe1 gaan1 gung1 si1 zoi3 ging1 zai3 bat1 ging2 hei3 si4 zin2 zin6 liu5 gin1 jan6 bat1 bat6 ging1 san4.)

**Example 2:** Children are often more resilient than adults when facing challenges.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 兒童面對挑戰時，通常比成人更有彈性。(Ji4 tung4 min6 doui3 tiu1 zin3 si4, tung1 soeng4 bei2 sing4 jan4 gang3 jau5 daan6 sing3.)

## 2. Ubiquitous

*   **Cantonese Translation:** 無處不在 (mou4 cyu3 zoi3 hai2), 普遍存在的 (pou2 bin3 cyun4 zoi3 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Present, appearing, or found everywhere.

**Example 1:** Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 在現代社會，智能手機已經變得無處不在。(Zoi3 soeng6 doi6 se5 gai3, zi1 nang4 sau2 gei1 ji5 ging1 bin3 dou1 mou4 cyu3 zoi3 hai2.)

**Example 2:** The company's advertising is ubiquitous; you see it on billboards, TV, and online.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 該公司的廣告無處不在；你在廣告牌、電視和網上都能看到。(Go1 gai1 gung1 si1 dik1 gwong2 gou3 mou4 cyu3 zoi3 hai2; nei5 hai2 gwong2 gou3 paai4, din6 si6 ho2 mong5 soeng6 dou1 nang4 dou3 fo2 dou3.)

---

## 3. Ephemeral

*   **Cantonese Translation:** 短暫的 (dyun2 zaan6 dik1), 短暫易逝的 (dyun2 zaan6 ji6 sai6 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Lasting for a very short time.

**Example 1:** The beauty of a sunset is ephemeral, lasting only a few minutes.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 日落的美是短暫的，只持續幾分鐘。(Jat6 lok6 dik1 mei5 hai6 dyun2 zaan6 dik1, zi2 ci4 zuk1 gei2 fan1 zung1.)

**Example 2:** Youth is an ephemeral stage of life.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 青春是人生中一個短暫易逝的階段。(cing1 ceon1 hai6 jan4 sang1 zung1 jat1 go3 dyun2 zaan6 ji6 sai6 dik1 gaai1 tyun6.)

---

## 4. Serendipity

*   **Cantonese Translation:** 意外發現的樂趣 (ji3 ngoi6 faat3 zin6 dik1 lok6 ceoi3), 意外之喜 (ji3 ngoi6 zi1 hei2)
*   **Definition:** The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.

**Example 1:** Meeting her old friend at the airport was a moment of pure serendipity.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 在機場遇到她多年的老朋友，真是純粹的意外發現。(Zoi3 gei1 coeng4 jyu6 dou3 taa1 do1 nin4 dik1 lou5 pang4 jau5, zan1 hai6 seon4 seoi3 dik1 ji3 ngoi6 faat3 zin6.)

**Example 2:** Many scientific discoveries are made through serendipity, not just planned research.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 許多科學發現都是通過意外之喜而成的，而不僅僅是計劃研究。(Heoi2 do1 fo1 hok6 faat3 zin6 dou1 hai6 tung1 gwo3 ji3 ngoi6 zi1 hei2 ji4 sing4 cing4, ja4 bat1 gan2 san2 hai6 gai3 waak6 jin4 gau3.)

---

## 5. Procrastinate

*   **Cantonese Translation:** 拖延 (to1 jin4), 延宕 (jin4 dong3)
*   **Definition:** To delay or postpone action; put off doing something.

**Example 1:** He tends to procrastinate on his assignments, leaving them until the last minute.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 他傾向於拖延他的作業，總是留到最後一刻才做。(Taa1 hing1 soeng6 jyu1 to1 jin4 taa1 dik1 zok3 jing6, zung2 si6 lau4 dou3 zeoi3 hau6 jat1 hak1 coi4 zou6.)

**Example 2:** If you procrastinate too much, you might miss important deadlines.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 如果你拖延太多，你可能會錯過重要的截止日期。(Jyu4 gwo2 nei5 to1 jin4 tai3 do1, nei5 ho2 nang4 wu5 co3 gwo3 zung6 jiu3 dik1 git3 zip3 jat6 kei4.)

---

## 6. Meticulous

*   **Cantonese Translation:** 一絲不苟 (jat1 si1 bat1 gau2), 審慎的 (sam2 san6 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise.

**Example 1:** The artist was meticulous in her brushstrokes, ensuring every detail was perfect.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 這位藝術家對她的筆觸一絲不苟，確保每一個細節都完美無瑕。(Zhe1 mei4 ji6 su6 ga1 deoi3 taa1 dik1 bat2 cuk1 jat1 si1 bat1 gau2, kok3 leon2 mei5 jat1 go3 hai2 zit3 dou1 man4 mei5 mou4 haa4.)

**Example 2:** A meticulous plan is needed for such a complex operation.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 如此複雜的手術需要一個審慎的計劃。(Ju4 ci2 fuk1 zaat6 dik1 sau2 sau6 seoi1 jiu3 jat1 go3 sam2 san6 dik1 gai3 waak6.)

---

## 7. Eloquence

*   **Cantonese Translation:** 雄辯 (hung4 bin6), 口才 (hau2 coi4)
*   **Definition:** Fluent or persuasive speaking or writing.

**Example 1:** Her eloquence captivated the audience during her speech.
**Cantonese Example 1:** 在演講中，她的雄辯迷住了觀眾。(Zoi3 jin2 gong2 zung1, taa1 dik1 hung4 bin6 mai4 zyu6 liu5 gun1 zung3.)

**Example 2:** The politician was known for his powerful eloquence and ability to inspire.
**Cantonese Example 2:** 這位政治家以其強大的口才和鼓舞人心的能力而聞名。(Zhe1 mei4 zing3 zi6 gaa1 ji5 kei4 koeng4 daai6 dik1 hau2 coi4 ho2 gu2 mou5 jan4 san1 dik1 nang4 lik6 ji4 man4 ming4.)
