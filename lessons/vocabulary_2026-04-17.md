# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-17

Hello everyone! Today we're learning some new English words from the news.

## 1. **Vetting** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈvɛtɪŋ/
**Definition:** The process of thoroughly investigating someone's background before offering them a job or position, especially one of trust or responsibility.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 審查; 背景調查

**Example 1:**
The politician underwent rigorous vetting before being appointed to the committee.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰位政治家喺被任命到委員會之前，接受咗嚴格嘅審查。

**Example 2:**
Security vetting is essential for all employees working with sensitive data.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
對於所有處理敏感數據嘅員工嚟講，安全審查都係至關重要嘅。

## 2. **Volatile** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈvɒlətaɪl/
**Definition:** Liable to change rapidly and unpredictably, especially for the worse.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 不穩定嘅; 易變嘅

**Example 1:**
The stock market is particularly volatile this week due to the economic uncertainty.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
由於經濟唔穩定，呢個禮拜股市特別波動。

**Example 2:**
The political situation in the region remains volatile.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢個地區嘅政治局勢依然唔穩定。

## 3. **Pragmatic** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /præɡˈmætɪk/
**Definition:** Dealing with things sensibly and realistically in a way that is based on practical considerations, rather than theoretical ones.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 務實嘅; 講求實際嘅

**Example 1:**
The government is taking a pragmatic approach to solving the problem.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府正採取務實嘅方法去解決問題。

**Example 2:**
She made a pragmatic decision to accept the job offer, even though it wasn't her dream role.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢做咗個務實嘅決定，接受咗呢份工作，即使唔係佢夢想嘅角色。

## 4. **Detain** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /dɪˈteɪn/
**Definition:** To keep someone in official custody, typically for questioning about a crime or in politically sensitive circumstances.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 拘留; 扣留

**Example 1:**
The police detained the suspect for questioning.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
警察拘留咗疑犯進行問話。

**Example 2:**
Immigration officials detained him at the airport.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
移民局官員喺機場扣留咗佢。

## 5. **Exploit** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪkˈsplɔɪt/
**Definition:** To make full use of and derive benefit from (a resource). To take advantage of someone or a situation, especially unfairly or unethically.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 利用; 剝削

**Example 1:**
The company is trying to exploit new technologies to improve efficiency.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
間公司嘗試利用新科技嚟提高效率。

**Example 2:**
Some companies exploit their workers by paying them very low wages.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
一啲公司剝削佢哋嘅工人，俾非常低嘅工資。

## 6. **Spat** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /spæt/
**Definition:** A petty quarrel or argument.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 小爭執; 口角

**Example 1:**
The celebrity couple had a public spat over social media.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢對名人情侶喺社交媒體上公開爭吵。

**Example 2:**
He had a minor spat with his colleague about the report.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢同佢嘅同事因為份報告而發生咗少少口角。

## 7. **Evade** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪˈveɪd/
**Definition:** To escape or avoid (something or someone), especially by guile or trickery.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 逃避; 躲避

**Example 1:**
The robbers evaded capture by fleeing through the tunnel.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
啲賊仔通過隧道逃走，避開咗被捕。

**Example 2:**
He tried to evade paying taxes.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢試圖逃避交稅。
