# Daily English Vocabulary Lesson - 2026-03-01

Hello everyone,

Here are some useful English words and phrases for today's lesson.

## 1. Serendipity
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 意外發現的樂趣；意外收穫
*   **Definition:** The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.
*   **Example 1:** Finding that rare book in a small, dusty shop was a moment of pure serendipity.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 在一家塵土飛揚的小店裡找到那本稀有的書，真是純粹的意外發現的樂趣。
*   **Example 2:** Their meeting was a happy serendipity that led to a lifelong friendship.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 他們的相遇是一次快樂的意外，這段意外帶來了一段終生的友誼。

## 2. Ephemeral
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 短暫的；瞬息的
*   **Definition:** Lasting for a very short time.
*   **Example 1:** The beauty of a sunset is ephemeral, lasting only a few moments.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 日落的美是短暫的，只持續幾分鐘。
*   **Example 2:** Fame can be ephemeral, quickly fading as new stars emerge.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 名聲可以是短暫的，隨著新星的出現而迅速消失。

## 3. Ubiquitous
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 無處不在的；普遍存在的
*   **Definition:** Present, appearing, or found everywhere.
*   **Example 1:** Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 在現代社會，智能手機已經變得無處不在。
*   **Example 2:** The company's logo is ubiquitous, appearing on billboards and products worldwide.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 該公司的標誌無處不在，出現在世界各地的廣告牌和產品上。

## 4. Meticulous
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 仔細的；一絲不苟的
*   **Definition:** Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise.
*   **Example 1:** She was meticulous in her preparation for the exam, reviewing every detail.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 她為考試做了仔細的準備，檢查了每一個細節。
*   **Example 2:** The artist's meticulous attention to detail brought the painting to life.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 這位藝術家對細節一絲不苟的關注，讓畫作栩栩如生。

## 5. Eloquent
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 雄辯的；有說服力的
*   **Definition:** Fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing.
*   **Example 1:** The speaker delivered an eloquent speech that moved the audience.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 發言者發表了一篇動人的演講，感動了聽眾。
*   **Example 2:** His writing style is known for being clear and eloquent.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 他的寫作風格以清晰和有說服力而聞名。

## 6. Resilient
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 堅韌的；有彈性的
*   **Definition:** Able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions.
*   **Example 1:** Children are often more resilient than adults, bouncing back from setbacks quickly.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 孩子們通常比成年人更堅韌，能迅速從挫折中恢復過來。
*   **Example 2:** The company has shown itself to be resilient, overcoming economic challenges.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 這家公司已經證明了它的堅韌，克服了經濟挑戰。

## 7. Conundrum
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 難題；謎語
*   **Definition:** A confusing and difficult problem or question.
*   **Example 1:** The company faced a conundrum: how to increase profits without cutting jobs.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 公司面臨一個難題：如何在不裁員的情況下增加利潤。
*   **Example 2:** It's a philosophical conundrum whether free will truly exists.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 自由意志是否真正存在是一個哲學難題。
