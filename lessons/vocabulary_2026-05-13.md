# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-13

Hello everyone! Today, we're going to learn some new English words and phrases from today's news.

## 1. **Agenda** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈdʒendə/
**Definition:** A list of matters to be discussed at a meeting. More broadly, a plan of things to be done or considered.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 議程 (yi5 ching4) / 計劃 (gai3 waak6)

**Example 1:**
The government's agenda includes tackling climate change and improving healthcare.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府嘅議程包括應對氣候變化同改善醫療保健。
(Zing3 fu2 ge3 yi5 ching4 baau1 kut3 ying1 deui3 hei3 hau6 bin3 faa3 tung4 gai2 sin6 yi1 liu4 bou2 gin6.)

**Example 2:**
What's on the agenda for today's meeting?
**Cantonese Example 2:**
今日嘅會議嘅議程係乜嘢？
(Gam1 jat6 ge3 wui2 ji5 ge3 yi5 ching4 hai6 mat1 je5?)

## 2. **Resignation** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌrezɪɡˈneɪʃən/
**Definition:** An act of resigning; the formal notification that one is leaving a job or office.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 辭職 (ci4 jik1)

**Example 1:**
Her resignation came as a surprise to everyone.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢嘅辭職令到每個人都好驚訝。
(Keoi5 ge3 ci4 jik1 ling6 dou3 mui5 go3 jan4 dou1 hou2 ging1 ngaa5.)

**Example 2:**
He handed in his resignation letter yesterday.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢尋日交咗佢嘅辭職信。
(Keoi5 cam4 jat6 gaau1 zo2 keoi5 ge3 ci4 jik1 seon3.)

## 3. **Assertive** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈsɜːrtɪv/
**Definition:** Having or showing a confident and forceful personality.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 有自信嘅 (jau5 zi6 seon3 ge3) / 果斷嘅 (gwo2 dyun3 ge3)

**Example 1:**
She needs to be more assertive in meetings to get her ideas heard.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢需要喺會議入面更加有自信去俾人聽到佢嘅諗法。
(Keoi5 seoi1 jiu3 hai2 wui2 ji5 jap6 min6 gang3 gaa1 jau5 zi6 seon3 heoi3 bei2 jan4 teng1 dou3 keoi5 ge3 lam2 faat3.)

**Example 2:**
An assertive leadership style can be very effective.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
一個果斷嘅領導風格可以好有效。
(Jat1 go3 gwo2 dyun3 ge3 ling5 dou6 fung1 gaak3 ho2 ji5 hou2 jau5 haau6.)

## 4. **Stifle** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈstaɪfəl/
**Definition:** To restrain or suppress.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 扼殺 (ak1 saat3) / 壓制 (aat3 zai3)

**Example 1:**
Excessive regulation can stifle innovation.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
過度嘅規管會扼殺創新。
(Gwo3 dou6 ge3 kwai1 gun2 wui ak1 saat3 chu1 san1.)

**Example 2:**
Don't stifle your creativity; let your ideas flow.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好壓制你嘅創意；俾你嘅諗法流動。
(M4 hou2 aat3 zai3 nei5 ge3 chong3 yi3; bei2 nei5 ge3 lam2 faat3 lau4 dung6.)

## 5. **Vacuum** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈvækjuːm/
**Definition:** A space entirely devoid of matter. More broadly, a state of emptiness or absence.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 真空 (zan1 hung1) / 空缺 (hung1 kyut3)

**Example 1:**
The resignation of the CEO has created a leadership vacuum.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
首席執行官嘅辭職造成咗領導層嘅空缺。
(Sau2 zik1 hang4 gung1 ge3 ci4 jik1 zou6 sing4 zo2 ling5 dou6 cang4 ge3 hung1 kyut3.)

**Example 2:**
Nature abhors a vacuum.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
大自然厭惡真空。
(Daai6 zi6 jin1 jim3 ok3 zan1 hung1.)

## 6. **Aggressive** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈɡresɪv/
**Definition:** Ready or likely to attack or confront; pursuing one's aims forcefully and sometimes unduly.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 具侵略性嘅 (geoi6 cham1 leok6 sing3 ge3) / 進取嘅 (zeon3 cheoi2 ge3)

**Example 1:**
The company adopted an aggressive marketing strategy.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
間公司採取咗一個進取嘅營銷策略。
(Gaan1 gung1 si1 choi2 cheoi3 zo2 jat1 go3 zeon3 cheoi2 ge3 ying4 siu1 chak3 leok6.)

**Example 2:**
Aggressive behaviour is not acceptable in the workplace.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
具侵略性嘅行為喺工作場所唔係可以接受嘅。
(Geoi6 cham1 leok6 sing3 ge3 hang4 wai4 hai2 gung1 jok3 cheung2 m4 hai6 ho2 ji5 zip3 sau6 ge3.)
