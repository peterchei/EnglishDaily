# Daily English Lesson - 2026-06-18

## 1. **Redevelopment** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌriːdɪˈveləpmənt/
**Definition:** The process of changing or improving an area of land or a group of buildings.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 重建；再開發

**Example 1:**
The city has announced a massive redevelopment project for the old industrial district.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
市政府已經宣佈了一項針對舊工業區的大型重建計劃。

**Example 2:**
The redevelopment of the town centre aims to attract more tourists and local businesses.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
市中心的重建旨在吸引更多遊客和本地企業。

## 2. **Intent** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪnˈtent/
**Definition:** Purpose or intention; the resolve to do something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 意圖；目的；決心

**Example 1:**
The team played with great intent and finally secured a victory.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
球隊踢得非常有決心，最終取得了勝利。

**Example 2:**
He declared his intent to resign from his position at the end of the year.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
他宣佈了他打算在年底辭去職務。

## 3. **Jab** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /dʒæb/
**Definition:** A medical injection, especially a vaccination (common British English term).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 注射；打針（常用於英式英語）

**Example 1:**
Many schools in England have successfully completed the HPV jab programme.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
英國許多學校已經成功完成了人類乳突病毒（HPV）疫苗接種計劃。

**Example 2:**
I need to book an appointment to get my annual flu jab.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我需要預約去打今年的流感疫苗。

## 4. **Solstice** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɒlstɪs/
**Definition:** Either of the two times in the year when the sun reaches its highest or lowest point in the sky at noon.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 至日（夏至或冬至）

**Example 1:**
The ancient stone circle was specifically aligned with the summer solstice.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
古老的巨石陣是特別針對夏至進行定位的。

**Example 2:**
People often gather at historic sites to celebrate the winter solstice.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
人們經常聚集在歷史遺蹟慶祝冬至。

## 5. **Pacifist** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈpæsɪfɪst/
**Definition:** Holding the belief that war and violence are unjustifiable and that all disputes should be settled by peaceful means.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 和平主義的

**Example 1:**
Japan has maintained a pacifist posture in its foreign policy for many decades.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
日本數十年來在外交政策上一直保持著和平主義的姿態。

**Example 2:**
She is known for her pacifist views and often participates in anti-war protests.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
她以和平主義觀點聞名，經常參與反戰抗議活動。

## 6. **Convention** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kənˈvenʃn/
**Definition:** A way in which something is usually done or a formal agreement between countries or groups.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 慣例；公約

**Example 1:**
The nationalist group chose to ignore the international convention regarding the religious site.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
民族主義團體選擇忽視關於該宗教場所的國際公約。

**Example 2:**
It is a social convention to greet people when you enter a room.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
進入房間時向人問候是一種社會慣例。

## 7. **Labour** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈleɪbə/
**Definition:** Work, especially hard physical work, or the group of people who work.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 勞動；勞工

**Example 1:**
The company is facing a labour shortage due to the rise of new technology.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
由於新技術的興起，該公司正面臨勞動力短缺的問題。

**Example 2:**
Manual labour can be very demanding, but it is essential for the construction industry.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
體力勞動可能非常辛苦，但對於建築業來說是不可或缺的。
