## 1. **Pivotal** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈpɪvətl/
**Definition:** Of crucial importance in relation to the development or success of something else.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 至關重要嘅, 關鍵嘅

**Example 1:**

**Cantonese Example 1:**

**Example 2:**

**Cantonese Example 2:**
