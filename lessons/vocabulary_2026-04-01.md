# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-01

Hi there,

## 1. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased; delighted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心；好滿意

**Example 1:**
I was so chuffed to win the competition.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我贏咗個比賽，真係好開心。

**Example 2:**
She was chuffed with her new promotion at work.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢對喺工作上得到晉升感到好滿意。

## 2. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed; devastated.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望；好沮喪

**Example 1:**
I was absolutely gutted when I failed my driving test.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我考車唔合格，真係好失望。

**Example 2:**
He was gutted to hear that his holiday was cancelled.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢聽到佢嘅假期取消咗，覺得好沮喪。

## 3. **Cheeky** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtʃiːki/
**Definition:** Impudent; disrespectful in a playful or amusing way.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 厚面皮；冇大冇細；鬼馬

**Example 1:**
Don't be so cheeky to your elders!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
唔好對長輩咁冇大冇細！

**Example 2:**
He gave me a cheeky grin.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢對我鬼馬咁笑一笑。

## 4. **Sorted** (Adjective/Verb - past participle)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɔːtɪd/
**Definition:** Organized; arranged; dealt with successfully.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 搞掂咗；安排好咗；整理好咗

**Example 1:**
We've got the tickets sorted for the concert.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我哋已經搞掂咗演唱會嘅飛。

**Example 2:**
Everything is sorted for the trip; we're ready to go.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
所有嘢都安排好咗，我哋準備好出發啦。

## 5. **Taking the mick** (Phrase)
**Pronunciation:** /teɪkɪŋ ðə mɪk/
**Definition:** To tease or make fun of someone.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 攞人嚟搞；玩嘢

**Example 1:**
Are you taking the mick out of me?
**Cantonese Example 1:**
你係咪攞我嚟搞？

**Example 2:**
He was just taking the mick, don't take it seriously.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢只係玩嘢啫，唔好咁認真。

## 6. **Dodgy** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈdɒdʒi/
**Definition:** Suspicious; unreliable; potentially dangerous.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可疑嘅；唔可靠嘅；危險嘅

**Example 1:**
That building looks a bit dodgy.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰棟建築物睇起嚟有啲危險。

**Example 2:**
I bought this watch from a dodgy street vendor.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我喺一個可疑嘅街頭小販度買咗呢隻錶。
