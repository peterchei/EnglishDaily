## 1. **Cheeky** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtʃiːki/
**Definition:** Impudent or disrespectful in a playful or amusing way.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 厚面皮, 牙擦擦

**Example 1:**
He gave me a cheeky grin.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢畀咗個厚面皮嘅笑容我。

**Example 2:**
Don't be so cheeky with your elders!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好對長輩咁牙擦擦！

## 2. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望, 頹到爆

**Example 1:**
I was absolutely gutted when I failed the exam.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我考試唔合格，真係頹到爆。

**Example 2:**
She's gutted that she can't come to the party.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢嚟唔到個派對，佢覺得好失望。

## 3. **Knackered** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnækəd/
**Definition:** Very tired; exhausted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好攰, 殘曬

**Example 1:**
I'm absolutely knackered after that long walk.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
行咗咁耐路，我殘曬。

**Example 2:**
He looks completely knackered; he needs a holiday.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢睇落去好攰；佢需要放假。

What a lovely dress!

## 5. **Sorted** (Adjective/Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɔːtɪd/
**Definition:** (adj) Organised and dealt with satisfactorily. (v) To organize or resolve a problem.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 搞掂咗, 整理好

**Example 1:**
"Have you paid the bill yet?" "Yes, it's all sorted."
**Cantonese Example 1:**
「你交咗張單未呀？」 「交咗啦，搞掂咗。」

**Example 2:**
I need to get my life sorted out.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我需要將我嘅生活整理好。

## 6. **Taking the Piss** (Phrase)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈteɪkɪŋ ðə pɪs/
**Definition:** To make fun of someone or something; to tease or ridicule.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 玩嘢, 搞搞震

**Example 1:**
Are you taking the piss out of my new haircut?
**Cantonese Example 1:**
你係咪玩嘢，笑我個新髮型呀？

**Example 2:**
He's always taking the piss, but he doesn't mean any harm.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢成日玩嘢，但佢冇惡意嘅。

## 7. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased; delighted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心, 好滿意

**Example 1:**
I was chuffed to bits when I won the prize.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我贏咗獎，開心到爆。

**Example 2:**
She was chuffed with her exam results.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢對自己嘅考試成績好滿意。
