# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-16

## 1. **Cheeky** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtʃiːki/
**Definition:** Impudent or disrespectful, typically in a playful or amusing way.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 厚面皮, 串串貢

**Example 1:**
He gave a cheeky grin when he was caught stealing a cookie.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢偷食曲奇餅俾人捉到嘅時候，笑得好厚面皮。

**Example 2:**
Don't be so cheeky to your elders!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好對你嘅長輩咁串串貢！

## 2. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed; devastated.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望, 傷心到極

**Example 1:**
I was absolutely gutted when I didn't get the job.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我冇得到嗰份工嘅時候，真係失望到極。

**Example 2:**
She felt gutted after losing the final match.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
輸咗決賽之後，佢覺得好傷心。

## 3. **Queue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people or vehicles waiting for something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊伍, 排隊

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the bakery this morning.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今朝喺麵包舖出面有一條好長嘅隊伍。

**Example 2:**
Please join the queue if you want to buy a ticket.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
如果你想買飛，請你排隊。

## 4. **Dodgy** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈdɒdʒi/
**Definition:** Suspicious, unreliable, or potentially dishonest.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可疑嘅, 唔可靠嘅

**Example 1:**
That website looks a bit dodgy; I wouldn't trust it.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰個網站睇落有啲可疑，我唔會信佢。

**Example 2:**
He gave me a dodgy excuse for being late.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢俾咗個唔可靠嘅藉口解釋佢點解遲到。

## 5. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪljənt/
**Definition:** Extremely clever or talented; very good.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好叻, 好出色, 好棒

**Example 1:**
She's a brilliant musician.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢係一個好出色嘅音樂家。

**Example 2:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰個係一個好棒嘅主意！

## 6. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased or satisfied.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心, 好滿意

**Example 1:**
I was chuffed to bits when I passed my driving test.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我通過咗駕駛考試嘅時候開心到不得了。

**Example 2:**
She was chuffed with the results of the experiment.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢對實驗嘅結果感到好滿意。
