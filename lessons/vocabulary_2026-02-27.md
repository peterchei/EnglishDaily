## 1. Ephemeral
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 短暫的 (dyun² zaam³ dik¹)
*   **Definition:** Lasting for a very short time.

    **Example 1:** The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a week or two.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 櫻花的美是短暫的，只能持續一兩個星期。(jing¹ faa¹ dik¹ mei⁵ si⁶ dyun² zaam³ dik¹, zi² nang⁴ ci⁴ hau⁴ jat¹ leung⁵ go³ sing¹ kei⁴)

    **Example 2:** Social media trends are often ephemeral, quickly replaced by new ones.
    **Cantonese Example 2:** 社交媒體的潮流往往是短暫的，很快就會被新的取代。(se³ gaau¹ mei⁴ tai⁴ dik¹ c¹au⁴ lou⁴ mong⁵ fong² si⁶ dyun² zaam³ dik¹, fan¹ faai³ zau³ wui⁵ bei³ san¹ dik¹ keoi² doi⁶)

## 2. Ubiquitous
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 無處不在的 (mou⁴ cyu³ zoi³ dik¹)

## 3. Mellifluous
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 悅耳動聽的 (jyut⁶ i⁵ dung⁶ ting¹ dik¹)

    **Example 1:** The singer's mellifluous voice captivated the audience.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 這位歌手悅耳動聽的聲音迷住了觀眾。(ze² wai⁶ go¹ sau² jyut⁶ i⁵ dung⁶ ting¹ dik¹ sam¹ ing¹ mai⁴ zyu⁶ zo² goon³ zoeng³)

## 5. Quixotic

## 6. Esoteric
*   **Cantonese Translation:** 艱深難懂的 (gaan¹ sam¹ naan⁴ dung² dik¹) / 只有少數人懂的 (zi² jau⁵ siu² sou³ jan⁴ dung² dik¹)
*   **Definition:** Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with a specialized knowledge or interest.

    **Example 1:** The lecture delved into esoteric philosophical concepts that were difficult to follow.
    **Cantonese Example 1:** 該講座深入探討了晦澀難懂的哲學概念，讓人難以理解。(goi¹ zoeng² zo⁶ san¹ sam¹ taam³ tou² zo⁶ fui³ sap⁶ naan⁴ dung² dik¹ zit³ fo¹ haai² gaan³ oi⁶, rang¹ jan⁴ naan⁴ ji⁵ lei⁵ gaai³)

    **Example 2:** He has an esoteric taste in music, preferring obscure jazz artists.
