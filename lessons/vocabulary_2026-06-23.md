# Daily English Lesson - 2026-06-23

## 1. **Mull** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /mʌl/
**Definition:** To think about a fact, proposal, or request deeply and at length.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 深思熟慮、仔細考慮

**Example 1:**
The board members are still mulling over the proposal for the new office policy.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
董事會成員仍在仔細考慮新辦公室政策的建議。

**Example 2:**
Take some time to mull the decision before you sign the contract.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
在簽署合同前，花點時間好好考慮這個決定。

## 2. **Coronation** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌkɒr.əˈneɪ.ʃən/
**Definition:** The ceremony of crowning a sovereign; in political slang, an uncontested appointment to a leadership position.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 加冕禮（政治上指未經競選直接獲委任）

**Example 1:**
Critics argued that the lack of candidates turned the leadership election into a coronation.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
批評人士認為，缺乏候選人使這次領導人選舉變成了一場「加冕禮」。

**Example 2:**
The party wants a competitive race rather than a coronation for the new leader.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
該黨希望進行一場競爭激烈的選舉，而不是讓新領導人直接被「加冕」。

## 3. **Searing** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɪə.rɪŋ/
**Definition:** Extremely hot or intense.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 灼熱的、極度炎熱的

**Example 1:**
We had to stay indoors to escape the searing heat of the afternoon sun.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
為了避開午後灼熱的陽光，我們不得不待在室內。

**Example 2:**
The searing temperatures are expected to break records across the country this week.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
預計本週全國各地的氣溫將會突破極高紀錄。

## 4. **Onset** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɒn.set/
**Definition:** The beginning of something, especially something unpleasant.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 發作、開始（通常指不好的事）

**Example 1:**
The new medication is designed to delay the onset of symptoms for patients with diabetes.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
這種新藥旨在延緩糖尿病患者症狀的發作。

**Example 2:**
We should prepare for the onset of the storm by securing all loose items in the garden.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我們應該固定好花園裡所有鬆動的物品，為風暴的來臨做好準備。

## 5. **Green light** (Noun/Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡriːn ˌlaɪt/
**Definition:** Permission to proceed with a project or activity.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 批准、開綠燈

**Example 1:**
The government has finally given the green light to the new transport infrastructure project.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府終於批准了新的交通基礎設施項目。

**Example 2:**
Once we get the green light from the manager, we can start the marketing campaign.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
一旦我們獲得經理的批准，就可以開始市場營銷活動。

## 6. **Hydration** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /haɪˈdreɪ.ʃən/
**Definition:** The process of causing something to absorb water; the state of having enough water in the body.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 水分補充、補水

**Example 1:**
Proper hydration is essential when working outside during a heatwave.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
在熱浪期間戶外工作時，適當的補水至關重要。

**Example 2:**
The fitness watch tracks your hydration levels throughout the day to ensure you drink enough water.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
這款健身手錶會全天追蹤你的補水水平，以確保你飲用足夠的水分。
