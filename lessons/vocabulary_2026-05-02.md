# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-02

## 1. **Flouted** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈflaʊtɪd/
**Definition:** Openly disregard (a rule or law); to treat with contemptuous disregard.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 公然藐視; 違反

**Example 1:**
The company flouted environmental regulations by dumping waste into the river.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰間公司公然藐視環境法規，將廢物倒落條河度。

**Example 2:**
Many young people flout the rules regarding social distancing.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
好多後生仔公然藐視社交距離嘅規則。

## 2. **Authorization** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
**Definition:** The act of giving official permission or approval.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 授權; 批准

**Example 1:**
The government needs parliamentary authorization to declare war.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府需要議會授權先可以宣戰。

**Example 2:**
We need your authorization before we can proceed with the project.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋需要你嘅批准先至可以繼續進行呢個項目。

## 3. **Backlash** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbækˌlæʃ/
**Definition:** A strong negative reaction by a large number of people, especially to a political or social development.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 強烈反對; 激烈反應

**Example 1:**
The new policy has caused a public backlash.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢個新政策引起咗公眾嘅強烈反對。

**Example 2:**
The company faced a backlash after announcing the layoffs.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
間公司宣佈裁員之後，面對好大嘅反彈。

## 4. **Attempted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈtɛmptɪd/
**Definition:** Having tried to do something, especially without success.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 企圖; 嘗試過嘅

**Example 1:**
He was charged with attempted robbery.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢俾人控告企圖搶劫。

**Example 2:**
The police are investigating an attempted break-in at the store.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
差佬調查緊一間舖頭嘅企圖爆竊案。

## 5. **Crop Yields** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /krɒp jiːldz/
**Definition:** The amount of a crop that is produced in a certain area.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 農作物產量

**Example 1:**
Poor weather conditions can significantly reduce crop yields.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
惡劣天氣可以大幅降低農作物產量。

**Example 2:**
New farming techniques aim to improve crop yields.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
新嘅耕種技術旨在提高農作物產量。

## 6. **Eligibility** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌɛlɪdʒəˈbɪlɪti/
**Definition:** The state of being qualified to do or receive something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 合資格; 符合條件

**Example 1:**
Check your eligibility for the scholarship online.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
上網檢查吓你係咪符合獎學金嘅資格。

**Example 2:**
The eligibility criteria are clearly stated in the application form.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
申請表清楚列明咗合資格嘅標準。
