# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-06

Hi there, let's learn some new English words today!

## N. **Unfold** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ʌnˈfəʊld/
**Definition:** (of a story or event) develop or be revealed gradually.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 展開, 發展

**Example 1:**
The full details of the rescue operation began to unfold as the press conference continued.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
隨著記者會繼續，救援行動的完整細節開始展開。

**Example 2:**
As the situation unfolds, we will keep you updated with the latest information.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
隨著情況發展，我們會為您提供最新的資訊。

## N. **Solitude** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɒlɪtjuːd/
**Definition:** the state or situation of being alone.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 獨處，孤獨

**Example 1:**
She enjoys the solitude of her mountain cabin.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
她享受山間小屋的獨處時光。

**Example 2:**
The astronaut welcomed the moment of solitude behind the moon.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
太空人歡迎在月球背面獨處的時刻。

## N. **Entitlement** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪnˈtaɪt(ə)lmənt/
**Definition:** the fact of having a right to something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 權利，應得的權利

**Example 1:**
The new law gives parents a legal entitlement to paid leave after a miscarriage.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
新法律賦予父母在流產後享有帶薪假的合法權利。

**Example 2:**
He felt a sense of entitlement, believing he deserved the promotion.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
他感到一種理所當然的感覺，認為他應該得到晉升。

## N. **Backlash** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbæklæʃ/
**Definition:** a strong negative reaction against something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 強烈反對，反彈

**Example 1:**
The company faced a public backlash after the controversial advertisement.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
該公司在有爭議的廣告之後面臨公眾強烈反彈。

**Example 2:**
The festival sponsor withdrew due to the backlash against the headline act.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
由於對頭條表演的反彈，音樂節贊助商撤回了贊助。

## N. **Allege** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈlɛdʒ/
**Definition:** claim or assert that someone has done something illegal or wrong, typically without proof.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 聲稱，指控

**Example 1:**
Hungary alleges a plot to blow up a gas pipeline.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
匈牙利聲稱有人陰謀炸毀一條天然氣管道。

**Example 2:**
The newspaper alleges that the politician accepted bribes.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
該報紙聲稱這位政客接受了賄賂。

## N. **Pontiff** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈpɒntɪf/
**Definition:** the Pope.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 教皇

**Example 1:**
The new Pontiff delivered his first Easter Mass.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
新教皇發表了他的第一次復活節彌撒。

**Example 2:**
The Pontiff called for peace and reconciliation in his speech.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
教皇在他的演講中呼籲和平與和解。

## N. **Frenzy** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfrɛnzi/
**Definition:** a state or period of uncontrolled excitement or wild behaviour.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 狂熱，瘋狂

**Example 1:**
The news sparked a frenzy of buying and selling on the stock market.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
這個消息引發了股票市場上的買賣狂潮。

**Example 2:**
There was a frenzy of excitement as the team approached the final.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
當球隊接近決賽時，出現了一陣興奮的狂潮。
