# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-22

Hello everyone! Let's learn some new words from today's news.

## 1. **Guidance** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡaɪdns/
**Definition:** Advice or information aimed at resolving a problem or difficulty, or at directing someone or something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 指導 (zi2 dou6) / 指引 (zi2 jan5)

**Example 1:**
The government issued new guidance on school safety.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府發布咗關於學校安全嘅新指導。
(zheng3 fu2 faat3 bou3 zo2 gwaan1 jyu1 hok6 haau6 on1 cyun4 ge3 san1 zi2 dou6.)

**Example 2:**
Follow the guidance provided in the instruction manual.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
跟住說明書入面提供嘅指引嚟做。
(gan1 zyu6 syut3 ming4 syu1 jaap6 min6 tai4 gung1 ge3 zi2 jan5 lei4 zou6.)

## 2. **Melanoma** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌmeləˈnəʊmə/
**Definition:** A type of skin cancer that develops from melanocytes (pigment-producing cells).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 黑色素瘤 (hak1 sik1 sou3 lau2)

**Example 1:**
Early detection of melanoma is crucial for successful treatment.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
及早發現黑色素瘤對於成功治療至關重要。
(kap6 zou2 faat3 yin6 hak1 sik1 sou3 lau2 deoi3 jyu1 sing4 gung1 zi6 liu4 zi3 gwaan1 zung6 jiu3.)

**Example 2:**
She was diagnosed with melanoma last year.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢舊年確診咗黑色素瘤。
(keoi5 gau6 nin4 kok3 can2 zo2 hak1 sik1 sou3 lau2.)

## 3. **Implemented** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɪmplɪˌmɛntɪd/
**Definition:** Put (a decision, plan, agreement, etc.) into effect.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 實施 (sat6 si1) / 執行 (zaap6 hang4)

**Example 1:**
The new regulations were implemented last month.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
啲新嘅規例上個月已經實施咗。
(di1 san1 ge3 kwai1 lai6 seung6 go3 jyut6 ji5 ging1 sat6 si1 zo2.)

**Example 2:**
We need to implement a better system for tracking expenses.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋需要實施一個更好嘅系統嚟追蹤開支。
(ngo5 dei6 seoi1 jiu3 sat6 si1 jat1 go3 gang3 hou2 ge3 hai6 tung2 lei4 zeoi1 zung1 hoi1 zi1.)

## 4. **Penalties** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈpɛnəltiz/
**Definition:** A punishment imposed for breaking a law, rule, or contract.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 懲罰 (ching4 fat6) / 罰款 (fat6 fun2)

**Example 1:**
There are severe penalties for driving under the influence.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
酒後駕駛會有好嚴重嘅懲罰。
(zau2 hau6 gaa3 sai2 wui5 jau5 hou2 jim4 zung6 ge3 ching4 fat6.)

**Example 2:**
The company faced financial penalties for violating environmental regulations.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
間公司因為違反環境條例而面臨財務罰款。
(gaan1 gung1 si1 yan1 wai6 fan2 waai4 ging2 teoi4 lai6 ji4 min6 lam4 choi4 mou6 fat6 fun2.)

## 5. **Instigate** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/
**Definition:** Bring about or initiate (an action or event).
**Cantonese Meaning:** 煽動 (sin3 dung6) / 發起 (faat3 hei2)

**Example 1:**
They were accused of instigating violence.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢哋被指控煽動暴力。
(keoi5 dei6 bei6 zi2 hung3 sin3 dung6 bou6 lik6.)

**Example 2:**
The government is trying to instigate reforms in the education system.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
政府嘗試發起教育制度嘅改革。
(zheng3 fu2 cheung4 si3 faat3 hei2 gaau3 juk6 zai3 dou6 ge3 goi2 gaak3.)

## 6. **Indictment** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪnˈdaɪtmənt/
**Definition:** A formal accusation by a grand jury that there is enough evidence to bring criminal charges against a defendant.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 起訴 (hei2 sou3) / 控告 (hung3 gou3)

**Example 1:**
The indictment included charges of fraud and conspiracy.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
起訴書包括詐騙同陰謀嘅指控。
(hei2 sou3 syu1 baau1 kut3 zaa3 pin3 tung4 jam1 mau4 ge3 zi2 hung3.)

**Example 2:**
The politician faced an indictment for corruption.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
個政客面臨貪污嘅起訴。
(go3 jing3 hak3 min6 lam4 taam1 wu1 ge3 hei2 sou3.)

## 7. **Consulate** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈkɒnsjʊlət/
**Definition:** A government office in a foreign city that represents its country's interests and provides assistance to its citizens.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 領事館 (ling5 si6 gun2)

**Example 1:**
The US consulate is located in the city centre.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
美國領事館位於市中心。
(mei5 gwok3 ling5 si6 gun2 wai6 jyu1 si5 zung1 sam1.)

**Example 2:**
She applied for a visa at the British consulate.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢喺英國領事館申請簽證。
(keoi5 hai2 ying1 gwok3 ling5 si6 gun2 san1 ching2 chin1 jing3.)
