# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-26

## 1. **Negotiators** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪtərz/
**Definition:** People who participate in discussions aimed at reaching an agreement.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 談判代表

**Example 1:**
The negotiators from both countries worked tirelessly to find common ground.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
兩國嘅談判代表唔停咁努力搵到共同點。

**Example 2:**
Experienced negotiators are crucial for resolving complex international disputes.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
經驗豐富嘅談判代表對於解決複雜嘅國際紛爭好重要。

## 2. **Aerial** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈeəriəl/
**Definition:** Existing or happening in the air.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 空中嘅

**Example 1:**
The city suffered an aerial bombardment during the war.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
喺戰爭期間，個城市受到空中轟炸。

**Example 2:**
Aerial photography provides a unique perspective on the landscape.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
空中攝影提供咗一個獨特嘅風景視角。

## 3. **Routinely** (Adverb)
**Pronunciation:** /ruːˈtiːnli/
**Definition:** In a regular or habitual manner.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 例行噉; 慣常噉

**Example 1:**
Doctors routinely check blood pressure during appointments.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
醫生喺預約期間會例行噉檢查血壓。

**Example 2:**
The software is routinely updated to improve security.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
個軟件會慣常噉更新嚟提升安全性。

## 4. **Applicants** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈæplɪkənts/
**Definition:** People who formally request something, especially a job.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 申請人

**Example 1:**
The company received hundreds of applicants for the open position.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
間公司收到數百個申請人嚟申請嗰個公開職位。

**Example 2:**
Successful applicants will be contacted for an interview.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
成功嘅申請人會被聯絡嚟面試。

## 5. **Managed** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈmænɪdʒd/
**Definition:** Controlled or directed something; oversaw.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 管理; 經營

**Example 1:**
The land is managed for game shooting, which raises concerns about bird deaths.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰塊地係經營嚟做狩獵遊戲，噉樣引起咗對雀鳥死亡嘅關注。

**Example 2:**
She expertly managed the team through a difficult project.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢熟練噉管理個團隊渡過一個困難嘅項目。

## 6. **Imminent** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɪmɪnənt/
**Definition:** About to happen; impending.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 逼近嘅; 即將發生嘅

**Example 1:**
The storm is imminent, so we should take shelter.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
風暴即將發生，所以我哋應該搵地方避雨。

**Example 2:**
Despite earlier optimism, a US-Iran deal is not imminent.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
儘管早前樂觀，但係美國同伊朗嘅協議唔係即將發生嘅。

## 7. **Slide** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /slaɪd/
**Definition:** To gradually decrease or decline.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 下滑; 下跌

**Example 1:**
Oil prices began to slide on hopes of a peace deal.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
油價因為對和平協議嘅希望而開始下跌。

**Example 2:**
The company's profits slid significantly last quarter.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
間公司上個季度嘅利潤大幅下滑。
