# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-04

Hello everyone!

## 1. **Entrenched** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪnˈtrentʃt/
**Definition:** Firmly established and difficult to change.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 根深蒂固

**Example 1:**
The old traditions are deeply entrenched in the village.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
啲舊傳統喺嗰條村度根深蒂固。

**Example 2:**
It's difficult to change people's entrenched attitudes.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
要改變人哋根深蒂固嘅態度好難。

## 2. **Vulnerabilities** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌvʌlnərəˈbɪlətiz/
**Definition:** Weaknesses or areas that are susceptible to attack or damage.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 弱點

**Example 1:**
The company needs to address its security vulnerabilities.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
間公司需要處理佢哋嘅安全弱點。

**Example 2:**
He was aware of his own vulnerabilities.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢知道自己嘅弱點。

## 3. **Summon** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsʌmən/
**Definition:** To order someone to appear in a particular place, or to officially request something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 召喚

**Example 1:**
The manager summoned him to her office.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
經理召喚佢去佢辦公室。

**Example 2:**
He was summoned for jury duty.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢被召喚去履行陪審團義務。

## 4. **Envoy** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈenvɔɪ/
**Definition:** A diplomatic agent or representative.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 使節

**Example 1:**
The President sent an envoy to negotiate a peace treaty.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
總統派咗個使節去傾和約。

**Example 2:**
She acted as an envoy between the two warring factions.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢擔任兩個交戰派系之間嘅使節。

## 5. **Terminal** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtɜːrmɪnl/
**Definition:** A building where passengers or goods begin or end a journey. Can also refer to a computer input/output device.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 總站, 終端機

**Example 1:**
The bus terminal was very crowded.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
巴士總站非常擁擠。

**Example 2:**
He was working at his computer terminal.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢喺電腦終端機度做嘢。

## 6. **Outbreak** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈaʊtbreɪk/
**Definition:** A sudden occurrence of something unwelcome, such as disease or violence.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 爆發

**Example 1:**
There was a sudden outbreak of measles in the school.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
學校突然爆發麻疹。

**Example 2:**
The outbreak of war surprised everyone.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
戰爭爆發令大家都感到驚訝。

## 7. **Nationals** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnæʃənəlz/
**Definition:** People who are citizens of a particular country.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 國民

**Example 1:**
The embassy provides assistance to its nationals abroad.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
大使館向其在國外嘅國民提供協助。

**Example 2:**
Many nationals were evacuated from the war zone.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
好多國民被從戰區撤離。
