# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-08

Hello everyone! Today we'll learn some useful British English vocabulary from today's news.

## 1. **Truce** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /truːs/
**Definition:** An agreement between enemies to stop fighting for a certain period of time.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 停戰協議

**Example 1:**
The two countries have agreed to a fragile truce after weeks of intense fighting.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
經過幾個星期嘅激烈戰鬥之後，兩個國家同意咗一個脆弱嘅停戰協議。

**Example 2:**
Negotiators are working hard to establish a lasting truce.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
談判人員正努力建立一個持久嘅停戰協議。

## 2. **Gain** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ɡeɪn/
**Definition:** An increase or improvement.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 增加; 獲益

**Example 1:**
The Reform UK party made significant gains in the local elections.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
改革英國黨喺地方選舉中取得咗重大嘅進展。

**Example 2:**
The company reported a gain in profits this quarter.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
公司報告呢個季度利潤有所增長。

## 3. **Tariff** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtærɪf/
**Definition:** A tax or duty to be paid on a particular class of imports or exports.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 關稅

**Example 1:**
The government imposed new tariffs on imported steel.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府對進口鋼鐵徵收咗新嘅關稅。

**Example 2:**
These tariffs could lead to higher prices for consumers.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢啲關稅可能會導致消費者要畀更高嘅價錢。

## 4. **Senedd** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɛnɛd/
**Definition:** The Welsh Parliament.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 威爾斯議會

**Example 1:**
The Labour party is expected to lose control of the Senedd.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
預計工黨將會失去對威爾斯議會嘅控制權。

**Example 2:**
The Senedd debated new legislation on environmental protection.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
威爾斯議會辯論咗關於環境保護嘅新法例。

## 5. **Hardware** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈhɑːdweə(r)/
**Definition:** The machines, wiring, and other physical components of a computer or other electronic system; in a military context, weapons and equipment.
**Cantonese Meaning:** (電腦)硬件; (軍事) 武器裝備

**Example 1:**
The parade featured soldiers but no military hardware.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
遊行隊伍有士兵但係冇軍事裝備。

**Example 2:**
The new computer hardware significantly improved processing speed.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
新嘅電腦硬件顯著提升咗處理速度。

## 6. **U-turn** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈjuːtɜːn/
**Definition:** A complete reversal of policy or course of action.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 一百八十度轉變; 政策大轉彎

**Example 1:**
The company made a U-turn on its privacy policy.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
公司喺私隱政策上做咗一個大轉彎。

**Example 2:**
The government's U-turn on tax increases was widely criticised.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
政府喺加稅上嘅政策大轉彎受到廣泛批評。

## 7. **Alleging** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈlɛdʒɪŋ/
**Definition:** Stating without proof that someone has done something wrong or illegal.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 聲稱; 指控 (未經證實)

**Example 1:**
Iran is alleging that the US targeted an oil tanker.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
伊朗聲稱美國以一艘油輪作為目標。

**Example 2:**
The report is alleging serious misconduct within the organisation.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
該報告聲稱該組織內部存在嚴重不當行為。
