# Daily English Lesson - 2026-06-26

## 1. **Renovations** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌrenəˈveɪʃnz/
**Definition:** The process of improving or repairing a building to make it look new or better.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 翻新工程

**Example 1:**
The palace renovations will take several years to complete.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
宮殿嘅翻新工程需要幾年時間先可以完成。

**Example 2:**
We are planning some minor renovations to our kitchen next month.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋計劃下個月幫廚房做啲小翻新。

## 2. **Reside** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /rɪˈzaɪd/
**Definition:** To live in a particular place.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 居住 / 定居

**Example 1:**
The King continues to reside at Clarence House.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
國王繼續居住喺克拉倫斯府。

**Example 2:**
Many international students reside in halls of residence during their studies.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
好多國際學生喺讀書期間會住喺學生宿舍。

## 3. **Anatomy** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /əˈnætəmi/
**Definition:** The detailed examination or analysis of something to understand how it works or why it happened.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 解剖 / 詳細分析

**Example 1:**
The journalist wrote a piece on the anatomy of the government's recent failure.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰位記者寫咗篇關於政府最近失敗嘅詳細分析。

**Example 2:**
She studied the anatomy of the business deal to see where things went wrong.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢研究咗嗰單生意嘅細節，睇吓邊度出咗問題。

## 4. **Downfall** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈdaʊnfɔːl/
**Definition:** A loss of power, status, or success; the cause of someone's failure.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 倒台 / 衰敗

**Example 1:**
The prime minister’s downfall was caused by a series of political errors.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
首相嘅倒台係由一系列政治錯誤所引起。

**Example 2:**
Greed was his eventual downfall.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
貪婪最終成為佢失敗嘅原因。

## 5. **Viably** (Adverb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈvaɪəbli/
**Definition:** In a way that is capable of working successfully or being effective.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可行地 / 能夠維持地

**Example 1:**
The company is struggling to operate viably in the current market.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰間公司喺現時市場下好難維持營運。

**Example 2:**
We need to find a plan that can be viably implemented by next year.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋需要搵一個出年可以順利執行嘅計劃。

## 6. **Geothermal** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌdʒiːəʊˈθɜːml/
**Definition:** Relating to the internal heat of the earth, often used as a source of energy.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 地熱嘅

**Example 1:**
Geothermal energy is considered a sustainable way to heat homes.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
地熱能源被視為一種供暖嘅可持續方法。

**Example 2:**
The new plant harnesses geothermal heat to generate electricity.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢座新發電廠利用地熱嚟發電。

## 7. **Outskirts** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈaʊtskɜːts/
**Definition:** The outer parts of a town or city, furthest from the centre.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 市郊 / 邊緣地區

**Example 1:**
The earthquake hit a small village on the outskirts of the capital.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
地震重創咗首都邊緣嘅一個小村莊。

**Example 2:**
They live on the outskirts of London, so they commute by train.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋住喺倫敦市郊，所以佢哋搭火車返工。
