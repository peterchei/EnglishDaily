# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-22

## 1. **Redundancies** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /rɪˈdʌndənsiz/
**Definition:** The state of being no longer employed because there is no more work available.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 裁員 (còi jyun)

**Example 1:**
The factory announced mass redundancies due to the economic downturn.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
工廠宣佈由於經濟衰退而大規模裁員。 (Gūng chong syūn bou yū yue ging jai sui teoi yi daai gūi mōc coi yun.)

**Example 2:**
She was offered a generous severance package following her redundancy.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢被裁員後獲提供豐厚嘅離職補償金。 (Keoi bei coi yun hau wok cung cung fung hau ge lei jik bo seung gam.)

## 2. **Strain** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /streɪn/
**Definition:** A particular type or variety of a disease, plant, or animal.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 品種 (bánjéung) / 毒株 (duk jū) (for viruses/diseases)

**Example 1:**
Scientists are working to develop a vaccine against the new strain of bird flu.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
科學家正努力開發一種針對新型禽流感毒株嘅疫苗。 (Fo hok ga jing nu lik hoi fat yat jung gan deui san ying kam lau gam duk jū ge yik miu.)

**Example 2:**
This particular strain of wheat is resistant to disease.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢個特定嘅小麥品種具有抗病能力。 (Ni go tak ding ge siu mak ban jung jui jau kong beng nang lik.)

## 3. **Escalate** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈeskəleɪt/
**Definition:** To become or make something become greater, more serious, or more intense.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 升級 (sīng gāp) / 惡化 (ok fa)

**Example 1:**
The conflict threatens to escalate into a full-scale war.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢場衝突可能會升級為全面戰爭。 (Ni cheung chung dot ho neng wui sing gap wai chuen min jin jaang.)

**Example 2:**
We need to take steps to prevent the argument from escalating further.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋需要採取措施防止爭論進一步惡化。 (Ngo dei sui yiu choi cheui mok si fong ji jaang leun jeun yat bo ok fa.)

## 4. **Mercenaries** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈmɜːsənriz/
**Definition:** Professional soldiers who fight for any country or group that will pay them.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 僱傭兵 (gu yung bing)

**Example 1:**
Reports suggest that foreign mercenaries were involved in the conflict.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
報告顯示外國僱傭兵參與咗呢場衝突。 (Bo gou hin si wai gwok gu yung bing chaam yu jor ni cheung chung dot.)

**Example 2:**
The government denies hiring mercenaries to fight in the war.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
政府否認僱用僱傭兵去打仗。 (Jing fu fau ying gu yung gu yung bing heui da jaang.)

## 5. **Back off** (Phrasal Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /bæk ɒf/
**Definition:** To retreat or move away; to stop being involved in something; to stop threatening someone.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 退縮 (teoi suk) / 停止 (ting ji)

**Example 1:**
The company had to back off from its original plan due to public pressure.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
由於公眾壓力，公司不得不從最初嘅計劃中退縮。 (Yau yu gung jung aat lik, gung si bat dak bat jung chui cho ge gai wak jung teoi suk.)

**Example 2:**
He told the protesters to back off and leave the area.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢叫示威者退縮並離開該區域。 (Keoi giu si wai je teui suk bing lei hoi goi kui wik.)

## 6. **Mogul** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈməʊɡəl/
**Definition:** An important or powerful person, especially in the entertainment or media industry.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 大亨 (daai hang) / 巨頭 (geui tau)

**Example 1:**
He is a powerful media mogul with interests in television and newspapers.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢係一位強大嘅傳媒大亨，喺電視同報紙方面都有利益。 (Keoi hai yat wai keung daai ge chuen mui daai hang, hai din si tung bou ji fong min dou jau lei yik.)

**Example 2:**
The tech mogul announced his retirement from the company.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢位科技巨頭宣佈佢從公司退休。 (Ni wai fo gei geui tau syun bou keoi jung gung si teui yau.)

## 7. **Vandalism** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈvændəlɪzəm/
**Definition:** Action involving deliberate destruction of or damage to public or private property.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 故意破壞 (gu yi po waai)

**Example 1:**
The police are investigating acts of vandalism at the local school.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
警方正在調查當地學校嘅故意破壞行為。(Ging fong jing joi diu cha dong dei hok haau ge gu yi po waai hang wai.)

**Example 2:**
The community was outraged by the vandalism of the historic monument.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
社群對歷史古蹟嘅故意破壞感到憤怒。(Seh kwan deui lik si gu jik ge gu yi po waai gam dou fan nu.)
