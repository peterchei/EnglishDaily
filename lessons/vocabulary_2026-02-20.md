# English Daily Lesson - 2026-02-20

**Words:**
- ephemeral: Lasting for a very short time. (Cantonese: 短暫的)
- serendipity: The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way. (Cantonese: 意外收穫, 奇遇)
- ubiquitous: Present, appearing, or found everywhere. (Cantonese: 無處不在)
- meticulous: Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise. (Cantonese: 嚴謹的, 一絲不苟的)
- resilience: The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness. (Cantonese: 韌性, 恢復力)
- ambiguous: Open to more than one interpretation; having a double meaning; unclear or inexact. (Cantonese: 模棱兩可的, 意義不明的)
- augment: Make (something) greater by adding to it; increase. (Cantonese: 增加, 擴大)
