# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-06

Hello everyone,

## 1. **Pause** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /pɔːz/
**Definition:** To stop temporarily; to interrupt an activity for a short time.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 暫停

**Example 1:**
The US decided to pause the operation in the Strait of Hormuz.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
美國決定暫停喺霍爾木茲海峽嘅行動。

**Example 2:**
Let's pause for a moment and take a break.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋停一停，休息一下啦。

## 2. **Fragile** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfrædʒaɪl/
**Definition:** Easily broken or damaged; delicate. Also used to describe situations that are unstable and easily disrupted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 脆弱嘅; 易碎嘅

**Example 1:**
The Gulf ceasefire is in a fragile state.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
波斯灣停火協議處於脆弱狀態。

**Example 2:**
Be careful with that vase, it's very fragile.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
小心啲個花樽，佢好易爛㗎。

## 3. **Engagement** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒmənt/
**Definition:** An official appointment or activity; also, involvement or participation.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 約會; 參與

**Example 1:**
The Princess of Wales has an overseas engagement in Italy next week.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
威爾斯王妃下個禮拜會去意大利進行海外訪問。

**Example 2:**
The company wants to increase customer engagement through social media.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
間公司想透過社交媒體增加客戶參與度。

## 4. **Sprint** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /sprɪnt/
**Definition:** A short, fast run; also, a final burst of activity.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 短跑; 最後衝刺

**Example 1:**
The election campaign is in its final sprint.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
選舉活動已經進入最後衝刺階段。

**Example 2:**
He won the race with a powerful sprint to the finish line.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢用強勁嘅衝刺贏咗比賽。

## 5. **Vessels** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈveslz/
**Definition:** A ship or boat; any type of container.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 船隻

**Example 1:**
The US is pausing an operation to guide vessels through the Strait of Hormuz.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
美國暫停咗一項引導船隻通過霍爾木茲海峽嘅行動。

**Example 2:**
These vessels transport goods across the ocean.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
呢啲船隻喺海洋上運送貨物。

## 6. **Halted** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈhɔːltɪd/
**Definition:** Stopped; brought to a standstill.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 停止咗

**Example 1:**
"Project Freedom" was halted after progress was made in negotiations.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
喺談判取得進展後，「自由計劃」就停止咗。

**Example 2:**
Production at the factory was halted due to the strike.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
因為罷工，工廠嘅生產已經停止。

## 7. **Frontline** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfrʌntlaɪn/
**Definition:** Situated in or relating to the area where opposing armies face each other; the most important or visible position in an activity or organisation.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 前線嘅

**Example 1:**
Ukrainian civilians are trapped in a frontline city.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
烏克蘭平民被困喺前線城市。

**Example 2:**
Nurses and doctors are on the frontline of the healthcare system.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
護士同醫生喺醫療系統嘅最前線。
