# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-27

Hi there!

## 1. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased; delighted.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心; 好滿意

**Example 1:**
I'm absolutely chuffed to have won the competition.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我贏咗個比賽，真係好開心。

**Example 2:**
She was chuffed with her exam results.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢對佢嘅考試成績好滿意。

## 2. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed; devastated.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望; 好傷心

**Example 1:**
I was gutted when I heard the news.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我聽到嗰個消息嘅時候，好失望。

**Example 2:**
She was gutted that she didn't get the job.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢冇得到份工，好傷心。

## 3. **Taking the mickey** (Phrase)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈteɪkɪŋ ðə ˈmɪki/
**Definition:** To tease or make fun of someone.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 玩嘢; 搞搞震; 揾人笨

**Example 1:**
Are you taking the mickey out of me?
**Cantonese Example 1:**
你係咪玩嘢呀？

**Example 2:**
Stop taking the mickey and be serious.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好再搞搞震，認真啲。

## 4. **Queue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people or vehicles waiting for something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊; 排隊

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the shop.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
間舖頭外面有一條長隊。

**Example 2:**
We had to queue for ages to get in.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋要排好耐隊先入到去。

## 5. **Rubbish** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrʌbɪʃ/
**Definition:** Useless waste or litter; nonsense.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 垃圾; 廢話

**Example 1:**
The streets were full of rubbish after the festival.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
個節日之後，條街都係垃圾。

**Example 2:**
Don't talk rubbish!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好講廢話啦！

## 6. **Sorted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɔːtɪd/
**Definition:** Organised, dealt with successfully; under control.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 搞掂; 處理好; 搞清楚

**Example 1:**
Everything is sorted now, so we can relax.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
而家所有嘢都搞掂曬，我哋可以放鬆下。

**Example 2:**
Have you got your tickets sorted?
**Cantonese Example 2:**
你搞掂咗啲飛未呀？

## 7. **Dodgy** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈdɒdʒi/
**Definition:** Suspicious, unreliable, or potentially dangerous.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可疑嘅; 靠唔住嘅; 危險嘅

**Example 1:**
That area of town is a bit dodgy at night.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰個區夜晚有啲危險。

**Example 2:**
He gave me a dodgy explanation.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢畀咗個靠唔住嘅解釋我。
