# Daily English Lesson - 2026-05-27

Hi there! Let's learn some new English words today.

## 1. **Coherent** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /kəʊˈhɪərənt/
**Definition:** Logical and consistent; forming a unified whole.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 連貫嘅; 有條理嘅

**Example 1:**
The politician's speech wasn't very coherent; it jumped from topic to topic with no clear connection.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
個政客嘅演講唔係好連貫; 佢由一個話題跳到另一個話題，冇乜嘢清晰嘅聯繫。

**Example 2:**
For the essay to be good, it needs a coherent argument that flows logically from start to finish.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
要篇論文好，佢需要一個連貫嘅論點，由頭到尾都好有邏輯咁流動。

## 2. **Impact** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɪmpækt/
**Definition:** A marked effect or influence.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 影響

**Example 1:**
The new regulations will have a significant impact on small businesses.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
新嘅法規將會對細嘅生意有顯著嘅影響。

**Example 2:**
The report analysed the environmental impact of the factory's operations.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
份報告分析咗嗰間工廠運作對環境嘅影響。

## 3. **Recovered** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /rɪˈkʌvəd/
**Definition:** Returned to a normal or healthy condition after an illness, injury, or shock.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 恢復; 搵返

**Example 1:**
The missing painting was recovered by the police.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
失蹤嘅畫作被警方搵返。

**Example 2:**
She recovered quickly from her cold and was back at work within a few days.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢好快就恢復咗，幾日之內就返返工。

## 4. **Permanent** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈpɜːmənənt/
**Definition:** Lasting or intended to last or remain unchanged indefinitely.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 永久嘅; 長期嘅

**Example 1:**
She's looking for a permanent job, not just temporary work.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢想搵一份長工，唔係淨係臨時工。

**Example 2:**
The museum has a permanent collection of ancient artifacts.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰間博物館有一個永久收藏嘅古代文物。

## 5. **Mediate** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈmiːdieɪt/
**Definition:** Intervene between people in a dispute in order to bring about an agreement or reconciliation.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 調解

**Example 1:**
The United Nations is trying to mediate a peace agreement between the warring factions.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
聯合國試圖調解交戰各方之間嘅和平協議。

**Example 2:**
A neutral third party was brought in to mediate the dispute between the two companies.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
搵咗一個中立嘅第三方嚟調解兩間公司之間嘅糾紛。

## 6. **Intensifies** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪnˈtensɪfaɪz/
**Definition:** Become or make more intense.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 加強; 變得更強烈

**Example 1:**
The pressure on the government intensifies as the economic crisis worsens.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
隨住經濟危機惡化，政府承受嘅壓力增加咗。

**Example 2:**
The rain intensifies as the storm approaches.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
當風暴接近時，雨勢就會加強。

## 7. **Critical Infrastructure** (Noun Phrase)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈkrɪtɪkəl ˈɪnfrəstrʌktʃər/
**Definition:** Systems and assets that are essential for a society to function.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 重要基礎設施

**Example 1:**
The government is working to protect critical infrastructure from cyber attacks.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
政府正致力於保護重要嘅基礎設施免受網絡攻擊。

**Example 2:**
Damage to critical infrastructure, such as power grids, can have devastating consequences.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
對重要基礎設施，例如電網嘅損壞，可能會產生災難性嘅後果。
