# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-17

Hi there, welcome to today's lesson!

## 1. **Queue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people waiting for something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊列, 排隊

**Example 1:**
There's a long queue outside the bakery this morning.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今朝喺麵包舖出面有條長長嘅隊。

**Example 2:**
Please join the queue for tickets.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
請排隊買飛。

## 2. **Cheers** (Exclamation)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃɪəz/
**Definition:** A word used to express gratitude or as a toast.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 多謝, 乾杯

**Example 1:**
Cheers for helping me with the shopping!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
多謝你幫我買嘢！

**Example 2:**
Cheers! To a happy new year.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
乾杯！祝新年快樂。

## 3. **Rubbish** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrʌbɪʃ/
**Definition:** Waste material; trash. Also used to mean nonsense.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 垃圾, 廢話

**Example 1:**
Please put the rubbish in the bin.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
請將垃圾放入垃圾桶。

**Example 2:**
That's a load of rubbish! I don't believe it.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰啲全部都係廢話！我唔相信。

## 4. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪliənt/
**Definition:** Exceptionally clever or talented; very good.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 聰明, 好棒

**Example 1:**
She's a brilliant student.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢係一個好聰明嘅學生。

**Example 2:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰個係一個好棒嘅主意！

## 5. **Fancy** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfænsi/
**Definition:** To want something or want to do something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 想要, 鍾意

**Example 1:**
Do you fancy a cup of tea?
**Cantonese Example 1:**
你想唔想飲杯茶？

**Example 2:**
I don't fancy going out tonight.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我今晚唔想出街。

## 6. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased or satisfied.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心, 好滿意

**Example 1:**
I'm really chuffed with my exam results.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我對我嘅考試成績感到非常滿意。

**Example 2:**
She was chuffed to win the competition.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢贏咗比賽，好開心。

## 7. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed or upset.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 極度失望, 好傷心

**Example 1:**
I was gutted when I didn't get the job.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我冇得到嗰份工，好傷心。

**Example 2:**
She's absolutely gutted that she can't come to the party.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢嚟唔到派對，覺得非常失望。
