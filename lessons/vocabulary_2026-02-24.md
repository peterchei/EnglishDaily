# Vocabulary Lesson - February 24th, 2026

This lesson focuses on understanding and using a few advanced English words.

## Ephemeral
*   **Cantonese:** 短暫的 (dyun2 zaam6 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Lasting for a very short time.
*   **Example 1:** The beauty of cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a few weeks.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 櫻花的短暫美麗，只能維持幾週。 (jing1 faa1 dik1 dyun2 zaam6 mei5 lai6, zi2 nang4 wai4 ci4 gei2 zau1.)
*   **Example 2:** Social media trends are often ephemeral.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 社交媒體的潮流經常是短暫的。 (se3 gaau1 mei4 tai4 dik1 ciu4 lau4 ging1 soeng4 si6 dyun2 zaam6 dik1.)

## Ubiquitous
*   **Cantonese:** 無處不在的 (mou4 cyu3 boi3 zi2 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Present, appearing, or found everywhere.
*   **Example 1:** Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 智能手機在現代社會已變得無處不在。 (zi3 nang4 sau2 gei1 zoi6 jin6 doi6 se5 wui6 ji5 bin3 dak1 mou4 cyu3 boi3 zi2.)
*   **Example 2:** Coffee shops are ubiquitous in most cities.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 在大多數城市，咖啡店都無處不在。 (zai6 daai6 do1 so3 sing4 si5, gaa1 fei1 dim3 dou1 mou4 cyu3 boi3 zi2.)

## Serendipity
*   **Cantonese:** 意外發現珍寶的運氣 (ji3 ngoi6 faat3 jin6 zan1 bou2 dik1 wan6 hei2)
*   **Definition:** The occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.
*   **Example 1:** It was pure serendipity that they met at the airport.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 他們在機場相遇，純粹是意外發現珍寶的運氣。 (taa1 mun4 zoi6 gei1 coeng4 soeng1 jyu6, soen4 seoi3 si6 ji3 ngoi6 faat3 jin6 zan1 bou2 dik1 wan6 hei2.)
*   **Example 2:** Many scientific discoveries happen through serendipity.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 許多科學發現都是通過意外發現珍寶的運氣而來的。 (heoi2 do1 fo1 hok6 faat3 jin6 dou1 si6 tung1 gwo3 ji3 ngoi6 faat3 jin6 zan1 bou2 dik1 wan6 hei2 ji4 loi4 dik1.)

## Meticulous
*   **Cantonese:** 一絲不苟的 (jat1 si1 bat1 gau2 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise.
*   **Example 1:** The artist was meticulous in her attention to detail.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 這位藝術家對細節的關注一絲不苟。 (ze2 wai3 ji6 su6 gaa1 deoi3 sai3 zit3 dik1 gwaan1 zyu3 jat1 si1 bat1 gau2.)
*   **Example 2:** He followed the instructions meticulously.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 他一絲不苟地遵循著指示。 (taa1 jat1 si1 bat1 gau2 dei6 zeon1 sau2 zoek3 zi2 ci3.)

## Eloquent
*   **Cantonese:** 能言善辯的；口才好的 (nang4 jin4 sin6 bin6 dik1; hau2 coi4 hou2 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Fluent or persuasive in speaking or writing.
*   **Example 1:** Her speech was eloquent and persuasive.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 她的演講能言善辯且富有說服力。 (taa1 dik1 jin2 gong2 nang4 jin4 sin6 bin6 jit6 fu4 jau5 syut6 fuk6 lik6.)
*   **Example 2:** He is an eloquent advocate for environmental protection.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 他是環保的能言善辯的倡導者。 (taa1 si6 waan4 bou2 dik1 nang4 jin4 sin6 bin6 dik1 coeng3 dou3 ze2.)

## Resilience
*   **Cantonese:** 適應力；恢復力 (sik1 jing6 lik6; foon1 fuk6 lik6)
*   **Definition:** The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness.
*   **Example 1:** The community showed great resilience in the face of adversity.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 社區在逆境中展現了極大的恢復力。 (se5 keoi1 zoi6 jik6 ging2 zung1 zin2 jin6 lik6 zaap6 jing6 lik6 zoi6 nei6 ging2 zung1 zin2 jin6 lik6 dak1 sing1 fong1 lou6.)
*   **Example 2:** Developing resilience is key to overcoming challenges.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 培養恢復力是克服挑戰的關鍵。 (pui4 joeng5 foon1 fuk6 lik6 si6 hat6 cuk1 tiu1 zin3 dik1 gaau3 min6.)

## Ambiguous
*   **Cantonese:** 模棱兩可的；含糊不清的 (mou4 ling4 loeng5 ho2 dik1; haam4 fu2 bat1 cing1 dik1)
*   **Definition:** Open to more than one interpretation; not having one obvious meaning.
*   **Example 1:** The instructions were ambiguous, leading to confusion.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1:** 這些指示模棱兩可，導致了困惑。 (ze2 di1 zi2 zi3 mou4 ling4 loeng5 ho2, dou2 zi3 lik6 kwan3 fok3.)
*   **Example 2:** His response was ambiguous, and I'm not sure what he meant.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2:** 他的回應含糊不清，我不確定他的意思。 (taa1 dik1 wui4 jing3 haam4 fu2 bat1 cing1, ngo5 bat1 heoi3 ding6 taa1 dik1 ji3 si1.)
