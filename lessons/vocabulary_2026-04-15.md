# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-15

Hello everyone! Today we'll learn some useful English words and phrases.

## 1. **Trawler** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtrɔːlər/
**Definition:** A fishing boat that uses a large net called a trawl to catch fish.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 拖網漁船 (to1 mong5 yu4 syun4)

**Example 1:**
The trawler returned to port with a large catch of cod.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
嗰艘拖網漁船返到碼頭，帶咗好多鱈魚。 (go2 sau2 to1 mong5 yu4 syun4 faan1 dou3 maa5 tau2, daai3 zo2 hou2 do1 syut3 yu2.)

**Example 2:**
Environmentalists are concerned about the impact of trawlers on the seabed.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
環保人士擔心拖網漁船對海床嘅影響。 (waan4 bou2 yan4 si6 daam1 sam1 to1 mong5 yu4 syun4 deoi3 hoi2 cong4 ge3 ying2 hoeng2.)

## 2. **Ease** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /iːz/
**Definition:** To make something less painful, difficult, or severe.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 減輕 (gaam2 hing1)

**Example 1:**
The new policy should ease tensions between the two countries.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
新嘅政策應該會減輕兩個國家之間嘅緊張關係。 (san1 ge3 zing3 caak3 ying1 goi1 wui5 gaam2 hing1 loeng5 go3 gwok3 gaa1 zi1 gaan1 ge3 gan2 zoeng1 gwaan1 hai6.)

**Example 2:**
A warm bath can ease muscle pain.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
浸個熱水浴可以減輕肌肉疼痛。 (zam3 go3 jit6 seoi2 yuk6 ho2 ji5 gaam2 hing1 gei1 yuk6 tung3 tung3.)

## 3. **Think tank** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈθɪŋk tæŋk/
**Definition:** A body of experts providing advice and ideas on specific political or economic problems.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 智庫 (zi3 fu3)

**Example 1:**
The think tank published a report on the future of education.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
個智庫發表咗一份關於教育未來嘅報告。 (go3 zi3 fu3 faat3 biu2 zo2 yat1 fan6 gwaan1 yu1 gaau3 yuk6 mei6 loi4 ge3 bou3 gou3.)

**Example 2:**
Several think tanks are advising the government on economic policy.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
有幾個智庫喺度向政府提供關於經濟政策嘅建議。 (yau5 gei2 go3 zi3 fu3 hai2 dou6 hoeng3 zing3 fu2 tai4 gung1 gwaan1 yu1 ging1 zai3 zing3 caak3 ge3 gin3 yi5.)

## 4. **Raffle** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈræfəl/
**Definition:** A lottery in which people buy tickets for the chance to win a prize.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 抽獎 (cau1 zoeng2)

**Example 1:**
The school is holding a raffle to raise money for new equipment.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
間學校搞緊抽獎嚟籌錢買新嘅設備。 (gaan1 hok6 haau6 gaau2 gan2 cau1 zoeng2 lai4 cau4 cin2 maai5 san1 ge3 sat1 bei6.)

**Example 2:**
I bought ten tickets for the charity raffle.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我買咗十張慈善抽獎嘅飛。 (ngo5 maai5 zo2 sap6 zoeng1 ci4 sin6 cau1 zoeng2 ge3 fei1.)

## 5. **Choppy** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtʃɒpi/
**Definition:** (Of water) having many small waves. Figuratively: Unstable or difficult.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 波濤洶湧 (bo1 tou4 hung1 yung2), 比喻 唔穩定 (bei2 yu6 m4 wan2 ding6)

**Example 1:**
The sea was too choppy for sailing.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
啲海浪太大，唔適合航行。 (di1 hoi2 long6 taai3 daai6, m4 sik1 hap6 hong4 hang4.)

**Example 2:**
The political situation remains choppy.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
政治局勢仍然唔穩定。 (zing3 zi6 guk6 sai3 ying4 yin4 m4 wan2 ding6.)

## 6. **Chronicle** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈkrɒnɪkəl/
**Definition:** A factual written account of important or historical events in the order of their occurrence.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 編年史 (pin1 nin4 si2)

**Example 1:**
The book chronicles the history of the city from its founding.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
本書記錄咗呢個城市由建立以嚟嘅歷史。 (bun2 syu1 gei3 luk6 zo2 ni1 go3 sing4 si5 yau4 gin3 lap6 yi5 loi4 ge3 lik6 si2.)

**Example 2:**
His diary is a chronicle of his experiences during the war.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢本日記係一本關於佢喺戰爭期間嘅經歷嘅編年史。 (keoi5 bun2 jat6 gei3 hai6 yat1 bun2 gwaan1 yu1 keoi5 hai2 zin3 zang1 kei4 gaan1 ge3 ging1 lik6 ge3 pin1 nin4 si2.)

## 7. **Overcrowding** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˌəʊvərˈkraʊdɪŋ/
**Definition:** The state of being too crowded; too many people in one place.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 過度擁擠 (gwo3 dou6 yung2 zai1)

**Example 1:**
Overcrowding in prisons is a major problem.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
監獄裡面嘅過度擁擠係一個主要嘅問題。 (gaam1 yuk6 leoi5 min6 ge3 gwo3 dou6 yung2 zai1 hai6 yat1 go3 zyu2 yiu3 ge3 man6 tai4.)

**Example 2:**
The overcrowding on the train made it difficult to breathe.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
火車上面嘅過度擁擠搞到呼吸都有啲困難。 (fo2 ce1 soeng6 min6 ge3 gwo3 dou6 yung2 zai1 gaau2 dou3 fu1 kap1 dou1 yau5 di1 kwan3 naan4.)
