# Daily English Lesson - 2026-03-20

Hi there, let's learn some new words!

## 1. **Chuffed** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃʌft/
**Definition:** Very pleased or satisfied.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好開心，好滿意

**Example 1:**
She was chuffed to bits when she won the competition.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢贏咗比賽，開心到不得了。

**Example 2:**
I'm absolutely chuffed with my new car.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我對我部新車非常滿意。

## 2. **Queue** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people or vehicles waiting their turn for something.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊列

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the cinema.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
戲院外面排咗條好長嘅隊。

**Example 2:**
Please join the queue and wait for your turn.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
請排隊等候。

## 3. **Cheeky** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtʃiːki/
**Definition:** Impudent or disrespectful in a playful or amusing way.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 厚面皮，無禮貌但得意

**Example 1:**
He gave me a cheeky grin.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢對我笑咗一個厚面皮嘅笑容。

**Example 2:**
Don't be so cheeky to your elders.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好對長輩咁無禮貌。

## 4. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed or upset.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望，好沮喪

**Example 1:**
I was gutted when I didn't get the job.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我冇得到份工，真係好失望。

**Example 2:**
He was absolutely gutted after losing the match.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
輸咗場比賽之後，佢非常沮喪。

## 5. **Sorted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɔːtɪd/
**Definition:** Organized, arranged, or dealt with satisfactorily.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 搞掂，安排好

**Example 1:**
Everything is sorted for the trip.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
旅行嘅所有嘢都搞掂曬。

**Example 2:**
I've sorted out the problem with the computer.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我已經解決咗電腦嘅問題。

## 6. **Taking the Mickey** (Phrase)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈteɪkɪŋ ðə ˈmɪki/
**Definition:** Making fun of someone; teasing or mocking.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 玩嘢，取笑

**Example 1:**
Are you taking the mickey out of me?
**Cantonese Example 1:**
你係咪玩嘢呀？

**Example 2:**
They were taking the mickey out of his new haircut.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢哋取笑佢嘅新髮型。

## 7. **Rubbish** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrʌbɪʃ/
**Definition:** Waste material; trash. Also, something that is considered to be of very poor quality or worthless.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 垃圾; 廢話

**Example 1:**
Please take out the rubbish.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
請拎走啲垃圾。

**Example 2:**
That's a load of rubbish!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰啲全部都係廢話！
