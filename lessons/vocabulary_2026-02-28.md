# Daily English Lesson - 2026-02-28

## 1. **Cheeky** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈtʃiːki/
**Definition:** Impudent or presumptuous, typically in a playful or amusing way.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 厚面皮; 古惑

**Example 1:**
He gave a cheeky grin when he realised he was caught.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
佢發現自己俾人捉到嘅時候，露出咗一個古惑嘅笑容。

**Example 2:**
Don't be so cheeky to your elders!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
唔好對長輩咁厚面皮呀！

## 2. **Gutted** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈɡʌtɪd/
**Definition:** Extremely disappointed or upset.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好失望; 崩潰

**Example 1:**
I was absolutely gutted when I didn't get the job.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
當我冇得到份工嘅時候，我真係好失望。

**Example 2:**
She was gutted to hear about her friend's accident.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢聽到朋友嘅意外之後好崩潰。

## 3. **Queue** (Noun/Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** (Noun) A line of people waiting for something. (Verb) To wait in a line.
**Cantonese Meaning:** (名詞) 隊; (動詞) 排隊

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the cinema.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
戲院外面排咗條好長嘅隊。

**Example 2:**
We had to queue for hours to get tickets.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋要排幾個鐘頭隊先買到飛。

## 4. **Fancy** (Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfænsi/
**Definition:** To want something, or to be attracted to someone.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 想要; 鍾意

**Example 1:**
Do you fancy going for a walk?
**Cantonese Example 1:**
你想唔想去行下？

**Example 2:**
I think he fancies her.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我覺得佢鍾意咗佢。

## 5. **Sorted** (Adjective/Verb - Past Participle)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈsɔːtɪd/
**Definition:** (Adjective) Organized and arranged. (Verb - Past Participle) Dealt with successfully; resolved.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 搞掂; 整齊

**Example 1:**
Okay, the problem is sorted now.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
好啦，而家個問題搞掂咗啦。

**Example 2:**
Everything is sorted for the trip.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
所有嘢都為咗個旅程準備好/整齊咗。

## 6. **Rubbish** (Noun/Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈrʌbɪʃ/
**Definition:** (Noun) Waste material; garbage. (Adjective) Of very poor quality; nonsense.
**Cantonese Meaning:** (名詞) 垃圾; (形容詞) 廢嘅; 垃圾嘅

**Example 1:**
The bin is full of rubbish.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
個垃圾桶滿晒垃圾。

**Example 2:**
That's a load of rubbish!
**Cantonese Example 2:**
嗰啲全部都係垃圾說話！
