# Daily English Lesson - 2026-04-04

Hello everyone! Today's lesson focuses on useful words for everyday British English.

## 1. **Cheers** (Exclamation/Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /tʃɪəz/
**Definition:** A word used to express gratitude, as a toast, or as a friendly farewell.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 多謝, 乾杯, 再見

**Example 1:**
Cheers for helping me with the shopping!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
多謝你幫我買嘢！

**Example 2:**
We raised our glasses and said, "Cheers!"
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋舉起杯，講咗聲「乾杯！」

## 2. **Knackered** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈnækəd/
**Definition:** Extremely tired; exhausted. (British slang)
**Cantonese Meaning:** 好攰, 殘晒

**Example 1:**
I'm absolutely knackered after that long walk.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
行完咁長嘅路，我真係殘晒。

**Example 2:**
He looks knackered; he should get some rest.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢睇落好攰，應該休息下。

## 3. **Queue** (Noun/Verb)
**Pronunciation:** /kjuː/
**Definition:** A line of people waiting for something. Also, to stand in line.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 隊伍, 排隊

**Example 1:**
There was a long queue outside the bakery this morning.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今朝喺麵包鋪外面有條長長嘅隊伍。

**Example 2:**
We had to queue for an hour to get into the museum.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
我哋要排一個鐘頭隊先可以入博物館。

## 4. **Lovely** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈlʌvli/
**Definition:** Beautiful; very pleasing.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 可愛, 美麗, 很好

**Example 1:**
What a lovely day for a picnic!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
今日天氣咁好，好啱去野餐！

**Example 2:**
She has a lovely smile.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢嘅笑容好可愛。

## 5. **Brilliant** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈbrɪljənt/
**Definition:** Very good; outstanding; clever.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 精彩, 叻, 好勁

**Example 1:**
That's a brilliant idea!
**Cantonese Example 1:**
呢個主意好精彩！

**Example 2:**
She's a brilliant student.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
佢係一個好叻嘅學生。

## 6. **Fortnight** (Noun)
**Pronunciation:** /ˈfɔːtnaɪt/
**Definition:** A period of two weeks.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 兩個星期

**Example 1:**
We're going on holiday for a fortnight.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
我哋會去放兩個星期嘅假。

**Example 2:**
The report is due in a fortnight.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
份報告兩個星期後要交。
