
## Daily English Lesson - February 24th, 2026

Here are 7 useful English words and phrases for today's lesson:

### 1. Serendipity
*   **Cantonese Translation**: 意外的發現；巧合 (yi3 ngoi6 dik1 faat3 jin6; haau2 hap6)
*   **Example 1**: Finding that old book in the library was a moment of pure serendipity.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1**: 在圖書館找到那本舊書純粹是意外的發現。 (Zoi6 tou4 syu1 gun2 zou2 dou3 naa5 bun2 gau6 syu1 syun4 seoi3 si6 ji3 ngoi6 dik1 faat3 jin6.)
*   **Example 2**: Their meeting was a happy serendipity that led to a lifelong friendship.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2**: 他們的相遇是一次快樂的巧合，最終發展成一生的友誼。 (Taa1 mun4 dik1 soeng1 jyu6 si6 jat1 ci3 faai3 lok6 dik1 haau2 hap6, zeoi3 zung1 faat1 zin2 sing4 jat1 sang1 ge3 jau5 ji6.)

### 2. Ephemeral
*   **Cantonese Translation**: 短暫的；朝生暮死的 (dyun2 zaam6 dik1; ziu1 sang1 mou6 sei2 dik1)
*   **Example 1**: The beauty of cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a few weeks.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1**: 櫻花的美是短暫的，只能持續幾週。 (Jing1 faa1 dik1 mei5 si6 dyun2 zaam6 dik1, zi2 nang4 ci4 har6 gei2 zau1.)
*   **Example 2**: In the fast-paced digital world, trends can be ephemeral.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2**: 在快節奏的數位世界裡，潮流可能轉瞬即逝。 (Zoi6 faai3 git3 zau1 dik1 syu3 wai6 sai3 gaai3 leoi5, ciu4 lok6 ho2 nang4 zyun2 syun3 zip3 si6.)

### 3. Ubiquitous
*   **Cantonese Translation**: 無處不在的；普遍存在的 (mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi6 dik1; pou2 pin3 cyun1 zoi6 dik1)
*   **Example 1**: Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1**: 智能手機在現代社會已變得無處不在。 (Zhi4 nang4 sau2 gei1 zoi6 jin6 doi6 se5 gaai3 ji5 bin3 dak1 mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi6.)
*   **Example 2**: The company's advertising is ubiquitous; you see it everywhere you go.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2**: 該公司的廣告無處不在；你走到哪裡都能看到。 (Goi1 gung1 si1 dik1 gwong2 gou3 mou4 cyu3 bat1 zoi6; nei5 zau2 dou3 naa5 leoi5 dou1 nang4 dou3 hon3 dou3.)

### 4. Resilient
*   **Cantonese Translation**: 堅韌的；有彈性的 (gin1 jam6 dik1; jau5 daan6 sing4 dik1)
*   **Example 1**: Despite facing many challenges, the community proved to be resilient.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1**: 儘管面臨許多挑戰，該社區卻展現出堅韌不拔的精神。 (Gan2 gwaan2 min6 lim4 heoi2 do1 tiu1 zin3, goi1 se5 keoi1 koek3 zin2 zin3 teu1 gin1 jam6 bat1 baa4 gei1 sing4 san4.)
*   **Example 2**: Children are often more resilient than adults in adapting to new environments.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2**: 兒童在適應新環境方面往往比成人更有彈性。 (Ji4 tung4 zoi6 sik1 jing6 san1 wun4 ging2 fong1 min6 wong5 wong5 gang3 bit6 sing4 jan4 gang3 jau5 daan6 sing4.)

### 5. Nuance
*   **Cantonese Translation**: 細微差別；微妙之處 (sai3 mei6 caat3 bit6; mei6 jiu3 zi1 cyu3)
*   **Example 1**: The artist is skilled at capturing the subtle nuance of light and shadow.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1**: 這位藝術家善於捕捉光影之間細微的差別。 (Ze2 wai6 gj1 seot6 gaa1 sin3 jyu6 bou2 zyu2 gwong1 jing2 zi1 gaan1 sai3 mei6 dik1 caat3 bit6.)
*   **Example 2**: Understanding the nuance of a foreign language takes time and practice.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2**: 理解一門外語的微妙之處需要時間和練習。 (Leoi5 gaai2 jat1 mun4 ngoi6 jyu5 dik1 mei6 jiu3 zi1 cyu3 seoi1 jiu3 si4 gaan1 wo4 lin6 zaap6.)

### 6. Procrastinate
*   **Cantonese Translation**: 拖延；延遲 (to1 jin4; jin4 ci4)
*   **Example 1**: I tend to procrastinate on tasks that I find difficult.
    *   **Cantonese Example 1**: 對於我覺得困難的任務，我傾向於拖延。 (Deoi3 jyu1 ngo5 gong2 nei5 gau3 kan3 nan4 ge3 jam3 mou6, ngo5 king1 soeng6 jyu1 to1 jin4.)
*   **Example 2**: Don't procrastinate on your assignments; start them early.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2**: 不要拖延你的作業；盡早開始。 (Bat1 jiu3 to1 jin4 nei5 dik1 zou6 jip6; zeon3 zou2 hoi1 ci2.)

### 7. Elucidate
*   **Cantonese Translation**: 闡明；闡述 (can2 ming4; can2 sut6)
*   **Example 1**: Could you please elucidate your point of view on this matter?
    *   **Cantonese Example 1**: 你能否闡明你對此事的觀點？ (Nei5 nang4 fau2 can2 ming4 nei5 deoi3 ci2 si6 dik1 gun1 dim2?)
*   **Example 2**: The professor used clear examples to elucidate the complex theory.
    *   **Cantonese Example 2**: 這位教授用清晰的例子來闡述這個複雜的理論。 (Ze2 wai6 gaau3 so3 jung6 cing1 sik1 dik1 lai6 zi2 loi4 can2 sut6 ze2 go3 fuk1 zaat6 ge3 lei5 leon6.)
