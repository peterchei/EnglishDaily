# Daily English Lesson - 2026-02-24

## 1. **Ephemeral** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /ɪˈfɛm.ər.əl/
**Definition:** Lasting for a very short time.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 短暫的 / 瞬息萬變的

**Example 1:**
The beauty of a sunset is often ephemeral.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
日落的美景往往是短暫的。

**Example 2:**
Fashion trends can be ephemeral, changing with each season.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
時尚潮流瞬息萬變，隨著季節而更迭。

## 2. **Resilient** (Adjective)
**Pronunciation:** /rɪˈzɪl.jənt/
**Definition:** Able to withstand or recover quickly from difficult conditions.
**Cantonese Meaning:** 堅韌的 / 有彈性的

**Example 1:**
Plants in the desert are resilient to the harsh climate.
**Cantonese Example 1:**
沙漠中的植物對惡劣的氣候具有堅韌性。

**Example 2:**
Despite setbacks, she remained resilient and focused on her goals.
**Cantonese Example 2:**
儘管遭遇挫折，她仍然保持堅韌，專注於她的目標。
